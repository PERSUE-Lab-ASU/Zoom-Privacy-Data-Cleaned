AI Summarization Process

As part of our service, we offer an AI-powered summarization feature to help users efficiently capture key points from their meetings. When you use our transcription feature, your speech is converted into text through the Web Speech API, and this text is temporarily stored on your device in a transcription buffer.

This buffered text is periodically sent to our backend, where it is processed by an AI model provided by Anthropic. The AI model generates a concise summary of your meeting, focusing on the main topics discussed, key decisions made, and any action items or deadlines. Once the summary is generated, the original transcription data is discarded by the AI service, ensuring that your data is not retained beyond what is necessary for summarization.

The generated summaries are stored according to your preferences and can be managed or deleted by you at any time. We do not store raw transcription data beyond the summarization process, and all data transmissions are encrypted to protect your privacy.
