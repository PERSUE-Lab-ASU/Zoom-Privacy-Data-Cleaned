Virtual Sapiens, Inc. understands the sensitivity of personal data, and we are committed to ensuring our users’ privacy at every step. Virtual Sapiens is a privacy-first company, built to protect your information through advanced, on-device processing and strict data security protocols. Our platform prioritizes the confidentiality of our users’ interactions, giving our users’ control over their own experience without compromising safety.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Virtual Sapiens, Inc., a Delaware C Corp (“Virtual Sapiens, Inc.”, “us”, “we”, or “our”) collects about our users and why; what we may do with that information and how we handle information, including Personal Data (as defined below).

This Privacy Policy applies to the information obtained by us through your use of www.virtualsapiens.co (the “Site”) and other products and services offered by Virtual Sapiens, Inc. and its affiliates (“Services”).

What information do we collect?

Virtual Sapiens, Inc. may collect the following types of Personal Data (information that identifies you, or your device), for the reasons set forth below:

Your name, email address, calendar (Sidekick Coach users only) and meeting metadata (Sidekick Coach users only).

  • This lets us notify you of any updates to your account. This information also helps us communicate with you about your use of our Services, product announcements and updates, as well as respond to your requests for assistance. In addition, we may use your name and email to send you email correspondence as further provided below.

  • Virtual Sapiens stores minimal calendar and meeting data that is necessary to provide a seamless integration with our Services including the convenience of auto-launch and video event reminders to help users get the most out of our coaching service. This is for the Sidekick Coach solution users only.

Audio/Video

  • We do not default store or transmit video recordings. We can optionally save a user’s video if the client organization allows it, and the individual user explicitly requests their video saved for their own review and playback – an important part of training and self-reflection for improvement purposes. This is only available for our practice assessment types, and not for meetings. The video is saved solely for playback (ie all video AI still happens on-device only).
  • We store text-based metadata (e.g. numerical coordinates representing body positioning) that is necessary for us to train and optimize our machine learning models. For Sidekick Coach users, we can optionally save screenshots which serve to help users visually see their own nonverbal behaviors as they align with the AI feedback.
  • We do not store audio recordings. We only save transcripts of your assessments. Transcription is needed to provide you with a full analysis of your behavior. The transcription feature while extremely valuable, may be turned off upon request.

  • Other User Generated Content:

  • Other user content such as survey responses and other data-points provided through interaction with our site may be stored by Virtual Sapiens, Inc.. We do not use this information for any purpose other than to provide you with the most relevant feedback and suggestions for improvement and to improve our products to serve our customers better. 

  • We may use third party providers to support payment, e-mail communications and certain GenAI offerings (see our section on the use of GenerativeAI).

Log data

  • As with most websites and technology services delivered over the internet, we may automatically collect data when you access or use our Site and/or Services and record it in log files. This log data may include the IP address, browser type, the date and time of use, information about browser configuration, language preferences, cookie data, and information about your computer or mobile device, such as the type of hardware and software you are using.

  • This log data does not contain any video, images or audio, not visually identifiable data.

Gathering this kind of information helps us administer our Services and provide convenient and direct communications. It also helps us improve and further develop our Products.

Use of Generative AI Services

Summary of AI Usage:
Our platform leverages API-level generative AI services provided by third party generative AI partners to enhance user experience through the following features:

  • Summarizing meeting transcriptions
  • Extracting key takeaways and suggested next steps
  • Providing advance verbal analytics to help users improve their communication 
  • Facilitating compelling role plays

Data Processing and Sharing:
To enable these features, we send certain user-provided audio transcriptions of specific communication practice events, assessments or meetings (if transcriptions are enabled by user preference), to our third-party APIs. This data is processed by our GenAI partners’ models in real-time to generate responses, summaries, and feedback.

Scope of Data Shared:
The data we share with our GenAI partners may include, but is not limited to:

  • Transcriptions of specific user practice scenarios and meetings – always based on user consent. 
  • Contextual information necessary for providing feedback.

We do not share personally identifiable information (PII) or sensitive data unless explicitly needed for the feature. For example, we may share the user’s name so the AI can more personally address the user during a role play scenario.

Retention and Use of AI-Generated Content:

  • AI-generated outputs, such as summaries, key takeaways, and feedback, may be stored by us to improve our service and for your convenience.
  • Our GenAI partners may retain anonymized inputs and outputs per their data usage policy. For more information on how our GenAI partners protect your privacy, please reach out to us at compliance@virtualsapiens.co for more information.

User Control and Opt-Out Options:
Our clients and partners have the right to opt-out of this feature at any time. If you do so, no data related to your communications will be processed by our GenAI partners for the purposes of advance verbal feedback and coaching.  Please contact compliance@virtualsapiens.co to exercise this option.
