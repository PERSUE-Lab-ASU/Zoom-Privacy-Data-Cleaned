Our Terms & Privacy

Last Modified: Sep 22nd 2024

At Cronos, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any personal information that you provide to us. This privacy policy sets out how we use and protect any information that you give us when using our service.

Cronos uses the Google Calendar API and Zoom OAuth to authenticate users and provide them with the ability to manage calendar events and conduct meetings. When you grant Cronos Calendar access to your Google and Zoom accounts, we will only use this information to provide you with the services you have requested. This includes securely storing your Zoom OAuth tokens using encryption for the purpose of creating or modifying Zoom meetings, as well as managing events in your Google Calendar.

We are committed to complying with the Google API Services & Zoom User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Our privacy policy reflects our commitment to protect user privacy and comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

 

We take all reasonable steps to ensure the security of your personal information. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk.

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time, so you may wish to check it each time you submit personal information to Cronos Calendar. The latest version of our privacy policy will always be available on our website.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the information we hold about you, please contact us at support@cronoscal.com.

 

Disclosure statement:

Cronos Calendar uses Google data to authenticate users and provide calendar services. We comply with Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use. More info: https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy.
