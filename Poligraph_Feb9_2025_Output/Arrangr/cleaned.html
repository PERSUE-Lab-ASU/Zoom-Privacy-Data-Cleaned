Introduction

Information We Collect
We collect various types of information about individuals and their usage of the Service. Some of this information may be information that can identify you ("personal information"). We try to only collect personal information that will enhance the experience of our Users and facilitate the use of our Services, and to make it clear what information we are collecting and how it will be used.

Account Information
In the process of creating an account and setting up a profile, we may collect your name, email addresses, phone numbers, business name and role, photos, time zone, bio and other similar types of information.

Settings, Preferences & History
Arrangr collects and stores information about the preferences and settings of our Members and Users. This includes favorite places, conference platform details, timing preferences, event types, and more. Arrangr also stores information related to the history of our users, including recent contacts they have scheduled with, recent places and call information they have selected, meeting lengths selected, time of day selected, and more. Arrangr uses this information to make suggestions to Users, which may help them to conduct similar actions in the future.

Arrangr keeps log files that record the interactions of users with our Service. This logging records information about the nature of the interactions. This may include the type of action taken, the time, the IP Address, the type of device, the operating system, the browser, the name and email address of the user, the page viewed on our Site, the location, and similar information.

Remarketing and Online Advertising
We may use remarketing services to advertise on third-party websites, including platforms such as Google and social media networks, to previous visitors of our site. These advertisements may be displayed based on your past interactions with our website, and they may involve the use of cookies.

Page Tag Information
Arrangr uses third party tracking services, including Google Tag Manager and Facebook Pixel. These services employ cookies and page tags or web beacons to collect data and track usage, user statistics, and conversions from marketing efforts.

Referral Information
Arrangr may use cookies or other tracking mechanisms to identify when another User, website, or other service directed you to Arrangr.

Cookies
Arrangr, like many websites, uses cookies to enhance our Service, as well as to collect additional usage data. By using our Service, you expressly consent to our use of cookies, as described on this page. More information about what cookies are and how we use them can be found below.

What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small data files that we store on your device. Cookies allow us to keep you logged in, remember your preferences from previous pages and previous visits to our Site, among other things. Arrangr uses both persistent cookies, and session cookies. Session cookies are deleted once you leave the Site. Persistent cookies remain on your device even after closing your browser, to be used on subsequent visits to our Site.

Required or Essential Cookies
Some cookies are required for certain aspects of our Service to function properly. For example, when you log on to our Site, we use authentication cookies to remember who you are and verify that you are securely logged in.

Functionality & Preference Cookies
Some cookies enable additional functionality and provide a smoother experience on Arrangr. For example, we may use cookies to remember preferences or settings you have chosen (like your preferred time zone), and to remember what actions you have taken, so we don't have to bug you for these things multiple times.

Third Party Analytics Cookies
Arrangr uses some third party services, like Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel, to collect information about your use of the Site, allowing us to improve our Service and our marketing efforts. For example, analytics cookies may be used to track how many views a particular page gets, record bugs or difficulties in our processes, and analyze whether some of our marketing efforts are successful.

Targeting or Advertising Cookies
We may use cookies to market our Service to you on other sites. For example, after visiting Arrangr, you may see an avertisement for Arrangr on another site, which is enabled by advertising cookies.

Cookie Management
Most internet browsers automatically allow websites to use cookies. If you want to disable cookies, your browser will allow you to do so. Instructions for doing so are browser-specific, so you may want to your browser's documentation on how to do so. You should be aware that disabling cookies is likely to result in and adverse experience if you continue to use our Service.
