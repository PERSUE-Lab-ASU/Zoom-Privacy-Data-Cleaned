Zinnia Privacy Statement

Last Updated: February 2022

Thank you for your interest in Zinnia. We respect the privacy of our users, and the following privacy policy is intended to let you know what information we may collect about you through our website and how that information is used and shared.

When we refer to “Zinnia”, “we,” “us,” or “our” in this policy, we are referring to Zinnia, LLC, 5801 S.W. 6th Avenue, Topeka, Kansas 68606.

By using our website (the “Site”), you consent to the collection, storage, disclosure and use of information about you in accordance with this privacy policy.

Information We May Collect

Through our Site, we, directly or indirectly (through our service providers), may collect different kinds of information from or about you. More specifically: Information You Provide. We collect information from you when you use our Site, share information through the Site, provide information through web forms, participate in surveys, subscribe to newsletters or other subscription web services or otherwise communicate with us or make information available to us, including personal information such as:

Your name;
Your email address;
Your telephone number; and
Your company.

Automated Information. When you use the Site, our systems may automatically record information (“log data”) sent from your web browser or mobile application. Such information may include: your IP address, browser type and settings, operating system used, the date and time of your access and pages you visit, any links to external sites you open, the identity of your Internet web service provider or mobile carrier, and information about the type of device you are using, including any information your device may provide, such as device settings and unique device identifiers.

Cookies and Similar Technologies. We may use cookies (small text files placed on your device) and similar technologies such as web beacons and pixel tags to help provide our Site and collect data.

Cookies allow us to keep track of and store your preferences, enable your sign-in to our Site, present interest-based advertising, track your activities across the Site (including third party websites), combat fraud and analyze performance of the Site.

Analytics Technologies. We use our own log data and third party analytic technologies to collect information about how you use our Site and monitor the performance and usefulness of our Site. Third party analytics web services may combine the information they collect in connection with our Site with information they have independently collected over time and across different web sites. Many of these third party analytics companies collect and use information under their own privacy policies.

Information from Third Parties. We may get information about you from other parties with which we have a business relationship. This information may be combined with information that you provide to us and our partners, along with information that is automatically collected through our Site.

How We May Use the Collected Information

We may use the information we collect from you to provide products and services to you, including to provide our Site, as well as to maintain, protect, and improve our products and services, and to develop new products and services. We may also use the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

  • To personalize and make our Site more relevant and useful to you and others;
  • To provide you with information about Zinnia or its affiliates;
  • To offer to you other products or services, including third party products and services;
  • To enable you to interact with us, our partners and others;
  • To perform data analysis, including market research, trend analysis and demographic analysis;
  • To process transactions initiated by you or to fulfill any other purposes for which you provided the information;
  • To respond to court orders and legal investigations or as otherwise required or permitted by federal and state law;
  • To contact you; and
  • As directed by you or your representative.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share with third parties information that identifies you, except as described below:

Affiliates. We may share information about you, including personal information, with our affiliates, who will use it in accordance with our privacy policy.

Our Service Providers. We may engage third parties to provide certain services offered on our behalf, such as information collection and processing, data analysis, or satisfaction surveys. We require these providers to maintain information about you as confidential, and to only use the information to perform the services specified by us in a manner consistent with this privacy policy and in compliance with applicable laws.

Corporate and Asset Transactions. If we sell all or substantially all of our business or sell or transfer all or a material part of our assets, or are otherwise involved in a merger or transfer of all or a substantial part of our business, we may transfer all information we have collected and stored, including personal information, to the party or parties involved in the transaction as part of that transaction.

Legal Disclosures. It is possible that we will need to disclose information about you when required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we have a good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to:

Investigate, prevent, or take action regarding suspected or actual illegal activities or to assist government enforcement agencies;
Protect the security or integrity of our Site;
Investigate or defend ourselves against third party claims or allegations;
Protect the rights, interests, safety, and security of users of our Site or members of the public;
Protect against fraud or for risk management purposes; or
Comply with prudent legal practice.

Your Choices

You may make certain choices about our collection of your information. For example:

You may choose not to provide personal information, although that may impact your ability to register for or receive a particular product or service;

You may choose not to receive marketing email from us by following the unsubscribe link provided in one of those emails; and

You may also disable cookies and similar technologies as supported by your browser or device. However, our Site may not function properly if you disable these technologies.

Children/Minors

The Site is a general audience site and it is not directed to minors under the age of 18. We do not offer products or services to minors under the age of 18 or knowingly collect personal information from users in this age group. No one under the age of 18 should submit or post personal information through our online services. We may collect information regarding individuals under 18 years of age from their parents or legal guardians, but only as necessary to provide our products and services.

Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, personnel, and physical security measures designed to protect information we collect about you from loss, theft, and unauthorized use, disclosure, or modification. However, there is no method of transmitting, storing, or using data that is completely secure. This means that we cannot guarantee or warrant the security of information we collect about you.

Disclaimers

In relation to your contact details, we will retain your data for so long as controller is contractually obligated. In some circumstances we may retain your personal data for longer periods of time, for example where we are required to do so to meet legal, regulatory, tax or accounting requirements, in particular where it forms part of an audit trail created to record your use of our software products. For the device data we will retain your personal data for as long as is reasonably necessary for the purposes listed in this notice.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time, so please review it frequently. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are material, we will post the revised privacy policy to our Site as of the effective date of such changes.
