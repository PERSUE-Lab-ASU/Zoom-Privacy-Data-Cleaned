I. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1. Information collected through Systems

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect information sent to us by your computer, mobile phone, or other access device. This information includes the IP address from which you access the service; the date and time of access; information related to your browser, operating system and device; the website from which you came (known as the "referring URL"); on-site/app activities (such as pages viewed and items clicked) to measure and report statistics about user interactions on the service; and location information. This information is used to analyze trends and usage patterns all in an effort to better tailor our Services to your needs.

The Site does not recognize or follow the direction of “do not track” or “DNT” settings that are available in some web browsers.

2. Information you provide us by using the Service

In addition to the information provided automatically by your browser when you visit the Site, when you sign up, we create an account for you on our servers (“Account”) to access our Services. We will store information such as your first name, last name, job title, department, email address and the name and email of your manager.

To access and use elements of the knowledge transfer platform, we will ask you to submit your LinkedIn profile URL or professional bio.

When you participate in a meeting, our software will collect meeting recordings and transcripts.

By registering or contacting us you are authorizing us to collect, store and use your email address and other Personal Information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

3. Information collected by your employer

If you receive Sugarwork via your employer, they may provide us with your name, email address, office location, job title, experience level, and manager information. This information is used to invite you to the Sugarwork platform and to grant any benefits given to you by your employer.

4. Minors

Sugarwork does not knowingly collect or solicit information from anyone under the age of 18 or knowingly allow such persons to register or use the Services. If you are under 18, you may not register for Sugarwork or send any personal information to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 18 may provide any information to or on Sugarwork. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13, we will delete that information. If you believe that a child under 13 may have provided us personal information, please contact us at privacy@sugarwork.com.

Our vendors use your information only at our direction and in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
