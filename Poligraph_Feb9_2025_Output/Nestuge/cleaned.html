Information You Give

As you navigate through Nestuge, we and our third-party providers may collect information such as the pages you visit, search terms, device information, operating system and other user interactions.

You can check Flutterwave’s privacy policy

How we Use your Information

We collect your information for various purposes such as to

  • verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • enhance your user experience
  • show you personalized suggestions of events you might like
  • process your payments and payouts
  • build an inclusive product for both hosts and customers.
  • understand how you use the Platform and make it better for you and everyone else.
  • send you communications, notifications including by email.
  • help maintain, protect and improve our product.

How we Share your Information

With Event Hosts

When joining an event or class, we provide your information such as display name, phone number and email address to the merchant. We also provide platinum hosts the extra information they may require from their customers.

With third party

We will never sell your data to any third party. We share your data with our service providers to assist us in providing certain services such as analyzing how the platform is used, processing of payment and settlement, email marketing and awareness. These providers are obligated to secure the data they receive either directly from you or that we share with them. The information you share with the platform is also available to the Company and is bound by this same policy. In the case of financing, liquidation, acquisition or merger, we may share your information with our partners.

In order to protect our users’ rights and integrity of the Company and the Platform, we may share information about you if we are reasonably required to comply with the local laws, legal process and government requests.

Data Security & Retention

We value the security of your personal information and as such we protect this data according to generally acceptable standards. We provided our contact details where you can report individual hosts or events that go against your policies. Our support team will investigate and act appropriately.

As much as we try to secure your information the best way possible, we can not guarantee you absolute security since no method of data transmission or storage over the internet is 100%. Incase of any data leaks or breach, we will promptly notify you. We will only retain personal information on our servers for as long as is reasonably necessary as long as we are providing Services to you. If you choose to delete your account which you can inform us about via email, your information is stored on our servers to the extent necessary to comply with regulatory obligations and for the purpose of fraud monitoring, detection and prevention. Where we retain your Personal Data, we do so in compliance with limitation periods under the applicable law.
