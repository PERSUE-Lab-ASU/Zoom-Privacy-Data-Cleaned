The Information we collect and why

We collect personal information that you knowingly and voluntarily provide, for example when you make an enquiry about our services or when you choose to book an appointment over the phone or through our form online on our website www.motivatept.co.uk. This information may include (amongst other things) your name, address, telephone number and email address. This personal information is only used to respond to your enquiry or to process your booking. This information will also be stored in our client and trainer portal, which is under the subdomain, https://my.motivatept.co.uk/

Information Sent to Us by Your Web Browser.

We collect information that is sent to us by your web browser. This information includes your IP address, the date of your visit and the name of your browser and operating system. We collect this non-personal information to create web statistics that help us improve our website for our visitors.

Information Collected Using “Cookies”.

In addition, we may use cookies to store and collect information regarding your usage of our site so that we can deliver content specific to your interests and improve your experience whilst at our site. Cookies can only identify you as a unique, anonymous user of our website and do not contain any personal information about you. If you prefer, you can set your browser to block cookies or you can delete cookies once you have left our web site. Access to our website is not dependent upon the use of cookies and currently all features of our website will still be available even if you choose to block cookies.

Information Collected from 3rd Party.

In addition, for trainers we use their Zoom details to automatically generate meetings for online sessions. We store user email address for verification purposes. Meetings created from platform are also stored to show meeting details on platform itself. We retains trainer Zoom email address till he/she discounnects the app from the platform and meeting details are retained till payment for those sessions are verified.

Some of the cookies we use are provided by Google Analytics and these are used to collect information about how visitors use our site. We use this information to help us improve the site and the user experience of users. Again, these cookies collect information in an anonymous form, including numbers of visitors to the site, the pages visited and where visitors have come to the site from.
