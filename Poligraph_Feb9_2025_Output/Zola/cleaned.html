V. WHAT ARE YOUR CHOICES REGARDING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We offer you choices about the collection, use and sharing of your Personal Information.

  • Opt-out of Marketing Communications. You may choose not to receive marketing email communications from us by clicking on the unsubscribe link in our marketing emails or by updating your preferences in your account settings. You may also change your email address for receipt of such communications in your account settings.

  • Restricting Cookies. Most browsers will tell you how to stop accepting new Cookies, how to be notified when you receive a new Cookie, and how to disable existing Cookies. Please note, however, that without Cookies you may not be able to take full advantage of all of our Website's features. In addition, disabling Cookies may cancel opt-outs that rely on Cookies, such as web analytics or targeted advertising opt-outs. Please note that most opt-out mechanisms (including browser-based signals) are Cookie-based and will only affect the specific computer and browser that is used to submit an opt-out request. If you have multiple computers or change computers, or use multiple browsers, you will need to submit opt-out requests for each such computer or browser. In addition, if you delete, block or otherwise restrict cookies, you will need to renew your opt-out choices to ensure that any opt-out Cookies remain effective.

  • Opt-out of Google Analytics. If you do not want Google Analytics to gather information about how you interact with our Website while you are on our Website, please install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on available here: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout and employ it at the start of each session on our Website. A Cookie will be set on your browser that instructs this technology not to start for that session. Please note that the next time you access our Website the analytics will be reactivated, so you will have to disable the technology each time. If you reject the Cookies we use, you can still use certain aspects of our Website, but you may not be able to take full advantage of all of our Website's features.

  • Text Messages.  You may opt in to receive text messages from us in a variety of ways, including, but not limited to, by entering a phone number on the Website/Services. Consent to receive automated marketing text messages is not a condition of any purchase. You can opt-out of receiving further marketing text messages by responding with any of the following replies: STOP, END, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, or QUIT. For additional opt-out information, please review our Terms of Use.  

If you are resident of certain states in the U.S., you may also have the following rights regarding your Personal Information (each of which are subject to various exceptions and limitations):

  • Right to Opt-out of Targeted Advertising and Sale/Sharing of Information for Interest-Based Advertising. As we described above, we share your Personal Information with third-party partners who provide advertising and marketing services to us. If you would like to exercise your right to opt-out of targeted advertising, as well as the sale/sharing of your Personal Information, please visit our Privacy Center and fill out our Opt-Out Request Form.

  • Some browsers offer you the ability to opt out of Cookie-based and other online tracking, which may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" of your Personal Information. At this time, we endeavor to respond to browser-based opt-out signals sent through the Global Privacy Control: https://globalprivacycontrol.org/. We do not respond to do not track or other browser-based signals.

  • Right to Access Your Personal Information. You can request, up to two times every 12 months, that we provide you with a copy of the specific pieces of Personal Information we have collected about you. In some states, you also have the right to receive information on the categories of third parties or specific third parties with whom we might share your personal information.

  • Right to Request Correction or Deletion of Your Personal Information. You can request that we correct or delete the Personal Information we have collected about you.

  • Right Not to Receive Discriminatory Treatment. You will not be discriminated against for exercising any of the above rights. However, please keep in mind that some of our Services cannot be provided if we do not have your Personal Information.

  • Right to Appeal. You have the right to appeal our denial of any request you make under this section. To exercise your right to appeal, please contact us by email at privacy@zola.com and indicate that you are appealing our response to your previous request.

To exercise the rights described above, you must send us a request that (i) provides sufficient information to allow us to verify that you are the person about whom we have collected Personal Information, including by verifying the email address associated with the request and (ii) describes your request in sufficient detail to allow us to understand, evaluate and respond to it. Each request that meets both of these criteria will be considered a "Valid Request." We may not respond to requests that do not meet these criteria. We will only use Personal Information provided in a Valid Request to verify you and complete your request. You do not need a Zola account to submit a Valid Request. Only you or someone legally authorized to act on your behalf (an "Authorized Agent") may submit a Valid Request. If an Authorized Agent submits a Valid Request on your behalf, we may require written proof of authorization and verification of identity directly from you.

You or your Authorized Agent may submit a Valid Request by visiting our Privacy Center or calling us at (866) 243-2344. In our Privacy Center, you can submit a deletion or access request, or you can submit a request to opt out of targeted advertising and the sale/sharing of your Personal Information for interest-based advertising. For requests to correct your Personal Information, please email us at privacy@zola.com, specifying the Personal Information that is incorrect and how it should be changed.

We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect the confidentiality and security of information transmitted to us. TLS encrypts your payment information, name and postal address so only our payment processors, as applicable, are able to decode your information. Unfortunately, however, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your information, we do not guarantee or warrant the security of any information you transmit to or from our Website, and you do so at your own risk.

We urge you to create a strong password that is unique to Zola, and not to divulge it to anyone. Also remember to log off your account and close your browser window when you have finished your visit. You are responsible for securing your account credentials for any activities that occur under your account.
