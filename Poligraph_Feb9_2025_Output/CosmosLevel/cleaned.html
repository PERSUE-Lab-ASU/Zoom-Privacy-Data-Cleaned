cosmoslevel.com Privacy

 

Dear Visitors,

Thank you for using cosmoslevel.com. We urge you to read carefully both the Terms and Conditions applicable to the account type you’ve opted for and this Personal Data Processing and Confidentiality Policy to be fully informed about how we handle and secure the personal data you provide to us.

cosmoslevel.com is a versatile SaaS (Software as a Service) platform designed to streamline the interaction between professionals and clients within an appointment-based business model. This innovative app facilitates a comprehensive suite of features to enable efficient management of appointments, services, and professional-client engagement across various industries.

For professionals, cosmoslevel.com provides a robust ecosystem to create and manage their service offerings. They can setup their availability with a connected calendar, design multi-language offers, and configure location preferences for in-person meetings or online sessions via integrations with Google Meet and Zoom. Professionals can also leverage the platform to define custom questions, helping to curate personalized experiences for their clients at the point of booking. For added convenience, professionals have the option to sync with their Google Calendar, check for scheduling conflicts, and employ smart scheduling options like time slot increments and minimum notice requirements before bookings.

Clients benefit from a seamless booking experience through a concise wizard that guides them from selecting a service to choosing a convenient date and time. Products associated with service offers can be selected, and reminders for scheduled appointments are sent out via email, ensuring a smooth preparatory process.

Combining these features with an intuitive user interface, cosmoslevel.com aims to elevate professionals’ ability to attract and retain clients through efficient scheduling, customizable offerings, and seamless integrations, while providing clients with a convenient and reliable way to access and book the services they need. Whether handling individual appointments or managing a suite of services, cosmoslevel.com is geared to enhance organizational capabilities and improve the customer experience in a modern, digital-focused world.
