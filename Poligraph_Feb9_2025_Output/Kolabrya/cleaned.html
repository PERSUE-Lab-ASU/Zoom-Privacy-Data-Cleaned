1. Introduction to Kolabrya Welcome to Kolabrya. We specialize in enhancing workplace investigation productivity through our AI-driven web application, kolabrya.ai. Our platform is designed to analyze large volumes of documents and transcripts and generate analysis and reports.

​

2. Kolabrya.ai - AI Web Application Kolabrya.ai utilizes AI LLM technology to transform the way meetings are conducted, offering a unique and efficient meeting experience.

​

  • Sharing of User Data with Third-Party AI Platforms: Our platform is committed to maintaining transparency in how we handle user data. An essential component of our service involves the use of advanced AI-powered Large Language Models (LLMs) to process and analyze meeting transcriptions. When you use our platform, the content of your files, meetings, including discussions and the names of participants, is securely transmitted to these AI models for processing. Your data is housed in our Microsoft Azure regions and we do not use your data to share with 3rd parties to train AI models.

  • ​Data Collection and Use: We collect calendar information including meeting titles, links, dates, and times and meeting transcriptions. This information, obtained with user consent, is used to schedule the Kolabrya bot for joining meetings and providing our services. We also collect file name, prompts you use and content of the files to send to LLM to process and return responses.

  • Integration with Conference Tools: Kolabrya.ai integrates with various conferencing tools such as Google Meet, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams.

  • Specifically for Google : Kolabrya's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements and we ensure that this data is accessed and used solely for the purpose of enhancing meeting productivity.

  • Data Storage and Security: User data is stored securely in encrypted databases. After meetings, users have the option to delete their data from our systems.

  • Data Sharing and Processing: User data is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes. We use Large Language Models (LLMs) to analyze files, meeting data and generate insights, ensuring that this data does not contribute to the training of these models. 

    • Zero decryption policy : You data is not decrypted for any purposes.

  • Data retention : We retain data to provide the advertised services including history. This information is stored in our secure encrypted data bases as long as the users want. Data submitted through the Files is retained until the user deletes the file.

  • Data Subject Access Rights: We recognize and respect your rights regarding your personal data. As a user or participant of our platform, you are entitled to exercise the following rights:

  1. Right to Access: You have the right to request access to your personal data that we hold. This includes the right to be informed about the nature of the data being processed, the purposes of processing, and the recipients to whom the data is disclosed.

  2. Right to Rectification: If you find that any personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request corrections or updates to this data.

  3. Right to Erasure: Under certain conditions, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, commonly referred to as the 'right to be forgotten'.

  4. Right to Restriction of Processing: You may request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, which means that your data can only be held by us and not used further.

  5. Right to Data Portability: Where applicable, you have the right to receive the personal data that you have provided to us in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and also to request that we transmit this data to another controller.

  6. Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data, especially where the processing involves direct marketing.
