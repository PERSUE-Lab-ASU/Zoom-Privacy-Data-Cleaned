VideoSync ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our VideoSync service.

Our service integrates with Google, Zoom, Vimeo, and Stripe. When you authorize our service to access your accounts on these platforms, we may collect and process information as described in their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies for additional information.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with our services, comply with legal requirements, or resolve disputes.

8. Your Rights

You may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • Access to your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@videosync.cloud.
