Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2023

RoomIn is committed to protecting the privacy of its users and complying with all applicable data protection laws and regulations. This privacy policy describes how users' personal information is collected, used, and protected.


    1. Information collected

    RoomIn may collect the following personal information from users:

    1. Contact information: Name, email address.

    2. Profile Information: User profile information, such as username and profile photo.

    3. Device Information: Information about the device used to access the Application, such as device model, operating system version, IP address, and advertising identifiers.

    4. Usage Information: Information about how the user uses the application, such as the functions used, the time of use and the frequency of use.


    2. Use of information

    RoomIn uses users' personal information for the following purposes:

    1. To provide and improve the Application and its services.

    2. To send application-related communications, such as application updates and notifications.

    3. To personalize the user experience in the application.

    4. To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.


    RoomIn will not sell, rent, or share users' personal information with third parties, except in the following situations:

    1. When necessary to provide the Application and its services, such as to process payments or to send emails on behalf of the Application.

    2. When the user expressly authorizes it.

    3. When disclosure is required by law or in response to a court order.


    3. Protection of information

    RoomIn takes reasonable steps to protect users' personal information from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include, among others, physical, technical and administrative security measures.

    4. Rights of users

    Users have the right to access, rectify or delete their personal information at any time. You also have the right to object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances. Users can exercise these rights by sending a request by email to bargasjavier@gmail.com.

    5. Changes in the privacy policy

    RoomIn may update this privacy policy at any time. If material changes are made to the privacy policy, users will be notified via an in-app notification or email.
