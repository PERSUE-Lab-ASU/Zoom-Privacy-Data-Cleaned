Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to FaxSIPit, Inc. (“FaxSIPit,” “we,” “us”). This Online Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes what information we collect, how we collect it, and how it is used, disclosed, transferred and safeguarded. It also describes your choices regarding use, access and correction of your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to the information we collect through our website (“Site”) and our FaxSIPit Customer Portal (“Customer Portal”). The use of information collected through our Customer Portal shall be limited to the purpose of providing the service for which the Client has engaged FaxSIPit. If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy you may contact us as indicated in the “Contacting FaxSIPit” section below.


We may collect the following personal information from you: The types of personal information users of our site or Customer Portal may submit include: Contact Information, such as name, email address, mailing address, or phone number; Preference information, such as order history or marketing preferences; Unique Identifiers, such as username, account number or password; Information about your business, such as company name, Tax Identification Number (TIN), business type and business contact information; Personal information submitted by job applicants, such as a résumé, employment and education history, and salary information; and Log Files. As is true of most websites, we gather certain information automatically. This information may include Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, the files viewed on our site (e.g., HTML pages, graphics, etc.), operating system, date/time stamp, and/or clickstream data to analyze trends in the aggregate and administer the site.


Tracking technologies

FaxSIPit and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track users’ movements around the website, and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level, but if you choose to disable cookies, it may limit your use of certain features or functions on our Site or services. FaxSIPit’s Customer Portal collects information under the direction of its customers, and FaxSIPit has no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data it processes. If you do business with one of our customers that use our service, and would like to no longer be contacted by them please engage with them directly. As described more fully below, we may transfer personal information to companies that help us provide our service. Transfers to subsequent third parties are covered by the service agreements with our customers. We may use the information described above to: Send promotional materials or other marketing communications; Send you an order confirmation; Assess the needs of your business to determine suitable products; Send you requested product or service information; Respond to customer service requests; Administer your account; Respond to your questions and concerns; Improve our website and marketing efforts; Conduct research and analysis; Display content based upon your interests; and Process a job application for which you have applied. We may also use the information in other ways for which we provide specific notice at the time of collection. In addition, we use information collected through automated means for the purposes set forth above, including by (i) customizing users’ visits to our Site, (ii) analyzing how users interact with our Site and online advertisements; (iii) delivering targeted advertising and content tailored to our users’ interests; and (iv) managing our Site and other aspects of our business. We will retain personal data we process on behalf of our customers for as long as needed to provide services to our customers. FaxSIPit will retain this personal information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. At FaxSIPit we consider the content of a document sent via fax to be confidential. Please be advised that all faxes are encrypted during transit. We employ no mechanisms, automatic or otherwise, to scan the contents of your faxes. We are, therefore, not aware of what data or information is being faxed. Fax content We may share your information with third-party business partners, for instance, for the purpose of enhancing our products and services or so that they can market their products or services to you. If you do not want us to share your personal information with these companies, contact us at privacypolicy@FaxSIPit.com. Sharing with third parties Sharing with service providers We may share your information with third parties who provide services on our behalf to help with our business activities. These companies are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us. These services may include sending marketing communications or providing customer service. Legal Notice In certain situations, FaxSIPit may be required to disclose personal data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet law enforcement requirements.

We may also disclose personal information as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or other legal process, when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

If FaxSIPit is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets that affects this Privacy Policy, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our website, of any change in ownership, uses of your personal information, and choices you may have regarding your personal information.

We may also disclose your personal information to any other third party with your prior consent.
