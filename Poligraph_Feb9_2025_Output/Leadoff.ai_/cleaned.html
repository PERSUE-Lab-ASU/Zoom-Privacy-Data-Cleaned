1. Introduction

Leadoff.ai is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information. Our commitment extends to compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Personal Information Collected

  • Financial Information: Payment details if purchasing services.

  • Usage Data: Information on how services are used, including interaction data.

Methods of Collection

  • Through direct interactions when you register, purchase, or request services.

  • Automatically via cookies and analytics when you visit our website.

3. Use of Information

We use the collected data to:

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures such as encryption, secure server environments, and access controls to protect your data. In the event of a data breach, affected individuals will be notified in accordance with legal requirements.

6. User Rights
