Topicflow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2024

Topicflow Technologies Inc. ("Topicflow", "us", "we", "our") values and respects your privacy. We have prepared this privacy policy to explain the manner in which we collect, use and disclose personal information through our Website located at www.topicflow.com (the "Website"), our web application available at app.topicflow.com (the "Web App") and otherwise interact with you.

  1. COLLECTION AND USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
  1. Create an Account

You do not have to register to visit or use our Website. To access the Web App, you will need to create a user account using the available interfaces and connect your calendar(s) using your account credentials from other platforms (collectively with your calendar(s), the "Third Party Accounts"). By connecting a Third Party Account and using the Web   App through a Third Party Account, you permit us to access certain information from it, consistent with your Third Party Account's settings. You can control how much information we have access to by adjusting these settings. We use this information to create and administer your account. We strongly recommend that you do not disclose your password to any Third Party Account to anyone. We will never ask you for your password in any unsolicited communication (such as letters, phone calls or email messages). If you become aware of any unauthorized access to or use of your account, you must notify us immediately.

  1. Use the Web App

In order to use the Web App, you will need to connect your calendar. By doing so, you authorize us to access account information like your name and schedule in a manner consistent with your calendar's privacy settings. You may disconnect your calendar at any time in your settings. We do not have control over the manner in which your personal information is treated on your calendar. Please review the privacy policies that apply to your calendar for more information.

We have access to all information you add to your calendar events (e.g. the event title, attendees, start and end times, date, description, and any information you input into the Web App). You hereby authorize us to access, collect, and use this information to: provide the Web App; provide support (including bug searches, updating, and troubleshooting); and improve and develop our products and services. We will not use this information for marketing or disclose it to a third party without your express consent.

  1. Marketing Communications and Advertisements

If you sign-up to receive electronic marketing communications such as emails, we will collect your email address. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link included at the bottom of each message. Alternatively, you can opt-out of receiving electronic marketing communications by contacting us at the contact information provided in the "Contact Us" section below. Please note that you may continue to receive transactional or account-related communications from us.

If you engage with our marketing campaigns on social media websites such as engaging with an influencer's post, we may collect information (e.g. likes, shares, and comments) for the purposes of evaluating the effectiveness of our marketing campaign.

  1. Surveys and Research

From time to time, we may offer you the opportunity to participate in one of our surveys or other research projects. The information obtained through our surveys and customer research is used in an aggregated, non-personally identifiable form to help us understand our customers and to enhance our product and service offerings.

  1. Careers

If you apply for a job with us, you may provide us with certain personal information about yourself, such as information contained in a resume, cover letter, or similar employment-related materials. We use this information for the purpose of processing and responding to your application for current and future career opportunities.

  1. Contact Us

When you contact us with a comment, question or complaint through email or the chat function on the Website, you may be asked for information that identifies you, such as your name and email address, along with additional information we need to help us promptly answer your question or respond to your comment. We may retain this information to assist you in the future and to improve our customer service and service offerings.

We also collect certain technical and usage information when you use the Website and the Web App, such as the type of device, browser, and operating system you are using, your Internet service provider or mobile carrier, unique device identifier, IDFA or IDFV, MAC address, IP address, location, device and browser settings, the webpages and mobile apps you use, advertisements you see and interact with, and certain Website usage information. The Website uses a technology called "cookies". You may set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie or to not accept certain cookies.
