Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 7, 2022

Welcome to Fanclb websites or mobile applications (as the case may be) operated by Genwin LLC, (including its subsidiaries, affiliates, agents, and service providers, collectively, “Genwin,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). Genwin provides the Fanclb platform to you and to creators through its website at Fanclb.com, Genwin.io , Genwin.app and attendant mobile applications and related services. Fanclb is a platform where Creators can create their own Fanclb websites in order to engage directly with their Fans and monetize their content. Creators can upload and manage content; manage social media links, marketing, and advertising campaigns; engage with their Fans; schedule and publish messages and online meeting requests; analyze the results of these activities; create and ticket events; create subscription packages and offerings; create crowdfunding campaigns and collect payments/donations; and perform a host of other functions (Collectively, we refer to these tools as our “Services”).

This updated Privacy Policy applies to Fans, Creators and all users of our platform, and is part of our Terms of Use.

By using our platform and our Services, you agree that your personal information that you provide directly to us, or that we collect through your use of the platform, may be transferred to and stored in the United States and handled as described in this Policy.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (“EEA”), please see also our GDPR Privacy Notice which supplements this Privacy Policy.

If you are a resident of the State of California in the United States, please see also our California Privacy Statement which supplements this Privacy Policy.

Information You Provide Through Your Account

This is information that you provide to us through text fields, such as your name, payment information and benefits. The information we collect differs depending on whether you make an account, become a Fan or become a Creator.

    • First and Last Name

    • Email Address

    • Username

    • Password

    • State and Country of Residence

You may also sign up using a Facebook, Apple or Google account. We will ask for permission to access basic information from your Facebook, Apple or Google account, such as your name, email and profile picture. You can choose to stop sharing that information with us at any time by going to Facebook or Google to remove Fanclb’s access to that account and your ability to log in.

You also have the option to add more information to your public profile, such as a location, social media links and an about section, which we store along with any comments or messages you submit on Fanclb.

Fans

A fan is someone who joins Fanclb’s platform to access a creator’s Fanclb content, products, events & membership. As a fan you must provide your payment information to Creator’s linked payment processor’s vendor & account. You can see the Privacy Policy for these payment processor vendors on the Stripe, Authorize.net , SparrowOne , Worldline , PayU and Checkout sites. Fanclb does not access or store your full card number as this sensitive information is stored directly on a secure compliant storage provided by Very Good Security. Very good security provide us with a token that represents your account, your card’s expiration date, card type and the last four digits of your card number. You can see the Privacy Policy of Very Good Security.

We collect and process information about the creators you engage with their content, the level at which you support them, what benefits you receive and how often you support them. As a fan, if you select a benefit tier with a physical benefit then you will provide your shipping information, including phone number, so the creator or Fanclb can ship you the benefit or provide updates related to the shipment.

Creators

A creator is someone who creates and provides content for their fans through Fanclb’s platform. To become a creator, you must create a Fanclb that describes what you are creating and any benefits you are offering and upload your content, events and products details. To receive payouts you have to create an account with one of our payment partners: Authorize.net, SparrowOne, PayPal, Stripe or PayU. We may also require your bank account information to process payouts. You must also provide us with additional information for tax purposes. Depending on your location you may have to fill out a form with some combination of your (First and Last Name, Address, Social Security Number or Employer Identification Number, Country of Citizenship, Foreign Tax Identification Number, Date of Birth, Your Nonprofit Registration Number, if you are a nonprofit business, Bank Account Information for Payouts, Phone Number)

Additional Information We Collect

We collect information automatically as you navigate the site or through our third-party analytics providers. We may store usage information such as the type of device you use to access Fanclb, your operating system, browser type, IP address, device ID, the pages you visit or request, links clicked, referring sites, user interactions and your search terms. User Surveys

From time to time, we may solicit your participation in user surveys. In order to better understand and serve our user base, we may ask you for demographic information, including information related to your gender, ethnicity, race, age, sexual orientation, earnings and accessibility, which you may choose to share or not. We collect such information to ensure we provide fair and equal access to our services and to showcase creators to fans for greater discoverability. We will store your survey responses as and when you participate in the surveys.

Event Information

We may request information from you at in-person and online events. This is done to better tailor the event experience to those attending. This information may include your name, email address, payment details, mobile phone number and QR code, demographic information, social media and other online accounts you maintain, details around the types of works you create, and survey or feedback responses.

Blogs

We may collect and store your email address, your comments and your profile information from our blog sites to better understand your interests and improve our services to you.

We process your information to:

  • verify your identity to comply with US federal, state, and international laws

  • allow you to pay and access content, products, events, exclusive memberships for a creator’s Fanclb

  • send merchandise to your shipping address
  • send you emails relevant to your usage, as controlled by your email preferences
  • reply to your questions

  • understand how you use the service and create better tools for creators to serve fans
