3. Retention and use period of personal information

In principle, the company destroys users' personal information without delay after the purpose of collecting and using personal information is achieved. However, the following information is retained for the specified period for the following reasons:

a) Reasons for information preservation according to company internal policy

  • General member personal information - reason for retention: Prevent confusion and illegal use of the service
    • Retention period: 1 year
  • Service use-related information (service use record, access log, access IP information)
    • Reasons for preservation: Protection of Communications Secrets Act
    • Retention period: 3 months
  • Member personal information using electronic payment
    • Reasons for preservation: Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce Transactions, etc.
    • Retention period: 5 years
  • Personal information of students and parents collected during entrance examination consulting
    • Reasons for preservation: Consulting history provided upon request, refund, etc.
    • Retention period : 2 years after achievement of purpose (completion of entrance examination consulting)

b) Reasons for information preservation according to related laws

If it is necessary to preserve it in accordance with the provisions of related laws, such as the Commercial Act, the Consumer Protection Act in e-commerce, etc., the company retains the member information for a certain period stipulated by the relevant laws. In this case, the company uses the information to be preserved only for the purpose of its preservation, and the retention period is as follows.
  • Log records on display/advertising
    • Reasons for preservation: Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce Transactions, etc.
    • Retention period : 6 months
  • Records on contract or subscription withdrawal
    • Reasons for preservation: Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce Transactions, etc.
    • Retention period : 5 years
  • Records on payment and supply of goods, etc.
    • Reasons for preservation: Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce Transactions, etc.
    • Retention period : 5 years
  • Records on consumer complaints or dispute settlement
    • Reasons for preservation: Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce Transactions, etc.
    • Retention period : 3 years

4. Personal information destruction procedure and methoda

In principle, personal information of users is destroyed without delay when the purpose of collecting and using personal information is achieved. The procedure and method of destroying personal information are as follows:
  • Destruction Procedure
    • The information entered by the user for membership registration, etc., is transferred to a separate DB (separate filing cabinet in the case of paper) after the purpose is achieved, and is scheduled according to the reason for information protection according to internal policies and other related laws (refer to retention and use period) It is destroyed after the period is saved.
  • Method of destruction
    • Personal information printed on paper is destroyed by shredding or incineration.
    • Personal information stored in the form of electronic files is deleted using a technical method that cannot reproduce the record.
  • Destruction of personal information of dormant accounts
    • In accordance with Article 29 of the Act of Promotion of Information and Communication Network Utilization and Information Protection, and Article 16 of the Enforcement Decree of the same Act, personal information of dormant accounts that have not used the service for one year after membership registration may be destroyed or stored separately.
    • The Company notifies users of dormant accounts up to 30 days before the expiration date, and the notification details are as follows:
      • Notification content: The fact that personal information is destroyed or stored separately, the date and time of destruction, and personal information to be destroyed
      • Notification method: email, phone, fax, etc.
    • The exceptions below can be retained without destruction even after 1 year.
      • In case a separate period is set by other laws
      • If the period is set differently at the request of the user

7. Matters concerning the installation, operation and rejection of automatic personal information collection devices

The company uses "cookies" that store and retrieve user information from time to time in order to provide personalized and customized services. Cookies are very small text files sent by the server used to operate the website to the user's browser and are stored on the user's computer's hard disk. Cookies are used to provide customized information optimized to users by analyzing the company's services and usage patterns used by users.

a) Purpose of use of cookies

  • Provides a targeted marketing and personalized service and offers Inclass login service by analyzing the access frequency and visit time of members and non-members, identifying users' tastes and interests, tracking traces and identifying the degree of participation in various events and the number of visits.
  • Users have the option of installing cookies. Therefore, the user may allow all cookies by setting options in the web browser, check each time a cookie is saved, or refuse to save all cookies. However, if you refuse to store cookies, some services of companies that require login may be difficult to use.

b) Methods to specify whether to allow installation of cookies

Internet Explorer web browser
  • Select [Internet Options] from the [Tools] menu at the top of the web browser.
  • Click the [Personal Information] tab.
  • You can set cookies for each site through [Site(S)], [Advanced(V)].
Chrome web browser
  • Select the [Settings] menu on the right side of the web browser.
  • [Show advanced settings] at the bottom of the screen.
  • You can set cookies by selecting [Content Settings] in the [Personal Information] tab.
  • If you refuse to save cookies, it may be difficult to use some services that require login.

8. Technical/administrative protection measures for personal information

The company takes the following technical/administrative measures to ensure safety so that personal information is not lost, stolen, leaked, altered or damaged in processing users' personal information.
  • Personal information encryption The company encrypts personal information such as passwords and payment methods according to the standards required by laws and regulations, and protects users' personal information by taking separate security measures such as encrypting files and transmission data for important data.
  • Countermeasures against hacking, etc.
    • The company is doing its best to prevent the leakage or damage of members' personal information by hacking or computer viruses.
    • The system is installed in an area where access is controlled from outside, and an intrusion prevention device is used and an intrusion detection system is installed to monitor 24 hours a day.
    • The vaccine program is updated periodically to prevent personal information from being infringed by applying it as soon as the vaccine is released if a virus suddenly appears.
    • Through encrypted communication, etc., personal information can be safely transmitted over the network.

b) Administrative protection measures

  • Minimization of handling staff and training
    • The company minimizes the number of employees handling personal information to the person in charge, and assigns and manages a separate password for this, and emphasizes compliance with the personal information handling policy through occasional training for the person in charge.
  • Operation of a dedicated organization for personal information protection
    • The company's personal information-related processing staff is limited to the person in charge, and the company always emphasizes compliance with the company's personal information processing policy by providing training to the person in charge.

In addition, if the wrong personal information has already been provided to a third party, we will notify the third party of the correction result without delay so that the correction can be made.
