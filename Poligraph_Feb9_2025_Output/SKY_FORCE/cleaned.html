Privacy Policy

Our company considers that it is a social obligation to recognize the importance of personal information handled through IT-related business, comply with the law and regulations, and protect customer's information with utmost care. We have established a privacy policy to make all users relieved, and follow this policy to handle information strictly.

  1. Use and Provision of Personal Information

    Our company uses personal information within the scope of predetermined purpose in consideration of business content and scale, and will not use it beyond the purpose. In addition, our company will not provide it without consent of individuals and corporations unless there is a request for disclosure based on law from a institution such as a court and police agency. To do so, our company prepares procedures for handling personal and corporate information to educate employees.

  2. Observance of Laws and Regulations

    For handling personal information, our company will confirm and comply with the laws and regulations regarding the handling of personal information, related guidelines and other national guidelines, and ordinaces.

  3. Campaign of Personal Information Protection

    In order to ensure that personal information is handled appropriately and safely, our company have established management system and internal rules as "Personal Information Protection Management System" to educate employees, and to prevent unauthorized access, loss, destruction, falsification and leakage of personal information appropriately. In addition, our company will detect any deficiencies at an early stage through daily checks and internal audits when any failures occur. Then prevent recurrence by investigating the cause and taking corrective measure.

  4. Complaints and Consultaions Regarding the Handling of Personal Information

    Our company sets up a "Personal Infomation Inquiry Desk" as a reception for complaints and consultations regarding the handling of personal information, and responds without delay.

  5. Continuous Improvement of Personal Information Protection Management System

    Our company regularly reviews the "Personal Information Protection Management System" and strive for continuous improvement in order to realize the protection of personal information in response to changes in our business environment, social conditions, and information technology.
