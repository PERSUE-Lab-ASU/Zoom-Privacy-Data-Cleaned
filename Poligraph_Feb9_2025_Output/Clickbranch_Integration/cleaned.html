PRIVACY NOTICE

Canal Park LLC is committed to protecting the privacy of your information and providing you with the best experience possible while using our products and visiting our websites. This privacy notice applies to all Canal Park, LLC websites, products and services. This notice will describe what information we collect, how we collect it and how it is used. If there is a change to our practices for handling personal information, it will be reflected here. Additionally, there may be supplemental privacy notices or policies that are specific to particular services. These notices will be available through those services where and when applicable.

Data Protection Office

Canal Park, LLC has appointed an internal privacy office for you to contact if you have any questions or concerns about personal data policies or practices. The privacy office can be reached at privacy@bvs.com.

How We Collect and Use (Process) Your Information

Canal Park, LLC will only collect and store information you provide to us through our online forms, such as contact requests. This could include name, title, institution, telephone number, ZIP code, email address and any other information you decide to leave in the questions or comments fields.

The information you provide is used solely for the purposes of delivering the functionality of the service you have purchased. This may additionally include newsletter subscriptions, marketing and sales communications. You may unsubscribe from these communications at any time.

Third-Party Sharing

Canal Park, LLC does not collect, sell or disclose any personal information regarding the use of any website, product or service with third parties. Should Canal Park, LLC engage a third party, and with the exclusion of complying with regulatory agencies, law enforcement, or any other legal obligations, you will be notified and required to give explicit consent prior to the sharing of any data.

Use of CANAL PARK, LLC Websites

Canal Park, LLC’s website collects certain information automatically and stores it in log files. The information may include internet protocol (IP) addresses, the region or general location where your computer or device is accessing the internet, browser type, operating system and other usage information about the use of our website. We use this information to help us design our site to better suit our users’ needs. We may also use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website, analyze trends, track visitor movements, and gather broad demographic information that assists us in identifying visitor preferences.

Transferring Personal Data from the E.U. to the U.S.

Canal Park, LLC is headquartered in the United States. Information we collect from you will be processed in the United States. The United States has not sought nor received a finding of “adequacy” from the European Union under Article 45 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Canal Park, LLC relies on derogations for specific situations as set forth in Article 49 of the GDPR. In particular, Canal Park, LLC collects and transfers to the U.S. personal data only (with your consent) to contract with you; or to fulfill a compelling legitimate interest of Canal Park, LLC in a manner that does not outweigh your rights and freedoms. Canal Park, LLC endeavors to apply suitable safeguards to protect the privacy and security of your personal data and to use it only consistent with your relationship with Canal Park, LLC and the practices described in this privacy notice. Canal Park, LLC also enters into data processing agreements and model clauses with its vendors whenever feasible and appropriate.

Data Subject Rights

This privacy notice is intended to provide you with information about what personal data CANAL PARK, LLC collects about you and how it is used. If you have any questions, please contact us at privacy@bvs.com.

You have the right to request a review, alteration of, and/or deletion of personal information CANAL PARK, LLC may have about you. To do so, please contact us at privacy@bvs.com. Canal Park, LLC will comply with the request within 45 days, which will include the following information, at a minimum:

  1. The categories of personal information about you that Canal Park, LLC has collected
  2. The categories of sources from which the personal information was collected
  3. The categories of personal information about you that Canal Park, LLC has sold to a third party
  4. The categories of personal information about you that Canal Park, LLC has disclosed for a business purpose
  5. The categories of third parties to whom Canal Park, LLC has sold or disclosed personal information
  6. The business or commercial purpose for which personal information was collected or sold
  7. The "specific pieces" of personal information Canal Park, LLC collected about you

Personal Information Safeguards

Canal Park, LLC secures all information collected, transmitted and stored using a combination of physical, logical and administrative controls. The security of our environment is audited annually through a System and Organization Controls (SOC) 2 Type 2 report. Additionally, we have an independent third party perform security assessments and penetration testing on the network and applications.

Further, we train our employees about the importance of confidentiality and maintaining the privacy and security of your information. We commit to taking appropriate disciplinary measures to enforce our employees' privacy responsibilities.

Data Storage and Retention

The information you provide to us via our online forms is stored, accessed and used by Canal Park, LLC to contact you regarding the type of information requested. The information provided is stored in perpetuity unless you specifically request that information to be purged. All requests to purge your information should be made to privacy@bvs.com.

Data storage during the use of our products is outlined in the contract, but it is limited to the information that is required to effectively use the product. The data stored as use of our product belongs to each customer and can be removed upon written request to the Canal Park, LLC privacy office, privacy@bvs.com.

Changes and Updates to the Privacy Notice

As organizational requirements change from time to time, this privacy notice is expected to change as well. We reserve the right to amend the privacy notice at any time, for any reason, without notice to you, other than the posting of the amended privacy notice at this site. We may email periodic reminders of our notices, and terms and conditions, but you should check our site frequently to see the current privacy notice that is in effect and any changes that may have been made to it.
