PII and Non-PII may be collected anytime you access or use a Payfunnels website. PII and Non-PII may also be collected by Payfunnels during in-person discussions, telephone conversations, and electronic or other written communications. Payfunnels also collects information via cookies; blocking cookies may render you unable to access certain Payfunnels products, services, or websites.

Payfunnels may also share PII when required by law, or when you have expressly requested Payfunnels to do so. Because Non-PII does not personally identify You, Payfunnels reserves the right to use and disclose to third parties Non-PII for any purpose.

Payfunnels maintains reasonable physical, administrative and technical safeguards to protect PII from loss, misuse, or unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. Payfunnels enforces its Privacy Policy via self-assessment.
