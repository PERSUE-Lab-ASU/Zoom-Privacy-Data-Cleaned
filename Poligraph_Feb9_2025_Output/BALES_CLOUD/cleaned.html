Personal Information Protection Policy

Privacy Policy

Smartcamp Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as the “Company”) is developing business in the areas of SaaS support, media, and blockchain. We will comply with the laws on the protection of personal information and other relevant laws and regulations and ensure to make accountable responses as a corporation with the establishment of this Personal Information Protection Policy and a personal information management system, in order to appropriately acquire, use, and provide personal information handled by the Company.

Compliance with laws, regulations, and norms

The Company will comply with the laws, regulations, guidelines established by the national government, and other norms on the handling of personal information. To do that, we will appropriately operate our personal information protection management system established in compliance with the Japanese Industrial Standards ”Personal information protection management systems — Requirements” (JIS Q 15001).

Security measures for personal information

The Company will take appropriate measures for the prevention and correction of divulgence, loss, or damage of personal information to ensure the accuracy and security of personal information.

Prohibition against unauthorized use

The Company will appropriately acquire, use, and provide personal information in consideration of the contents and size of the business. That includes avoiding the handling of personal information beyond the scope necessary to achieve the specified purpose of use and taking measures for it.

Outsourcing of handling of personal information

If the Company outsources all or a part of the handling of personal information, the Company will provide necessary and appropriate supervision to the outsourcee to ensure the security management of personal information the handling of which is outsourced.

Appropriate response to complaint and consultation

The Company will endeavor to make appropriate and prompt response to complaints and consultations on the handling of personal information. We will also accept requests, etc. for disclosure or the like (notification of the purpose of use, disclosure, correction, addition or deletion, and suspension of use or provision) of personal data held by the Company. For the procedures for request for disclosure or the like, please send an email to the contact for complaints and consultations on personal information.

Review and improvement

The Company will review the content of this Personal Information Protection Policy as necessary and endeavor to continuously improve the personal information protection management system.
