Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

SalesBop ("we," "us," or "our") is an innovative platform committed to providing sales teams with Artificial Intelligence (AI)-powered coaching and actionable insights from their sales call recordings. Our software aims to transform the way sales teams perform and learn, optimizing success rates and driving business growth.

At SalesBop, we understand that privacy is a fundamental human right, and we are committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect from our users, how this information is used, and how it is protected.

The Privacy Policy applies to all users of our platform and services, including individuals who have registered for a SalesBop account and those who visit our website. By using our platform or services, you agree to the collection, use, disclosure, and procedures this Privacy Policy describes.

Please note that this policy does not extend to third-party websites or services, such as those that may be linked from our services or our website, so we advise you to familiarize yourself with the privacy policies provided by these third-party services.

This Privacy Policy is an integral part of our Terms of Service. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, or our practices in relation to your personal information, please contact us using the details provided in the "Contact Information" section of this Policy.

2. Definitions

In this Privacy Policy, the following terms are defined as follows:

2.1 "Personal Information" refers to any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual. An identifiable individual is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, in particular by reference to an identifier such as a name, an identification number, location data, an online identifier, or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural, or social identity of that individual.

2.2 "Anonymized Information" refers to data that has been processed in such a manner that it can no longer be associated with an identified or identifiable individual, thereby it does not constitute personal information.

2.3 "User" refers to any individual who uses our platform or services, including individuals who have registered for a SalesBop account and those who visit our website.

2.4 "Services" refers to the sales training and analytics platform provided by SalesBop, which includes AI-powered coaching and actionable insights from sales call recordings.

2.5 "Third-party Services" refers to services provided by entities other than SalesBop, including but not limited to transcription services, web hosting, database management, and AI analytics.

3.1 Agreement to Privacy Policy: By using our services or accessing our website, you are confirming that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and our data collection practices as detailed herein. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is to not use our services.

3.2 Consent to Collect and Use Information: When you register for a SalesBop account or use our services, we will explicitly ask for your consent to collect, use, and process your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. By giving your consent, you are granting us permission to perform those actions.

3.3 Consent to Share Information: We may also seek your explicit consent before sharing your personal information with third parties for purposes not described in this Privacy Policy.

3.4 Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal. If you want to withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal information, please contact us using the details provided in the "Contact Information" section of this Policy.

4. Information We Collect

4.1 User-provided Information: When you use our services or access our website, you may provide and we may collect what is generally called "personally identifiable" information, or "personal information," which is information that specifically identifies an individual. Examples of personal information include name, email address, mailing address, mobile phone number, and credit card or other billing information.

4.2 Sales Call Recordings: As part of our service, we analyze sales call recordings that you upload to our platform.

4.3 Information from Use of our Services: When you use our services, we may collect information about your engagement with and utilization of our services, such as processor and memory usage, storage capacity, navigation of our services, and system-level metrics.

4.4 Automatic Data Collection: We may collect certain information automatically when you use the Services, such as your Internet protocol (IP) address, user settings, MAC address, cookie identifiers, mobile carrier, details about your browser, operating system or device, location information, Internet service provider, pages that you visit before, during and after using the Services, and other information about how you use the Services.

5. Use of the Information

5.1 General Uses: We use the information we collect about you (including personal information, to the extent applicable) for a variety of purposes, including to:

Provide, operate, maintain, improve, and promote our services;

Enable you to access and use our services, including uploading, downloading, collaborating on, and sharing content;

Process and complete transactions, and send you related information, including purchase confirmations and invoices;

7. Security

7.1 Commitment to Security: We are committed to protecting your personal information. We use a variety of security measures, including encryption and authentication tools, to help protect and maintain the security, integrity, and availability of your information.

7.2 Secure Data Transfer: All data exchanged with our Services is protected using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology. We follow generally accepted standards to protect the personal data submitted to us, both during transmission and once it is received.

7.3 Data Breach Procedures: In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will act promptly to mitigate the impact of the breach and notify any affected users without undue delay, as required by applicable laws. We will also notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, if required.

7.4 User Responsibility: Your account is protected by your account password, and we encourage you to take steps to keep your personal information safe by not disclosing your password and by logging out of your account after each use. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential and for any activities or actions under your account.

7.5 Limitation of Liability: While we implement safeguards designed to protect your information, no security system is impenetrable, and due to the inherent nature of the Internet, we cannot guarantee that data during transmission through the Internet or while stored on our systems or otherwise in our care is absolutely safe from intrusion by others.

7.6 Data Minimization and Retention: We adhere to the principle of data minimization and ensure that personal data is only collected, processed, and stored as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected. We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7.7 Rights of Data Subjects: As a data subject, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data, as well as the right to restrict or object to certain types of processing of your data. You also have the right to data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@salesbop.io. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

7.8 International Transfers: If we transfer your personal data outside of the European Economic Area (EEA), we will ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with GDPR requirements.
