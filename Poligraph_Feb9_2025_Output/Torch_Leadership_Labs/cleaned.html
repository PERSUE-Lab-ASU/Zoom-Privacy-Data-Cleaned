This Privacy Policy is incorporated by reference into the Torch Master Services Agreement (the “Agreement“). All capitalized terms not defined in this Privacy Policy have the meaning set forth in the Agreement. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Website and users of the Torch Service subject to the Agreement (“user”, “you”, “your”), and your use of Torch indicates your consent to this policy and our data processing practices.

If Torch processes Personal Information in the role of Data Processor under the terms of a commercial agreement, and you wish to exercise any of your rights with respect to your Personal Information for which we are a Data Processor, please contact your employer (the Customer) directly.  Additional information on data subject rights for cases where Torch is the Data Controller is included below.

We will update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our data processing.

How We Collect Information

  • Information you provide.  The information you provide in order to access or use our Service or Website, including registration information, account information, profile details, inquiries information, and customer support requests.
  • Automatic Information. We may collect information about how you use our Service or Website, such as the types of content you view or engage with, and the frequency and duration of your activities, or where you are inquiring information from. You have options to control or limit how we or our partners use cookies and similar technologies. For more information, please see www.allaboutcookies.org/cookies.  If you do not wish to receive cookies, you may set your browser to reject cookies or to alert you when a cookie is placed on your computer. If you limit or disable the use of cookies when you visit our Websites and Service, you may be unable to use their full functionality.

How Do We Use The Information We Collect?

We use the information we collect in order to:

As Part of a Merger or Sale of Business

Use of AI Services

As of August 2024, Torch uses AI services to enhance the coaching experience. We do not use your data to train AI models, and we filter all inputs to remove personal and sensitive information. The AI services used are provided by our cloud infrastructure provider and data is not shared or transmitted elsewhere. We maintain strict controls for our AI summarization, do not share this information with other third parties, and customers may opt out by contacting customer support.

How Do We Share Your Personal Information?

We do not sell, or share your Personal Information except with your prior consent and we do not share Personal Information with third parties for their own use. To help us provide the Torch Service, we do share Personal Information with sub-processors, companies which provide specialized services we use within the Torch platform. A current list of sub-processors is available at:
https://torch.io/security/torch-subprocessors/

All sub-processors are evaluated to ensure their data security practices are robust, and Torch has contractual agreements governing the transfer and protection of Personal Information.

How We Secure Your Data

We take the security of your Personal Information seriously. Torch employs industry-standard security measures to protect your Personal Information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. We use secure cloud infrastructure with encryption for data in transit and at rest and access to Personal Information is restricted to authorized personnel. While no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, we regularly update our security practices to match industry best-practices.

How We Retain Your Data

We retain Personal Information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected including contractual, legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Telemetry data is stored for no more than 90 days unless a longer retention period is required by law or regulatory obligations. See below for additional information on your rights regarding personal data we have collected or otherwise processed.

Cookie Policy

The Service uses cookies and similar technologies such as pixel tags and web beacons (collectively, “Cookies”) to enable our servers to recognize your web browser, tell us how and when you visit and use our Service, analyze trends, learn about our user base and operate and improve our Service. Cookies are small pieces of data placed on your computer, tablet, phone or other device when you use that device to access our Service. We may also supplement the information we collect from you with information received from third parties, including third parties that have placed their own Cookies on your device(s). Please note that because of our use of Cookies, the Service do not support “Do Not Track” requests sent from a browser at this time.

We, or contracted third party sub-processors, may automatically collect log files and other information about your usage of the Torch Service. For example, we use technologies like cookies, session replay tools, and similar technologies to help us analyze performance, customize our Service, serve content, measure promotional effectiveness and promote trust and safety. You may delete and block all cookies via your browser, but parts of the Service will not perform as expected.

As outlined above, information on your connection and browser is automatically collected. This information will be collected every time you access the Service and it will be used for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.
