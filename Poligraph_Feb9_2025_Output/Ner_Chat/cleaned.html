Use of the Ner Chat Inc. Website

As is true of most other websites, Ner Chat Inc.’s website collects certain information automatically and stores it in log files. The information may include internet protocol (IP) addresses, the region or general location where your computer or device is accessing the internet, browser type, operating system and other usage information about the use of Ner Chat Inc.’s website, including a history of the pages you view. We use this information to help us design our site to better suit our users’ needs. We may also use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website, analyze trends, track visitor movements, and gather broad demographic information that assists us in identifying visitor preferences.

Ner Chat Inc. has a legitimate interest in understanding how members, customers and potential customers use its website. This assists Ner Chat Inc. with providing more relevant products and services, with communicating value to our sponsors and corporate members, and with providing appropriate staffing to meet member and customer needs.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Ner Chat Inc. uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website, analyze site traffic, and improve our services. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize repeat visitors, save preferences, and provide personalized content.


Types of Cookies We Use:

  1. Essential Cookies: These are required for website functionality and cannot be disabled.

  2. Performance Cookies: These track website usage data to improve site performance and user experience.

  3. Functional Cookies: These remember your preferences and settings for a personalized experience.

  4. Marketing Cookies: These track your browsing activity to provide relevant ads and promotions.

How We Use Tracking Technologies:

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Intercom, and Cloudflare for analytics, customer support, and security purposes. These services may set their cookies when you interact with our website.

Data Subject rights

The European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other countries’ privacy laws provide certain rights for data subjects. Data Subject rights under GDPR include the following:

  • Right to be informed

  • Right of access

  • Right to rectification

  • Right to erasure

  • Right to restrict processing

  • Right of data portability

  • Right to object

  • Rights related to automated decision making including profiling


This Privacy Notice is intended to provide you with information about what personal data Ner Chat Inc. collects about you and how it is used. 

If you wish to confirm that Ner Chat Inc. is processing your personal data, or to have access to the personal data Ner Chat Inc. may have about you, please contact us.

You may also request information about: the purpose of the processing; the categories of personal data concerned; who else outside Ner Chat Inc. might have received the data from Ner Chat Inc.; what the source of the information was (if you didn’t provide it directly to Ner Chat Inc.); and how long it will be stored. You have a right to correct (rectify) the record of your personal data maintained by Ner Chat Inc. if it is inaccurate. You may request that Ner Chat Inc. erase that data or cease processing it, subject to certain exceptions. You may also request that Ner Chat Inc. cease using your data for direct marketing purposes. In many countries, you have a right to lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority if you have concerns about how Ner Chat Inc. processes your personal data. When technically feasible, Ner Chat Inc. will—at your request—provide your personal data to you.

Reasonable access to your personal data will be provided at no cost. If access cannot be provided within a reasonable time frame, Ner Chat Inc. will provide you with a date when the information will be provided. If for some reason access is denied, Ner Chat Inc. will provide an explanation as to why access has been denied.

For questions or complaints concerning the processing of your personal data, you can email us at support@nerchat.com. Alternatively, if you are located in the European Union, you can also have recourse to the European Data Protection Supervisor or with your nation’s data protection authority.

For Leaf Users 

Overview
Leaf is a communication application available on mobile devices developed by Ner Chat Inc. This section details the data practices that apply only to Leaf. In the event of any conflict between this section and other parts of our Privacy Policy, the terms in this Leaf-specific section will prevail for Leaf users.

1. Data We Collect Through Leaf

  • Account Information (if applicable): To create an account on Leaf, we may collect minimal information such as username, school email address (if applicable), or a parent/guardian email address.

  • Usage Data: We may collect non-identifying information about how users interact with Leaf (e.g., features used, time spent on different parts of the app) to help us improve the user experience.

  • No Automated Tracking for Ads: Leaf does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies for advertising or promotional targeting. We do not collect browsing behavior or track users across websites for ad purposes when they use Leaf.

2. How We Use This Information

  • To Provide Leaf Services: We use collected information to enable core functionalities, such as saving progress, personalizing learning experiences, and supporting administrative or teacher oversight.

  • App Improvement: We analyze usage data (in aggregated or anonymized form) to troubleshoot technical issues, understand feature usage, and enhance the app.

  • No Commercial/Promotional Targeting: We do not use any personal data gathered through Leaf for targeted advertising, commercial promotions, or selling user information.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Service Providers: We may share limited user information with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) only to help operate or improve Leaf. These providers are contractually prohibited from using Leaf user data for any purpose other than supporting Leaf.

  • Compliance and Safety: We may share information to comply with legal obligations (e.g., responding to lawful requests by public authorities) or protect the rights, property, and safety of Leaf users.

  • No Advertising Partners: We do not share Leaf user information with advertising networks, data brokers, or marketing companies.

4. Data Retention and Security

  • Retention: We retain Leaf user data only as long as necessary to provide the Leaf service or as required by law or school policies.

  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures (e.g., encryption, access controls) to protect Leaf data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Deletion: Parents, guardians, or eligible students may request to review or delete personal information at any time by contacting us using the details below.

  • Email Communications: For school or district-managed accounts, we generally do not send promotional messages to students or educators. However, we may send administrative or service-related emails (like account confirmations or important updates) to ensure proper app functionality, and these communications are typically required for maintaining your Leaf account.

We may send promotional or marketing emails (e.g., announcements of new features, discounts, or newsletters) to individuals who sign up for Leaf independently of a school or district. These messages are optional, and you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in the email or by contacting us directly.
