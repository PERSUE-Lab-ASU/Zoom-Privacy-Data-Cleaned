ABOUT US

The Quizmodeon project started on January 2020 and was released 6 months later on July. After the team's long and hard work we are proud to present a gamechanger service for online quiz experiences.

Now, after being more than 1 year old Quizmodeon is widely used on Education, HR Recruitment, Corporate Training, Product Launch, Annual Meetings, Motivational Events, etc. One of the key elements on its development is the valuable feedbacks coming from brands all around the world.

Memorable Experiences with Quizmodeon

Especially during the pandemic period, when many people were working from home, Quizmodeon became a valuable asset for corporates organizing online meetings.

This platform goes beyond the normal quiz dynamics with special features such as Audio Questions and Sponsor Questions. It offers a memorable experience with its brand-themed interface design by appealing to both public audiences and company employees.

Brands that made a difference by using Quizmodeon

Yıldız Holding, one of the biggest corporates in Turkey, chose Quizmodeon, had a routine quiz test in their online courses for HR Recruitment.

Paribu, on an internal event with its users, rewarded the winner with bitcoin. It was a competitive challenge full of fun for the participants.

Microsoft, Sanofi, ING, Yapı Kredi, Penti, Yıldız Holding, Mercedes, Toyota and many other corporates held different Quizmodeon events with employee participation.

The most requested feature: Quizmodeon Portal

After many feedbacks coming from our clients, we faced a huge demand on a potential Content Management System where the clients can customize the interface using a portal. And here it is now: The Quizmodeon Portal which offers the possiblity for brands and agencies to create their own events, schedule them and customize with a variety of different games and themes.

The Portal makes it possible for you to choose among various game types (Quiz, Poll, Guess What, Tombola and more) and play in 4 different modes (24/7, Live, Team, Tournament). By having a subscription plan you can create and launch events and of course get all the latest updates that included in your plan with no additional cost.

Quizmodeon is being developed and updated constantly and many new features are to be launched soon. In order not to miss them, don't forget to subscribe to our newsletter.

Quizmodeon - Powered by Codemodeon

Founded on March 2013, Codemodeon, one of the leading companies in Turkey for VR/AR solutions now offers a brand new product. By having 8+ years of experience on dealing with corporate events, Codemodeon is now bringing the game-based marketing services to the next level.

Beside adding new features to the existing services, we will continue to develop new products following Quizmodeon and The Tombala.
