Web beacons are small graphics with a unique identifier that may be invisible to you, and which are used to track the online activity of Internet users. Web beacons are embedded in the web pages you review or email messages you receive. Web beacons or similar technologies may be used for a number of purposes, including, without limitation, to count visitors to our Service, to monitor how TeachRN Users navigate the Service, to count how many emails that were sent were actually opened, or to count how many particular articles or links were actually viewed.
