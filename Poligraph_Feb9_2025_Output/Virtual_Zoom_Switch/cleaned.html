Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2024

Trevan and the Zoom Status Light Integration operates the Alexa Skill "Zoom Status Light".

​

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data. We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

​

1. Information Collection And Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data: While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include:

  • Email address

  • First name and last name

  • Amazon User ID

​

2. How personal data is used?

The information collected is only utilized to correlate a users Zoom Account and Amazon Account so that a webhook sent by Zoom will send Alexa Device Change Reports to the appropriate Alexa Account.  Email may also be utilized to send any necessary updates to users who have authorized the Alexa Skill.

​

3. Who can see my information?

Only authorized developers may view customer data for the purpose of troubleshooting.

​

4. Retention

Data at rest is stored on an encrypted Database hosted by AWS DynamoDB and users have the right to request their personal data deleted at any point. If users request their data to be deleted the application will no longer function.

If we make material changes to this Privacy Statement, we will notify you and provide you an opportunity to review before you choose to continue using our products and services.
