5. DATA RETENTION POLICY

Access Logs: Personal data is not stored in access logs. Logs reference user activities by unique IDs, and any sensitive data, such as IP addresses, is truncated to protect user privacy. All logs have a time-to-live (TTL) of 6 months, after which they are automatically deleted. User-Generated Content: Personal data and user-generated content are retained until the user actively chooses to delete it through the platform. This ensures that data is only kept for as long as necessary for its intended purpose. Personal Account and Profile Information: This information is stored until the user specifically requests its deletion. Upon receiving such a request, the data is permanently deleted from the platform.

LaunchPad Central visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using the Website, with the drawback that certain features of the Website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
