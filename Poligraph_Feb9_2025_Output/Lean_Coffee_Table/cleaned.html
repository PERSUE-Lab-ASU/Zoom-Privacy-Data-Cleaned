Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

This Policy explains when and why we collect personal information about people who use Lean Coffee Table, how we keep it secure and how we may use it.

We may change this Policy from time to time so please check this page occasionally to ensure that you’re happy with any changes. By using Lean Coffee Table, you’re agreeing to be bound by this Policy.

Any questions regarding this Policy should be sent to us

Who are we?

We are a small company based in the UK. We primarily work with companies adopting Agile, to whom we provide training, coaching and consulting. We have used our Agile experience to create Lean Coffee Table.

How do we collect information from you?

We collect information when you register to use Lean Coffee Table

What type of information is collected from you?

If you register with us, we will collect your first name, last name and email address.

How is your information used?

We may use your information to:

  • Enable you to login to our application
  • Provide you with a way to reset your password

Data Retention

We only retain data for the purposes of providing a service to you.

We will retain your data until:

  • You delete data accessible to you through the Lean Coffee Table website
  • You ask us to delete your account

We review our retention periods for personal information on a regular basis.

Who has access to your information?

The information in our databases is only accessible by our small team of developers.

We will not sell or rent your information to third parties.

When you are using our secure payment pages, your payment is processed by a third party payment processor, who specialises in the secure online capture and processing of credit/debit card transactions. If you have any questions regarding secure transactions, please contact us.

Your choices

You have a choice about whether or not you wish to receive information from us.

We will not contact you for marketing purposes by email or phone unless you have given your prior consent.

You can change your marketing preferences at any time by contacting us

How you can access and update your information

You have the right to ask for a copy of the information from RippleRock Ltd. Please contact us if you want us to provide this to you.

Security precautions in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of your information

When you give us personal information, we take steps to ensure that it’s treated securely. All information is encrypted and protected with the following software 128 Bit encryption on SSL.

Once we receive your information, we make our best effort to ensure its security on our systems which are hosted in Azure. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential, please do not share your password with anyone.

Use of 'cookies'

Lean Coffee Table does NOT use cookies to authenticate you to the website. We do however make use of Google Analytics which will use cookies..

Where your information is stored

Your information is stored on Azure cloud services within the European Economic Area ("EEA").

If you use our services outside the EEA, your information may be transferred outside the EEA in order to provide you with those services.

Review of this Policy

We keep this Policy under regular review. This Policy was last updated in April 2018.
