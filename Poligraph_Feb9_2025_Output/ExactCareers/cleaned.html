Integrated Google Services

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. Here we describe how our application accesses, uses, stores, and shares Google user data when integrating Google Services, specifically Google Meet and Google Calendar, for scheduling and conducting interviews.

Data Access and Usage

  • We do not collect any passwords or sensitive personal identifying information. We only use the response we receive from Google once you authenticate yourself as a valid Google user. This authentication data is solely used to verify your identity and ensure secure access to our services.
  • We use Google Meet to schedule and conduct interviews. The integration allows us to create and manage Google Meet sessions for interview purposes.
  • We access your Google Calendar to display your schedule inside ExactCareers’ Calendar feature. This ensures efficient scheduling of interviews without conflicts.

Data Storage and Security

  • We do not store any Google user data on our servers. All data, including Google Calendar’s data is accessed and used in real-time for authentication and scheduling purposes only.
  • We do not record Google Meet meetings. Any meetings conducted through Google Meet remain unrecorded and are not stored in any form by ExactCareers.
  • We only store Google Meet meeting’s ID to make a meeting accessible once you want to join it.

Data Sharing

  • We do not share any Google user data with third parties for any purpose, including marketing or analytics. Your data remains private and secure, accessible only for the intended purposes of authentication and interview scheduling.
  • We want to reassure you that our application does not share any Google user data with any external AI models. Moreover, ExactCareers’ proprietary AI models, do not use any Google user data for analytics, prediction or any other purpose whatsoever.

Compliance with Google API Services User Data Policy

At ExactCareers, we are committed to ensuring that the use and transfer of any information received from Google APIs strictly adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements specified by Google.
