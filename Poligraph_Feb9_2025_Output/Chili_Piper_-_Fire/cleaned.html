1. TYPES OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect two basic types of information from you when you provide it to us or when you use or interact with our Services or otherwise engage in Communications with us: personal information and non-personal information.

Personal information includes all information that relates to you or which are opinions about you personally and either identifies or may be used to identify you personally (collectively, “personal information”). We may collect the following types of personal information from you depending upon the device you are using and how you interact with us or use or interact with our Services, such as your:

  • Contact Information. Name, mailing address, email address, phone number, company name, and other contact information.
  • Device Information. IP address, browser type and version, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform, device type and device identifiers.
  • Geolocation Data. This is imprecise geolocation information that permits us to determine your general location.
  • Billing Information. Credit and debit card numbers and other financial information necessary to receive payment for Services purchased, including the number of individuals within the organization that will be using the Services.
  • Account Information. If you create an account, we may store and use your username, password, hints, and other personal information you may provide to authenticate your account. You can modify some of the personal information associated with your account. If you believe that someone has created an unauthorized account, you can request its removal.
  • User Content. This is information such as your Communications with us and any other content you voluntarily provide, including survey responses, comments, reviews, and testimonials.

Non-personal information includes information that does not personally identify you or information that has been anonymized (collectively, “non-personal information”). Non-personal information includes optional information that you may choose to provide us and does not identify any individual, including company annual revenues, number of employees, or industry/sector. When we combine non-personal information with personal information, we treat the combined information as personal information.

You can always refuse to provide personal information, but please note that some personal information is necessary to provide our Services or to otherwise engage in Communications.
