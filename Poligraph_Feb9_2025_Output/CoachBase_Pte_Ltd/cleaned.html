Introduction

This Website is owned and operated by CoachBase Pte Ltd, a company registered in Singapore under ACRA number 202018754G.

CoachBase.io (or also referred to as www.CoachBase.io or “the Website” or "this Website" or "the Platform") is an online directory of executive, business, career, life, relationship, mental health and wellness Coaches (referred to as "Coach(es)" or "Trainee Coach(es)" or "Peer Coach(es)"). Individuals who are seeking Coach or Coaches are also known as "Client(s)" or "Peer Client(s)".

This Data Privacy Policy sets forth the terms and conditions under which we collect and process data and information on the Website and in relation to the services offered.

This Website offers visitors online executive, business, career, life, relationship, health and wellness coaching services.

By accessing or using the Website and the services, you approve that you have read, understood, and agree with our Data Privacy Policy.

Please read the Data Privacy Policy below, even if you have read them before. CoachBase Data Privacy Policy is regularly updated.

The terms "CoachBase" or "we" or "us" or "the Company" or "the Platform" or "the Website" refer to CoachBase Pte Ltd, and the owner of the Website. The term "you" or "Users" or "Visitors" refers to the user or viewer of the Website.

1. What type of information is collected?

We receive, collect, and store any information you enter on the website or provide us in any other way.

We can also collect the Internet Protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet, computer, and connection information. We may use software tools to measure and collect web session information, including page response times, length of visits to certain pages, page interaction information, and methods used to browse away from the page.

We also collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily share with us (including name, email, phone, Skype ID, and communication content); payment details, comments, feedback, testimonials (i.e., either your rating as a client using coaching services and related comments following a coaching session, or your testimonial on your experience with the Website itself - both may include personally identifiable information prior to posting for which you provide permission), and recommendations.

From time to time, we may provide you the opportunity to participate in contests or surveys on our Website. If you participate, we may request certain personally identifiable information from you. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and you therefore have a choice on whether or not to disclose this information. The requested information typically includes contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code). We use this information to notify contest winners and award prizes, to monitor site traffic or personalize the Website.

If you are a Coach who registers with us, we can collect additional information such as your type of services and related pricing, your Skype ID (or other communication medium), your coaching time slot availabilities, links to your professional websites or social media (e.g., LinkedIn) that you provide, your coaching training/education/certification and service details, your picture and bio descriptions, location (city, state, province, time zone), or your publications (e.g., articles, videos, events) that you submit to us. We are also providing Coaches with the ability to voluntarily connect their personal Zoom account through an API, so that their video conference call solution can be proposed to end clients at the occasion of the booking of a coaching session. For that purpose, we collect and store users token, personal meeting URL, Zoom ID, account ID, pmi, phone number, country, and location.

Upon submission of your publications, you provide your consent that the latter can be made visible to public on our website and our/your social media, together with the name of the Coach who has submitted it and a link to his/her profile on the Platform. In order to enable us to manage volumes and storage, the duration of the publication made visible to the public will be at the Platform’s discretion. Submitted publications are stored by the Platform for an unlimited duration on a free and secured cloud database. If Coaches wish to withdraw, amend or delete a publication stored by us, they can do so by contacting us at: info@coachbase.io.

This information is collected for the purpose of being publicly displayed on the Website for prospective coaching clients to see (e.g., via the Coach directory and public profile); we will list you in our publicly accessible Coach directory unless you choose to not offer services to prospecting coaching clients (doable through the “unable/disable” option from your Coach profile; by emailing us at info@coachbase.io or by deleting your account from your profile page).

We also collect your Stripe account details if you choose to offer your services to prospective coaching clients on the Platform.

Apart from your password, credit card and Stripe account information, all of the information that you voluntarily submit to the Website may be publicly displayed.

We also collect information related to the coaching session booking processes, related payments (e.g., service selected, amount paid, credit card for payment processing only) and feedback/queries to ensure smooth coordination and quality of the coaching services, this information may be shared between the Clients and the Coaches.

Lastly, we also collect information related to key performance indicators/statistics to feed the dashboard and benchmarking functionality offered by the Platform (viewable by registered Coaches in an anonymized way only).

We ensure that any transfer of your data outside of Singapore is done in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). We take all necessary steps to safeguard your personal information and maintain its confidentiality.

The Website is hosted on aws.amazon.com. Aws.amazon.com provides us with the online platform that allows us to offer services to you. Your data may be stored through aws.amazon.com’s data storage, databases and the general aws.amazon.com applications. They store your data on secure servers behind a firewall.

For booking and payment processing purposes, when you write us or sign up to receive our newsletter, we will share your email address with our email service provider.

Payments
When you subscribe to a paid plan or service on the Website, we use a credit card processor to store your credit card details in a secure location, and also to charge your credit card when payments are due for payments. We use Stripe.com as a payment gateway. Stripe is one of the leading payment providers in the world. Your payment information may be stored by Stripe and their business partners such as but not limited to Visa, Mastercard, or American Express. Additionally, Stripe collects identifying information about the devices that connect to its services. Stripe uses this information to operate and improve the services it provides to us, including for fraud detection. You can learn more about Stripe and read its privacy policy.

Coaches are provided with the ability to voluntarily connect their video call solution providers (e.g., Zoom) and calendar solution providers (e.g., Google, MS 365, iCloud, Exchange) to synchronize their availabilities and coach booking slots. We use API to connect each of these solutions through the Website.

Publications
Coaches are provided with the ability to voluntarily publish their own publications (e.g., articles, videos, events) on the Website and CoachBase’s social media pages (LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Telegram, YouTube) as well as their own social media pages through CoachBase (button that the Coach can unable/disable upon submitting the publications). By submitting their publications, Coaches give their consent for CoachBase and their third-party digital marketing providers to publish their publications on their behalf, and make them visible to the public.

In order to enable us to manage volumes and storage, and ensure adequacy with the platform brand positioning, the publication and duration of the publication made visible to the public will be at the Platform’s discretion.

Submitted publications are stored by the Platform for an unlimited duration on a free and secured cloud database. If Coaches wish to withdraw, amend, or delete a publication stored by us, they can do so by contacting us at: info@coachbase.io.

Third Party Advertising
Ads appearing on the Website may be delivered to users by CoachBase or one of our Web advertising partners. Our Web advertising partners may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement. In this way, ad servers may compile information about where you, or others who are using your computer, saw their advertisements and determine which ads are clicked on. This information allows our advertising network to deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Data Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by CoachBase only and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers. We have no access or control of these third-party cookies.

Our third-party advertising partners may employ a software technology called clear gifs (a.k.a. Web Beacons/Web Bugs), that help us better manage content on our site by informing us what content is effective. Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Web users. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a user’s computer hard drive, clear gifs are embedded invisibly on Web pages and are about the size of the period at the end of this sentence. We do not tie the information gathered by clear gifs to our customers personally identifiable information.

5. How do we communicate with site visitors?

We may contact you to notify you regarding the services we provide to you and manage related bookings, payments, and queries, to share marketing campaigns that we may find valuable to you, to poll your opinions through surveys or questionnaires, to send updates about the company, or as otherwise necessary to contact you to enforce our Terms of Use, applicable national laws, and any agreement we may have with you. For these purposes we may contact you via email, telephone, and text messages.

We may also send you our newsletter if you opt to receive this communication from CoachBase. If you wish to unsubscribe from promotional emails including newsletters, you may do so by following the unsubscribe instructions included in each promotional email.

Lastly, CoachBase may send you service-related announcements from time to time through the general operation of the service. Generally, you may opt out of such emails which are not promotional in nature by editing your notifications settings, though CoachBase reserves the right to send you notices about your account even if you opt out of all voluntary e-mail notifications.

If you request a consultation as part of the booking process, we will ask for your name, phone, location/time-zone, email address and your comments if any via the Platform to process the booking and payment if any on your behalf. You and your Coach will receive emails with the booking and payment details.

In all cases, CoachBase stores personal information to track the success of our services.

6. How do we use cookies and other tracking tools?

As is true of most websites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data.

We may combine this automatically collected log information with other information we collect about you. We do this to improve services we offer you, to improve marketing, analytics, or site functionality.

The Website may track personal information using software, cookies and tracking tools (e.g., cookies, flash cookies, web beacons, tags, scripts etc.). This information is used by CoachBase and our analytics affiliate, or email partner.

These technologies are used in generating statistics, measuring and collecting session information, including page response times, length of visits to certain pages, page interaction information, and methods used to browse away from the page, analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users’ movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by these companies on an individual as well as aggregated basis.

We use cookies to remember users’ settings (e.g., language preference) and for authentication. Users can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. If you reject cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some features or areas of our site may be limited.

Please note that third-party services, such as Google Analytics or other applications offered through aws.amazon.com, placing cookies or utilizing other tracking technologies through aws.amazon.com's services, may have their own policies regarding how they collect and store information. As these are external services, such practices are not covered by aws.amazon.com's data privacy policy.

Third parties with whom we partner to provide certain features on our site or to display advertising based upon your Web browsing activity use LSOs such as HTML 5 to collect and store information.

Various browsers may offer their own management tools for removing HTML5 LSOs.

We follow generally accepted standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. CoachBase takes appropriate precautions to protect our users’ information. Your account information is located on a secured server behind a firewall. When we collect sensitive information such as your credit card, we use SSL encryption to protect this data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Because email and instant messaging are not recognized as secure communications, we recommend you to be mindful of the private information you share with us by e-mail or instant messaging services.
