Execify.ai is the “data controller” of its Prospects’, Users’, Technology Partner’s and Customers’ personal data, as detailed in Section 1 above. Accordingly, we assume the responsibilities of a data controller (solely to the extent applicable under law), as set forth in this Privacy Policy.
