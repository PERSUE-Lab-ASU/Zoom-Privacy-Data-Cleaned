Privacy Policy

Last Updated:November 30, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Calfrenzy service, which is operated by Softlux, LLC, a company dedicated to respecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. As the proprietor of Calfrenzy.com, Softlux, LLC provides this policy to inform you of our practices concerning the collection, use, and protection of personal information solely for the Calfrenzy.com website. It is tailored to reflect the distinct operations of Calfrenzy and does not extend to any other websites or services owned or managed by Softlux, LLC.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Provided by You

  • We collect your name, email, password hash, timezone, date & time format settings, profile picture, availability (schedule), and event data.
  • You provide us with this information directly during signup or by using our app.

2.2 Information from Third Parties

  • From Google and Microsoft accounts, we access a list of calendars, a list of events for the primary calendar, calendar settings, and your contact information.
  • We also subscribe to your calendars to pull all updates using webhooks, which may occur without your active knowledge each time.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Your data is stored and used to generate available time slots and show contact emails for invitations.
  • We do not create any derivative data or profiles based on your information.

4. Data Storage

  • All user data is securely stored on Google Cloud Platform, protected with AES-256 encryption.
  • While we do not currently have an automatic deletion or anonymization process in place, we are committed to protecting your data for as long as it is under our care.

5. Data Transmission

  • Data transmitted between Calfrenzy and third-party integrations like Google and Microsoft is secured using the HTTPS protocol and TLS.
  • We only communicate with trusted third-party services that adhere to stringent data protection standards.

6. Consent Mechanisms

  • You grant us access to your Google, Microsoft, and other connected accounts through signup or by using our app.
  • You can review, update, or revoke these permissions at any time through your account settings on Google, Microsoft, or directly within our app.

7. Third-party Services and Tools

  • Besides Google and Microsoft, we do not integrate with other third-party services or tools.
  • The event’s data is the only type of information shared with Google and Microsoft.

8. Logs and Backups

  • User data is logged and stored in Google Cloud Logging service, retained for a period of 3 months.
  • Backups are conducted regularly to ensure the integrity and security of your data.

9. Access Controls

  • Access to collected user data is limited to our System Administrator and Lead Developer.
  • Access controls and permissions are managed through Google Cloud Platform, ensuring only authorized personnel can access sensitive data.

10. Data Breach Protocols

  • We employ comprehensive monitoring and testing protocols to prevent and detect potential data breaches.
  • In the unlikely event of a breach, affected users will be promptly notified, and all necessary steps will be taken to secure their data and prevent future incidents.

11. User Requests

  • Users can request to access, modify, or delete their data by contacting our support team.
  • We verify user permissions before processing these requests to ensure data security.

  • Right to Access: You have the right to access your personal information that we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to obtain and reuse your personal data for your own purposes across different services.
