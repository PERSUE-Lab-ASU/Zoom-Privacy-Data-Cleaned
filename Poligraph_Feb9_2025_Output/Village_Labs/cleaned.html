About Atlas

Atlas makes your team radically faster and more productive.

How does Atlas do that? It gives you and your team perfect visibility into individual and team progress, plans, and problems across your company by connecting to all the tools your team uses to get work done – Slack, Google Docs, Linear, Github, HubSpot, etc – and using custom data pipelines and LLMs to synthesize and summarize the underlying content and data into updates and summaries on individual and team work, goals, projects, problems and blockers. 

This includes: 

  • Chief of Staff: AI and Automations that connect to your data to reduce management admin, provide automated summaries of team performance, plans, blockers and redundancies, as well as alerts, reminders and comms to keep you and your team productive.
  • Leadership Insights: real-time key performance metrics and alerts across your organization to better understand things like employee engagement and where resources are being allocated.
  • Directory: an engaging way to keep your team connected through an org directory with personalized profiles. These include bios, a summarized view of what you’re working on, badging, and leaderboards.
