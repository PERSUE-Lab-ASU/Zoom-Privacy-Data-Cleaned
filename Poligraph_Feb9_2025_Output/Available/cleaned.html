Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2023-01-01

Thank you for using availabletimes.com, which allows you to create new calendar events using Google Calendar and Zoom. We take your privacy and data protection seriously, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your information.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use availabletimes.com:

  • Your Google Calendar events and Zoom meeting details: We access your Google Calendar to read and create new events, as well as to read and create Zoom meetings associated with those events.
  • availabletimes.com usage data: We collect information about how you use availabletimes.com, such as which features you use and how often you use them.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve availabletimes.com, as well as to personalize your experience. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Create and manage new calendar events and Zoom meetings on your behalf.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve availabletimes.com's functionality and features.

3. How we share your information

We do not share your information with third parties except as required by law or to provide the services you have requested. Specifically, we may share your information with:

  • Google and Zoom: We use Google Calendar and Zoom to create and manage your calendar events and Zoom meetings. We share your information with Google and Zoom to the extent necessary to provide these services.
  • Law enforcement authorities: We may disclose your information to law enforcement authorities if required by law or if we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others.

availabletimes.com's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Data retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services to you and as required by applicable law. We may also retain your information for as long as necessary to resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, or for other legitimate business purposes.

5. Data security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no security measures are completely foolproof, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Your choices

You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but this may limit your ability to use availabletimes.com's features. You can also modify your Google Calendar and Zoom settings to control the information we access.

7. Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by posting a notice on our website or within availabletimes.com or by other means.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@skycatchfire.com.
