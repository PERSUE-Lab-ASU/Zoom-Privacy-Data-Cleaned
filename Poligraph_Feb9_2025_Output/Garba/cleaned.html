Privacy Policy

Garba ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of users ("you," "your"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Garba, which provides services for recording, transcribing, and summarizing meetings. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Garba collects two types of information: personal information and non-personal information.

a. Personal Information:

When you use Garba, we may collect information that personally identifies you, such as:

  • User Account Information: Name, email address, and login credentials.
  • Meeting Data: Recordings of meetings, transcriptions, summaries, and any associated metadata.
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, IP address, operating system, and browser type.

b. Non-Personal Information:

Non-personal information includes anonymized data or information that does not directly identify you. We may collect:

  • Usage Data: App usage metrics, features accessed, session lengths, and performance data.
  • Aggregated Meeting Insights: Statistical data about meetings that is anonymized.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Garba app.
  • Transcribe and summarize meeting content as requested.
  • Communicate with you regarding account updates or technical support.
  • Conduct analytics to improve user experience and app performance.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers that assist in the app’s operation (e.g., cloud storage, transcription services).
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, in response to legal requests, or to protect the safety and rights of Garba and its users.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Meeting recordings, transcriptions, and summaries are stored until deleted by you or your organization.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request restriction or object to the processing of your data.

Please contact us at info@garba.ai to exercise any of your rights.

7. Children’s Privacy

Garba is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website or app, and the "Effective Date" at the top will be updated.
