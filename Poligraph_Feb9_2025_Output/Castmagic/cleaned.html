This Privacy Policy explains the manner in which Magic Studios LLC ("us," "we," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you access and utilize our Castmagic AI content generation tool. This includes the Castmagic App for the web, the Castmagic website (the "Site"), and all associated tools and services (collectively referred to as the "Castmagic Service").

We may modify this Privacy Policy periodically. Should any changes occur, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of the policy and, in certain cases, we may provide additional notice (such as displaying an announcement on our homepage or sending you an email notification). We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the Castmagic Service to remain informed about our information practices and the methods you can use to help protect your privacy.

‍Collection of Information You Provide to Us.
We gather information that you directly provide to us. Examples include creating an account with Castmagic, completing a form, creating a project, uploading content, requesting support, emailing us, or engaging with us in other ways. The types of information we collect may encompass your name, email address, postal address, password, demographic details, voice, image, and other information you decide to share.

‍Information We Collect Automatically.
When you access or use the Castmagic Service, we may automatically gather information about you, including:
‍
- Log Information: We log details about your usage of the Castmagic Service, such as how you utilize the App, the type of browser you use, browser language, access times, pages viewed, duration of page views, your IP address, and the page you visited before navigating to the Castmagic Service.
‍
- Device Information: We collect data about the computer or mobile device you employ to access the Castmagic Service, including the hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
‍
- Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: We use various technologies to amass information, which may involve sending cookies to your computer or mobile device. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us understand how the Castmagic Service is used and enhance the Castmagic Service and your experience. We may also collect data using web beacons (also referred to as "tracking pixels"). Web beacons are electronic images that may be used in the Castmagic Service or emails and aid in delivering cookies, counting visits, understanding usage and campaign effectiveness, and determining whether an email has been opened and acted upon. For more information about cookies and how to disable them, please see "Your Choices" below.

Security Measures
We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement a variety of administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your data from unauthorized access, destruction, use, modification, or disclosure. Here are some of the key security measures we use:

‍Data Transmission Security: All data transmitted to and from our service is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (TLS). This includes all HTTP requests and other data transmissions, ensuring that your personal information is secure during transit.
‍
‍Secure Cloud Storage: We store all personal data in secure cloud environments that are maintained using state-of-the-art security measures. These environments are regularly audited and updated to ensure high levels of security are maintained.
‍
‍Encryption of Sensitive Information: All sensitive data, including your passwords and application secrets, are encrypted at rest. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to ensure that your data is protected from unauthorized access.
‍
‍Regular Security Assessments: We conduct regular security assessments and penetration testing to identify and address potential security vulnerabilities. Additionally, we work with external security experts to validate the security of our systems.
‍
‍Access Controls: Access to personal data is strictly limited to authorized personnel who need access to perform their job functions. We enforce a role-based access control system, and our staff receive regular training on data protection and security best practices.
‍
‍Incident Response Plan: In the unlikely event of a data breach, we have an incident response plan in place to promptly address and mitigate any potential damage. We will notify affected individuals in accordance with applicable laws.
