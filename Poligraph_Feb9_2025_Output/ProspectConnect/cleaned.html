The Types of Information That Prospect Connect Collects About You and How Prospect Connect Collects Information About You

Prospect Connect may collect two types of Information from you when you visit the Platform: Personal and Non-Personal (collectively "Information").

"Personal Information" refers to data by which you may be personally identified, such as name, email address, employer, job title and department, and telephone number.

"Non-Personal Information" means data about you but does not identify you specifically. While visiting our Platform, do nothing but browse, read pages, or view content. We will gather and store Information about your visit that does not identify you personally.

We collect Information:

Directly from you: when you provide it to us. When you subscribe to a newsletter, please create an account, make a purchase, or request Information from Prospect Connect; we will ask for things like your name, contact, billing, shipping, communication Information, and account ID or credentials to fulfill your request. If you set up an appointment with us through the Platform, attend a trade show or event, or otherwise contact us, you may also voluntarily provide similar Information. Suppose you submit Personal Information about other people to us or our service providers. In that case, you are responsible for ensuring that you have the authority to do so and to allow us to use their Personal Information per this Privacy Policy (for example, by asking for their consent).

From third parties: We obtain Information from partners, vendors, suppliers, and third parties. The parties from whom we obtain Information are typically corporate enterprises (although some may also be educational or public enterprises), and they may be located in any of the locations where we do business. These enterprises broadly fall into the following categories: Advertising and marketing companies, data set and information vendors, public database providers, social media platforms, partners, providers of products or services, hosts or vendors at events or trade shows, research partners, or enterprises that use Prospect Connect Services. We take steps to confirm that the Information we receive from these third parties has been collected with your consent or that these parties are otherwise legally permitted to disclose your Personal Information to us. We might also obtain Information through a partner or co-create datasets with a partner as part of our business operations. This kind of data is used for work like improving the Platform and other Prospect Connect Services, enhancing existing products, and developing new capabilities and features. In some cases, we combine. Personal Information about individuals that we receive from multiple sources, including directly collected from you or through your use of the Platform.

We automatically: collect Information about how you interact with the Platform through cookies, pixel tags, and similar technologies as you navigate through the Platform or utilize our Services. Please view our separate Cookies Policy for more Information on our use of cookies and similar technologies, how you can manage cookies, and how we respond to Do Not Track signals.

How Prospect Connect Uses Personal Information, It Collects About You and the Purposes for the Collection and Use

We use the Personal Information that we collect about you or that you provide to us for the following purposes:

For Functionality and Development of the Platform and Prospect Connect Services: We use Information to provide, offer, and personalize the Platform and other Prospect Connect Services provided to you. Some information, like your IP address, is used to communicate with your device to provide network connectivity, measure usage levels of the Platform, diagnose server problems, and offer security features. Other business purposes that depend on using your Information include data analysis related to testing, modifying, improving, or developing new products, services, and technologies and identifying trends. Some Platform features may leverage artificial intelligence tools to enhance performance capabilities. We do NOT retain Personal Information to develop, improve, or train generalized artificial intelligence or machine learning models, including user data provided via third-party APIs, including but not limited to Google Workspace APIs. We use cookies to make our websites and the Platform operate, work more efficiently, and provide analytic Information. Technologies similar to cookies, such as pixel tags, are also used in connection with the Platform. For more Information on our use of cookies, please read our Cookies Policy

For Customer Support, Platform Updating, and Reporting: The Platform may use Information to provide Prospect Connect with updates and reports and to check that the Platform is working correctly. Update functions may automatically check your system to see whether files need to be refreshed, updated, or modernized to provide you with up-to-date security, versions, features, options, and controls associated with your systems or devices. We rely on Information to analyze performance and improve and maintain the Platform. We also rely on the Personal Information you provide us to provide you with customer support for the Platform and other Prospect Connect Services and verify eligibility for promotional offers.

For Business Operations: We use Information to operate our business, for example, to perform accounting, auditing, billing, reconciliation, and collection activities. Other business purposes that depend on using your Personal Information include crime or fraud monitoring and prevention, protecting our legal rights, and performing contractual obligations. We also use personal Information to contact you to advertise, market, and sell Prospect Connect Services based on your communications preferences.

To Communicate: We use contact information to send messages to provide Prospect Connect Services, respond to customer service requests to provide alerts such as security updates or changes in our policies or about subscriptions that are ending, and send marketing or informational materials like newsletters or white papers, by your communication preferences. We occasionally conduct surveys or do focused research or studies that may require you to share personal Information to participate voluntarily.

These activities typically have additional notices that provide more Information about using your Personal Information, to which you may be asked to consent.

For Advertising and Marketing: We may use personal Information collected from you, along with Information about what advertisements you viewed and other Information we collect, to enable us to provide personalized content and study the effectiveness of advertising and marketing campaigns. By changing your settings, you may choose whether to allow or deny using or sharing your device's location. Still, if you choose to deny such uses or sharing, we may not be able to provide you with certain personalized Prospect Connect Services and content on the Platform.

For Statistical Purposes to Improve the Platform: We may compile Platform statistics into traffic reports, which help Prospect Connect understand, anticipate, and respond to user needs. If we learn, for example, of heightened interest in certain aspects of the Prospect Connect Platform, we are likely to highlight that Information on the Platform home page. This Information helps us create a better overall experience for Platform users.
