INFORMATION XL8 COLLECTS

We, XL8 Inc., collect information to provide better services to all our users.

The information XL8 collects, and how that information is used, depends on how you use our services.

Unregistered user

When you’re not signed in to an account, we store the information collected through our use of tracking and analytics tools. This information includes:

  • Device Information: Details about your device, such as the type, operating system, and browser version.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our services, such as pages visited, time spent on each page, and actions taken (e.g., button clicks).
  • Crash and Performance Data: Information related to technical errors, performance metrics, and crashes to improve service reliability and performance.
  • IP Address and General Location: Your IP address, which may be used to infer general location, such as the country or city level.

When you’re signed in, we also collect information that we store with your account, which we treat as personal information.

Registered user

When you create an account, you provide us with your personal information that includes your name and a password. You can also choose to add a phone number or payment information to your account. Even if you aren’t signed in to an account, you might choose to provide us with information — like an email address to communicate with XL8.

Unless you agree otherwise, we do not collect any content you create, upload, or stream while using our services. We only store the content you request on a temporary storage for the sole purpose of your later reviews, and only for a period not exceeding seven days, unless you configure or request otherwise.

Information we collect as you use our services

‍
1. Your browsers & devices

We may collect information about the environment of you as a user of our services to improve the user experience. This information may include unique identifiers, browser type and settings, device type and settings, operating systems, mobile network information including carrier names and phone numbers, and application version numbers. We may also collect information about the interaction of your apps, browsers, and devices with our services, including IP address, crash reports, system activity, and the date, time, and referrer URL of your request. All this information gets anonymized before collection and cannot be used to identify you as a user.

2. Your activities

We collect information about your activities in our services. The activity information we collect may include:

  • Your activities of using the UI, such as the buttons your click or text you type in, which may be stored in texts, audio, video, or other types of debug information
  • Purchase activities
3. Your location information

We may collect the location information when you use our services, which helps us offer the best user language of the UI.We use various technologies to collect and store information, including cookies, pixel tags, local storage, such as browser web storage or application data caches, databases, and server logs.

WHY XL8 COLLECTS DATA

We use data to build better services. We use the information we collect from all our services for the following purposes:

To provide our services in the best user experience possible.

We use your information to deliver our services.

To maintain our services.

We also use your information to ensure that our services are working as intended, such as tracking outages or troubleshooting issues that you report to us.

To measure the performance of our services.

We use data for analytics and measurement to understand how our services are used. For example, we analyze data about your visits to our sites to do things like optimizing the product design.

To communicate with you.

We may use the information we collect, like your email address, to interact with you directly. For example, we may let you know about upcoming changes or improvements to our services. And if you contact XL8, we’ll keep a record of your request in order to help solve any issues you might be facing.

To protect the services, our users, and the public.

We use information to help improve the safety and reliability of our services. This includes detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud, abuse, security risks, and technical issues that could harm XL8’s services, our users, or the public.

YOUR PRIVACY CONTROLS
‍

Managing, reviewing, and updating your personal information

When you’re signed in, you can always manage, review and update your personal information by visiting the services you use.

Exporting & deleting your content

You may export or delete your content that we store only if you have separately agreed to it. However, since our storage period for your content does not exceed a maximum of seven days unless you have agreed otherwise, your content will be automatically deleted after seven days.

Sharing your personal information

We do not share your personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of XL8 and its affiliates except in the following cases:

1. With your consent

We’ll share personal information outside of XL8 when we have your consent. We’ll ask for your explicit consent to share any sensitive personal information.

2. For external processing

We may provide information to other trusted businesses or persons to process it for the services, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

3. For legal reasons

We will share personal information outside of XL8 if we have a good-faith belief that disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to:

  • Respond to any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
  • Enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations.
  • Detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security, or technical issues.
  • Protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of XL8, our users, or the public.

We may share non-personally identifiable information publicly and with our partners — like publishers, advertisers, developers, or rights holders.

‍

KEEPING YOUR INFORMATION SECURE

All XL8 products are built with security features that continuously protect your personal information and content. And if we do detect something risky that we think you should know about, we’ll notify you and help guide you through steps to stay better protected.

We work hard to protect you and XL8 from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold.

‍

DELETING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

When the purpose of collecting and using personal information is completed, we delete the collected personal information in a quick and safe way.

If the user has obtained separate consent for the storage period or the law sets the storage period, it is safely stored during that period and deleted after that period has elapsed.

COMPLIANCE & COOPERATION WITH REGULATORS

We regularly review this Privacy Policy and make sure that we process your information in ways that comply with it.

We maintain servers around the world and your personal information may be processed on servers located outside of the country where you live. Data protection laws vary among countries, with some providing more protection than others. Regardless of where your information is processed, we apply the same protections described in this policy. We also comply with certain legal frameworks relating to the transfer of data. When we receive formal written complaints, we respond by contacting the person who made the complaint. We work with the appropriate regulatory authorities, including local data protection authorities, to resolve any complaints regarding the transfer of your data that we cannot resolve with you directly.
