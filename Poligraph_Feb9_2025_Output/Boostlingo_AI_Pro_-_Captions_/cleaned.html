INTRODUCTION

Boostlingo, LLC, on behalf of itself and its wholly owned subsidiaries (collectively referred to herein as, “Boostlingo”), the Boostlingo platform, “we” or “us” and the associated Boostlingo websites, applications and services were formed to create the world’s first truly unified interpretation management system and interpretation communication and delivery platform. The privacy of all our partners, customers, end-users and stakeholders – your privacy – is something we take extremely seriously.  This Privacy Policy details how we treat the personal information that we collect, what settings we provide for you to control how your information is used on Boostlingo, and how you can contact us with any questions or concerns. This Privacy Policy is part of, and incorporated into, our Terms of Service.

This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the website, Boostlingo.com or any of our affiliated websites under the Boostlingo brand (collectively referred to as the“Website”) and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information. It also applies to all personal information we collect as part of our provision of services whether or not as part of the Website (together, “Services”) including, for example: (a) in email, text, and other electronic messages between you and Boostlingo; (b) from our mobile and desktop applications you download and that reference this Privacy Policy; (c) when you interact with our advertising and applications on third-party websites and services, if those applications or advertising include links to this Privacy Policy; and (d) through any other sources associated with Boostlingo. Except as specifically provided below, it does not apply to employee information.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. By accessing or using the Services, you expressly agree to this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy may change from time to time. Your continued use of the Services after we make changes is deemed to be acceptance of those changes, so please check the Privacy Policy periodically for updates.

We will only share the minimum information necessary to comply with such legal requirements.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT THROUGH AUTOMATIC DATA COLLECTION TECHNOLOGIES

As you navigate through and interact with our Website, we may use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns, including: (a) details of your visits to our Website, including traffic data, location data, logs, and other communication data and the resources that you access and use on the Website; and (b) information about your computer and internet connection, including your IP address, operating system, and browser type.

The information we collect automatically may include your IP address, device ID and other identifiers that may be considered personal information, as well as statistical data and other data that is not personal information, but that may be associated with personal information we collect or receive from third parties. All information that we associate with personal information is treated by us as personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include cookies, pixels, web beacons and other tracking technologies. Usage data is information about how you use our Services, such as our Website, and may be collected by us or our third-party providers automatically through “cookies,” log files and other tracking tools and technology embedded within our Services. This information may be collected over time and across devices. We use this information to administer and improve our Services.

A “cookie” is a piece of data stored on the user’s device containing information about the user. If a user rejects or deletes certain of the cookies, use of our Website may be adversely impacted, and functionality may be unavailable or limited in some areas of our Website. Cookies also enable us to track and aggregate data about the way you navigate our Services in some cases. By doing this, we can better target the interests of our users and enhance their experience on our Services.

We use cookies and other web-based data collection technologies to:

  • Help you navigate our Website and apps;
  • Personalize and provide a more convenient experience to you;
  • Analyze which pages you visit, and which features you use;
  • Determine pages you visit prior to and after visiting our Website to help us customize and target our advertising;
  • Provide features such as social sharing widgets and videos;
  • Market to you and measure advertising and promotional effectiveness;
  • Assess which areas of our Website to remarket to you; and
  • Provide content to you from our third-party partners.

When you visit our Website, we automatically gather server log file information including but not limited to IP address, browser type, referring and exit web pages, and your operating system. We do this to better understand how our Website operates, administer our Website and our technical solutions, and understand how our Website visitors navigate through our Website. We do not, however, link this information with customer accounts. For example, opting out of receiving cookies will not opt you out of receiving other marketing based on your account information.

We may further analyze information we gather online from you to improve the online experience, resources, and tools we provide to our users.

Boostlingo, our service providers or other third parties may use web logs or pixel tags, and/or other tracking technologies (sometimes referred to as “smart cookies”) on your computer or other device. Smart cookies are images embedded in a webpage or e-mail for the purpose of measuring and analyzing usage and activity. Our Website and service providers acting on our behalf, may use smart cookies to help us analyze usage and improve our functionality. Some cookies and other technologies may serve to recall personal information previously provided by a user.

Boostlingo may acknowledge and/or respond to “do not track” beacons, tags or other such signals including browser-based prompts or controls in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations. Global Privacy Control (“GPC”) is an add-on or browser preference that signals that you do not want personal information collected. Our Website will honor GPC signals. You also may receive tracking mechanisms from other parties prior to visiting our Website that will continue to track your activity while on our Website. Such data would be subject to the privacy policy of the other provider from whom you received the mechanism. We do not control or receive data from such mechanisms.

Some Boostlingo advertising at non-Boostlingo sites may be serviced by our service providers, who may use cookies to collect information about your visits to those sites. Information collected may include the pages visited, date and time, browser information and/or IP address. This information is gathered to help us customize and target our advertising to the needs and interests of our customers.

Information about how to opt-out of our interest-based or targeted advertising can be found in the section below titled “Your Rights and Choices about How We Use and Disclose Your Information.” For more information on how to manage advertising controls including tracking mechanisms, please visit FTC Online Tracking.

With the exception of cookies that are required for our Website to operate, you can choose which types of cookies our Website sets on your device by interacting with a cookie consent banner available on our sites or by clicking on Cookie Settings. Below are the categories of cookies we use:

  • Required Cookies: These cookies provide service, application, and other essential functionalities of our Website.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies are used to provide increased functionality of our Website.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our Website.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies may be set through our Website by our advertising partners to provide you relevant advertisements on other sites.

See more about automatic data collection, cookies and opting out in our Cookies Policy.

Rights Regarding Your Personal Information

Additionally, some data protection regulations confer certain data protection rights on individuals. We provide these options to you regardless of your location:

  • The Right to Know About Personal Information Collected, Disclosed, or Sold. You have the ability to request that Boostlingo disclose what personal information it collects, uses, discloses, and sells. This is called the “Right to Know”.
  • The Right to Access and Receive your Specific Personal Information. You have the right to request that Boostlingo provide a portable copy of your personal information it collects, uses, discloses, and sells.
  • The Right to Correct Personal Information Collected or Disclosed. You have the right to request that Boostlingo correct the personal information it collects, uses, discloses, and sells. This is called the “Right to Correct”. Under the Right to Correct, you can request a correction of any inaccurate personal information, and Boostlingo will use commercially reasonable efforts to correct this information if inaccurate.
  • The Right to Delete Personal Information about You. You have a right to request the deletion of your personal information collected or maintained by us.
  • The Right to Opt-Out of the Sale or Cross-Context Behavioral Advertising of Personal Information. You have the right to opt-out of the sale and/or cross-context behavioral advertising or certain “profiling” uses of your personal information by Boostlingo. PLEASE NOTE THAT WE DO NOT SELL ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION.
  • The Right to Restrict or Object to the Processing of Your Personal Information. You have the right to object to or restrict at any time the processing of your personal information.
  • The Right to Not Be Subject to Automated Decision Making or Profiling. You have the right to object to automated decision-making where the decisions impact a legal or similar right or profiling from your personal information.
  • The Right to Limit the Use or Disclosure of Sensitive Personal Information. You have the right to limit the use or disclosure of sensitive personal information by Boostlingo.
  • The Right to Appeal a Business or Controller’s Refusal to Take Action. You have the right to appeal your privacy rights-related request where such request is denied in whole or in part.
  • The Right to Non-Discrimination for the Exercise of a Consumer’s Privacy Rights. You have the right not to receive discriminatory treatment for exercising any of your privacy rights conferred by consumer privacy laws in your jurisdiction. Boostlingo will not discriminate against any consumer because such person exercised any of the consumer’s rights under these privacy laws, including, but not limited to:
    • denying goods or services;
    • charging different prices or rates for goods and services, including through the use of discounts or other benefits or imposing penalties;
    • providing a different level or quality of goods or services; and
    • suggesting that the consumer will receive a different price or rate for goods or services or a different level or quality of goods or services.

We may, however, charge a different price or rate, or provide a different level or quality of goods or services, if that difference is related to the value provided by your data.

Authorized Agents. You may designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf. We will deny requests from agents that do not submit proof of authorization from you. To verify that an authorized agent has authority to act for you, we may require a copy of a power of attorney or require that you provide the authorized agent with written permission and verify your own identity with us.

EXERCISING YOUR RIGHTS

If you wish to exercise any of the rights, you may visit our Support page. You may also be able to exercise certain rights, such as updating and correcting your personal information, through your account. If you make a request to exercise one of your privacy rights via e-mail, please include “Privacy Rights Request” in the subject line of the email. When you make a request, you can expect the following:

  • We will verify your identity. Making a verifiable request does not require you to create any account with us but may require you to provide some information to enable us to carry out such verification. We cannot respond to your request or provide you with personal information if we cannot verify your identity or authority to make the request and confirm the personal information relates to you. We will only use personal information provided in a verifiable consumer request to verify the requestor’s identity or authority to make the request.
  • We will confirm our receipt of your request within ten (10) business days. If you have not received a response within ten (10) business days after that, please send us an email at [email protected].
  • We will respond to your request within the legally required time period. We may need additional time to respond where allowed by law, but we will reply either way within the legally required period and, if we need additional time to respond to your request, we will explain why.
  • In certain cases, a request may be denied. For example, if we cannot verify your identity or if providing you the information could create an unreasonable risk to someone’s security (for example, we do not want sensitive information disclosed inappropriately or improperly). As another example, we will also deny a request to delete your personal information if the law requires us to keep it (for example, if we are required to keep a record of services you have purchased in order to comply with tax reporting requirements). If we deny your request, we will explain the reasons for the denial and will comply with as much of your request as possible.

Any payment transactions and other sensitive information will be encrypted using secure socket layer (SSL) technology. Boostlingo follows generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once Boostlingo receives it.

The safety and security of your information also depends on you. Where we have given you (or where you have chosen) a password for access to certain parts of our Website, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential. We ask you not to share your password with anyone.

Unfortunately, the transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure. Although we do our best to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information transmitted to our Website. Any transmission of personal information is at your own risk. While we implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security for information transmitted over the internet. Users should exercise caution when transmitting personal information online.
