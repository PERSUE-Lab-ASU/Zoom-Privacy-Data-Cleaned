Privacy Policy

YAGO, inc. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") recognizes that the protection of customers' personal information is an important responsibility of the Company, and complies with the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (hereinafter referred to as the "Personal Information Protection Act") . We will follow below rules (hereinafter referred to as "this Privacy Policy") and we will strive to handle and protect customer information appropriately.

1 Collection of Personal Information

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, customer information is information about a living individual that can be used to identify a specific individual by name, date of birth, address, telephone number, or other description contained in the information (including information that can be easily cross-checked with other information to identify a specific individual).

When using the Company's web services "YAGO", "RECAST", "classmall" and their associated services (hereinafter collectively referred to as "the Services"), the Company shall not be liable for any loss or damage arising from the use of the Services. In the use of these services, we may issue IDs and passwords for member registration and collect your personal information in order to provide the services. The collection of personal information by the Company is based on the voluntary provision by the customer, and when the customer provides personal information, it is assumed that the customer has agreed to the Company using the personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2 Appropriate acquisition

The Company shall acquire personal information in an appropriate manner and shall not acquire such information through deception or other wrongful means.

3 Purpose of use

The Company will use personal information for the purposes notified to customers at the time of acquisition and collection of personal information or for the following purposes. We will not use your personal information for any purpose other than those stated above, except in the cases described in "6. Provision to third parties" below or when we have received your prior consent.

  1. To register users for this service and to provide this service
  2. To confirm the use of this service
  3. For billing and payment of the Service
  4. To provide information about the Service
  5. To resolve problems in the operation of the Service
  6. To notify you of changes to the Terms of Service
  7. To improve the Service for the betterment of the Service and to help develop new services for the Company
  8. To send emails and to notify users of the suspension of e-mail delivery
  9. To send information on services for members and other various services
  10. To send rewards for surveys cooperated with
  11. To apply for employment
  12. To respond to consultation from customers
  13. To respond to inquiries from customers
  14. To aggregate personal information statistically processed and publish it as survey results
  15. To contact the customer for any other reason

4 Change of the purpose of use

The Company may change the purpose of use of customer information to the extent that it is reasonably recognized as having considerable relevance, and in the event of such change, the Company will notify the customer or make a public announcement.

5 Safety control measures

The Company shall exercise necessary and appropriate supervision over its employees to ensure that personal information is managed securely against risks such as loss, destruction, falsification, and leakage.
To ensure the security of personal information when it is transmitted, we use SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption. Credit card information is sent directly to the settlement agent without passing through our server, and credit card information is stored under the strict security control of the settlement agent.

6 Provision to third parties

The Company will not provide customer information to any third party without prior consent of the customer, except in the following cases

  • Disclosure or sharing of information on the Service when the Company deems it necessary in order to provide the Service
  • Personal information, including the name and e-mail address of users who have transacted on the Service will be disclosed to the Service Owner for the purpose of the transaction. The information of users who made transactions on this service will be disclosed only to the service owner who used the service, and will not be disclosed to any other owner or third party without the user's consent
  • When necessary to provide this service
  • When the Company deems it necessary for the operation of the Service
  • When entrusted or provided to a subcontractor
  • In order to process personal information and provide information to our customers, we may outsource all or part of our business to companies that we judge to be trustworthy and have signed a confidentiality agreement for personal information. In these cases, we may provide personal information to the subcontractor to the minimum extent necessary to conduct the business under our strict control
  • Partnerships with our cooperating companies and partners
  • In order to provide the Service, the Company may enter into a cooperative or alliance relationship with a company that the Company judges to be trustworthy and with which the Company has concluded a confidentiality agreement for personal information. In this case, the Company may provide the relevant cooperative or alliance company with personal information to the minimum extent necessary to provide the Service under the strict control of the Company
  • Provision of personal information to sellers in the event of business transfer
  • As a company, we may merge or sell our business. In such cases, we may provide personal information to the third party to whom the business is sold on the condition that the third party complies with the corporation's rules for handling personal information
  • When it is necessary to protect the life, body, or property of an individual and it is difficult to obtain the consent of the individual
  • When the provision of personal information is particularly necessary for improving public health or promoting the sound growth of children, and it is difficult to obtain the consent of the individual concerned
  • When it is necessary to cooperate with a national agency, a local government, or an entity entrusted by either of the foregoing in executing the affairs prescribed by laws and regulations, and obtaining the consent of the individual is likely to impede the execution of the affairs concerned

7 Disclosure, correction, and deletion of personal information

In the event that a registrant requests the disclosure, correction, or deletion of his/her personal information, the Company will respond appropriately in accordance with the relevant laws and regulations.

8 Handling of Cookies

The Company's services may use cookies and similar technologies. These technologies help the Company to understand the usage of the Company's services and contribute to the improvement of the Company's services. However, if you set your browser not to accept cookies, the functions available on the Service may be limited.

9 Contact for inquiries

We will respond promptly to requests from customers to disclose, correct, or delete their own personal information, or to make other requests. Please contact the following.

10 Continuous Improvement

The Company will review the operation of the handling of personal information from time to time and strive for continuous improvement, and may change this Privacy Policy as necessary.
