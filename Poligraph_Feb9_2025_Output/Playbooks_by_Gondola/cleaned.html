Gondola offers a next generation online travel agent that books travel for you the optimal way for your preferences and loyalty. This Privacy Policy describes Gondola's privacy practices and how we handle personal information that we collect through our website, our applications, and our other interactions and correspondence with you.

Before we describe the categories of personal information that we collect, use, and disclose, below we provide a short summary of how Gondola works and how Gondola integrates with and handles your data.

How Gondola Works

Gondola creates a travel profile for you consisting of your past/future travel and a loyalty dashboard that is derived from your email and credit card data. To learn about your travel history, we will request permission to connect to your Gmail account to sync hotels that you've stayed in, flights you've taken, how many loyalty points you have, and other travel-related emails in your inbox. Using AI tools, we build your loyalty dashboard based on your travel-related information. If we need more details, we will ask for your permission to contact travel partners on your behalf to learn more about your loyalty status and rewards. Using the information we have collected, we will help you find the best hotel and airline rates and book trips on your behalf.

How We Collect, Use, and Disclose Information through the Gmail Integration

With users' explicit permission, we use the Gmail API to access travel-related emails in your inbox. We use this information to build a travel history and populate your loyalty dashboard. We collect and use this information in accordance with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Notwithstanding anything else in this Privacy Policy, our use of the data we collect through the Gmail API is subject to the following restrictions:

  • We will never access emails that are not travel related. Travel related emails are those from airlines, hotels / vacation rentals, rental car agencies, online travel agencies, and credit card companies.
  • We will only use access to the email inbox to read message bodies (including attachments), metadata, headers, and settings, and then structure this information into your travel profile. We will never save or store your raw emails on our servers.
  • We will not transfer this structured email data to others unless doing so is necessary to provide and improve these features, comply with applicable law, or as part of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
  • We will not use this Gmail data for serving advertisements.
  • We will not allow humans to read this data unless (1) we have your affirmative agreement to read specific messages, (2) doing so is necessary for purposes such as investigating a bug or abuse, (3) doing so is necessary to comply with applicable law, or (4) the data has been aggregated and anonymized for use in Gondola's internal operations.
