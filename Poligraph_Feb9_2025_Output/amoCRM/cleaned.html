    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file: This scope is needed in order to give our customers the ability to create a template in Google Docs, and then use our bot to fill it in. It also allows the automatic creation and update of leads by syncing a Google Sheet with Kommo as well as exporting leads to Google Sheets
