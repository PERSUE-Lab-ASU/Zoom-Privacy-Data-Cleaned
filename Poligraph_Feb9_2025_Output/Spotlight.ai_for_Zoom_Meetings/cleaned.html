Privacy Policy

General

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail and the website, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by the consumer (such as survey information and/or site registrations). The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web pages and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

​

  • It is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, or as otherwise required by law.

  • We transfer information about you if Spotlight.ai is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Spotlight.ai will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

​

Information Gathering and Usage

When you register for an account with Spotlight.ai, we ask for information such as your name, email address, and phone
Spotlight.ai uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provisioning, identification and authentication, service improvement, contact, and research.

​

Data Sharing with Third-Party Tools
Our app shares certain user data with third-party AI models to enhance our services. The specific data shared includes:

  • Meeting transcripts

  • Meeting participants

​Google Workspace APIs are not used to develop, improve, or train generalized/non-personalized AI and/or ML models.
 

​

User Consent for Data Sharing
Before any data is shared with third-party AI models, we obtain explicit user consent. This consent is collected during the first login to the Spotlight.ai service.  Users have the option to opt out of data sharing at any time by sending email request to support@spotlight.ai.
Please note that the consent process for data sharing is independent of the Google OAuth consent process.

​

Data Retention 

We store your personal information for a period of time that is consistent with our business purposes.
We will retain your personal information for the length of time needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When the data retention period expires for a given type of data, we will delete or destroy it.
You may request for your data to be deleted by sending an email to support@spotlight.ai or submitting a request through SUPPORT.  

Data Protection Policy 
We use security measures to help protect your personal information. Security procedures are in place to protect the confidentiality of your data. We use encryption to protect your information.

​

Cookies

  • A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

  • Cookies are required to use the Spotlight.ai website and app.

  • We use cookies to record current session information but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to re-login to your Spotlight.ai account after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.
