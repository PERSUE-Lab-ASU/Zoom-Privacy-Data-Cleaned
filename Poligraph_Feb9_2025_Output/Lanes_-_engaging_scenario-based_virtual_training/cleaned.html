We collect and process your personal data only with your willing consent. With your permission, we may collect and process the following data: first name and last name, e-mail address, company, and job title. This data helps us improve training programs, understand participant engagement, and enhance overall training effectiveness. The content is used solely for these purposes and is not shared with third parties unless explicitly stated and consented to by the participants.

Cookies processing

We use cookies for the correct display of the website’s content and for the convenience of browsing www.lanes.ai. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help the website remember information about you, such as the language you use and the pages you have already opened. This information will be useful on your next visit. Thanks to cookies, browsing the website becomes much more convenient. You can set up cookies reception and blocking in your browser. The inability to receive cookies may limit the functionality of the website.

Users who have provided www.lanes.ai with their personal data have the right to alter and remove it, as well as the right to recall their agreement to data processing. Your personal data will be stored for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. The retention period is determined based on the nature of the data and the purpose for which it was collected. Once the processing of your personal data is complete, the website’s administration will permanently delete it. To access your personal data, you can contact the administration at privacy@in.lanes.ai. We will pass your data to a third party only with your willing consent.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These include secure servers, encrypted data transfer, and regular security audits.
While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will promptly notify affected users via email and/or a prominent notice on our website. We will also take necessary steps to mitigate the breach and prevent future occurrences.

Records of your time of visit, browser settings, operating system, and other technical information are saved in the database when you visit www.lanes.ai. This data is necessary for the correct display of the website’s content. It is impossible to identify the person of the visitor using this data.
