Privacy Policy

Condens is a web-based software tool developed by Condens Insights GmbH. Its purpose is to store, analyze and share user research data.

TLDR:

  • You have full control over your data. We don’t own it and will never sell it to others.
  • Condens is fully GDPR compliant. All data you enter or upload into Condens is stored and processed in data centers in the EU by trusted and certified infrastructure subprocessors .
  • Condens doesn’t need any personal data to function (except for your billing and signup information). You are free to use your participants’ real data or anonymize it in any way you want.
  • We improve the Condens webapp based on your feedback and usage. Therefore we monitor how you interact with it.
  • On our website, we use cookies to better understand how we can improve our offering and marketing efforts.
