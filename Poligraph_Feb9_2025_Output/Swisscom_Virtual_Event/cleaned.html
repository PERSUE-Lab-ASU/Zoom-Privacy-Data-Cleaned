Events & Webinars

By registering for an event or a Swisscom business customers webinar, you give your consent for Swisscom to also display, edit, analyse and use your information internally for any subsequent processing. The data you enter when registering for events will be made available for a maximum period of 2 years, particularly to the Swisscom customer advisers responsible for your company. We also reserve the right to check all registrations and revoke them in justified cases (e.g. registrations by employees of competitors or other people who, in our sole discretion, are not part of the event’s target audience). When registering for a webinar, please note that the login information provided in the form will be forwarded to LogMeIn, Inc. and used to conduct the webinar. LogMeIn’s separate privacy policy shall apply.

We need your consent

We use cookies and similar technologies on the websites of the Swisscom Group (including swisscom.ch or blueplus.ch and bluecinema.ch) in order to enable you to use the websites properly. Click on “Accept All” if you consent to the processing of your data for statistical and analytical purposes, for the personalisation of our website and for the personalisation of our advertising on third-party websites, as well as to recognise visitors as our customers.

If you do not agree to this processing, click on “Settings” and save your own cookie preferences. For further information, please refer to ourCookie Policy

We need your consent

In order to guarantee proper basic functionality and thus proper operation of our websites (swisscom.ch or blueplus.ch and bluecinema.ch), we store strictly necessary cookies. The data stored in these cookies is only used by us and does not reach third parties. Irrespective of this, you have the option of making individual settings in the following areas of application

Manage Consent Preferences

Statistics and analysis

With this setting, you allow us to check the number of visitors to our websites and to analyse how visitors use our websites. You also help us to ensure that the website runs stably, reliably and quickly and can be constantly optimised.

Personalisation of our website

With this setting, you allow us to optimise and personalise your visit to our website for you, e.g. by saving your username or your language selection. In addition, it is possible for us to store your usage behaviour on our websites and to derive information about your interests from it. We show you offers tailored to you and can address you personally on the websites. Your customer data will only be used within the framework of your existing settings for data use in My Swisscom.

Swisscom advertising with third parties

With this setting, you allow us to use services of third-party providers such as Google or Facebook to show you our advertising and offers on their websites. This relates to offers that are sold by directly Swisscom or via our third-party brands such as Wingo, M-Budget or Coop Mobile. For this purpose, we transmit data about your usage behaviour on our webistes to the providers. Depending on your existing settings for data use in My Swisscom, other customer data may also be transferred. We also measure how well our advertising works on the websites of the third-party providers.

Visitor identification

If you allow us, we can identify you as a Swisscom customer using your IP address, even if you are not logged into My Swisscom. This means you see offers that are suitable for you and that correspond to your usage behaviour across all devices.
