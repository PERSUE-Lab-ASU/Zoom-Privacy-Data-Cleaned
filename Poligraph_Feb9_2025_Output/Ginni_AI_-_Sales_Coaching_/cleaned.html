Cookies and Online Tracking

At Ginni AI, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience with our services, perform analytics, and personalize content and advertising.

What are Cookies? Cookies are small data files stored on your device by a website. They are used to remember your preferences, login information, and browsing history. We utilize both session cookies, which expire when you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a set period or until deleted.

Types of Cookies Used

  1. Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic functioning of our website and services.

  2. Performance and Analytics Cookies: Collect anonymous data on how users interact with our website, helping us improve user experience.

  3. Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings, enhancing your experience on our platform.

  4. Advertising Cookies: Used to display relevant advertisements and gauge their effectiveness.

Third-Party Cookies We may also use third-party service providers for analytics and advertising purposes. These cookies are subject to the privacy policies of these third parties.

Managing Cookies You have the option to control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Blocking or deleting cookies may impact your user experience and some features of our service. Most browsers offer instructions on how to reset the browser to reject cookies in their "Help" or "Settings" sections.

Data Subject Rights

We recognize and respect your rights regarding your personal data. This section outlines the rights you hold and the process for exercising them depending on your jurisdiction and applicable laws, including the EU or UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), or the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA).Your rights may include:

  1. Right to Access: You can ask for confirmation on whether we are processing your personal data, request a copy of the data collected, and obtain information on how it is used.

  2. Right to Rectification: If you find that your personal data with us is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to have it corrected.

  3. Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data, especially where the data is no longer necessary for the purposes it was collected for or if you withdraw your consent.

  4. Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit the way we use your personal data.

  5. Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive the personal data you provided to us in a structured, commonly used format, and to transfer that data to another controller.

  6. Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your personal data, particularly for direct marketing purposes.

  7. Right to Withdraw Consent: Where our processing is based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw this consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

  8. Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority if you have concerns about how we are handling your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@ginni.ai. When you make a request, we may need specific information from you to confirm your identity and ensure the right to access the data (or to exercise any of your other rights). This is a security measure to ensure that personal data is not disclosed to any person who has no right to receive it.
