Privacy Policy

This is the Privacy Policy for the learning and mindfulness platform mindseed that enables our customers (“Customers”) to create, share and provide mindfulness-based learning experiences to their end users (“End Users”). mindseed is a service of Kudima GmbH, c/o Startplatz Köln GmbH, Im Mediapark 5, D-50670 Köln Germany  (“Kudima”, "we", "us").

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy informs you about the processing of personal data. The privacy policy applies to our platform and the services mentioned hereunder. Specific or supplementary privacy policies may apply to certain services.

We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy in accordance with the applicable laws. We will give you notice of such changes by posting the revised Privacy Policy on this website or by other means.

1. Introduction & general information

The controller for the processing of personal data in connection with the offering of the platform services is Kudima. However, where a Customer uses our platform to provide its services to its End Users, such Customer is the controller for the processing of personal data of its End Users in connection with the Customer’s offers and Kudima will be only the data processor for the Customer.

You can find more information and contact details of Kudima in our legal notice https://mindseed.online/en/legal-notice

We process personal data on the basis of your consent, for the conclusion or performance of a contract or to the extent necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by us or by a third party (Art. 6 (1) (a), (b) and (f) DSGVO). In particular, we process personal data in order to provide our services to you, to offer your assistance in using our services, to answer your enquiries, to adjust and improve our products and services and to support our marketing. For further details please see the following.

As a Customer or End User you may access general information provided on our platform without identifying yourself or registering. However, when using our platform, you transmit data to us via your browser that is technically necessary to establish the connection and use our website. This includes, in particular, the date and time of the connection, your IP address, requested web pages and downloaded files, data on the transmission and use of the website, information on the browser and browser settings, operating system and device and the internet address from which you accessed our site. The data is stored and processed for technical reasons. We use this information to ensure the security of our web pages, to measure the effectiveness of internet advertising, to compile statistics and to measure the activities on the website with the aim of improving our offer.

If you would like to use our platform and services as a Customer or End User you will need to register. In such case we will collect certain information, including personal data, as further set out below.

When we ask you to provide us with certain personal data, we indicate whether the information is necessary to provide you with the relevant service. Required information is marked with an asterisk (*) or otherwise. All other information you provide to us is voluntary.

If you choose not to provide us with personal data when using services on our website, your decision will not have any material adverse consequences for you. However, you may then not be able to receive certain information via the website, you may not be able to use personalised services or register, or we may not be able to respond to enquiries fully or at all.

To the extent the processing of your personal data is based on your consent, you can withdraw your consent for the future at any time. You can use the contact details below for this purpose. We will promptly process any withdrawal notice.

Newsletter registration

We might offer newsletters in general or on specific topics, to which you might subscribe. If you would like to subscribe to a newsletter, we need your email address or other contact information as applicable. After registering for a newsletter, you will receive an email so that you can confirm your registration. By confirming, you allow us to send you regular information about our products and services in the form of a newsletter.

When we send a newsletter, we collect data about the sending and use of the newsletter. For this purpose, newsletters contain links to image files stored on our web servers. Consent to advertising and customer satisfaction surveys

Depending on the circumstances, we process information about your consent (date and time), about the services you use, the sending of the advertising or the customer satisfaction survey (in particular the content of our messages, date and time), information about the delivery and opening of the email, the data for establishing the connection between the website and your browser and device, and the information you provide in connection with the customer satisfaction survey. 4. Cookies, analysis tools, tracking and targeting, social media

General Information

We use cookies on our website. Cookies are small files that are temporarily stored on your device. We use cookies for various purposes, which are described below.

We use our own cookies, in particular functional cookies, to make our website more user-friendly, to simplify registration and logging into the platform, to provide certain functions, to store the consent to or rejection of cookies and to ensure the security of our website and our systems. The corresponding cookies are necessary in order to be able to use our website functionally. Consent is not required for such necessary cookies.

We might also use third-party cookies, particularly in connection with the use of analytics tools, tracking and targeting technologies.

Measurement and analysis tools are used to better understand the use of our website and to optimise it accordingly. With the corresponding cookies, we might collect information about how you interact with our platform, and which pages you have visited. The information we collect or store with these cookies is aggregated and not linked to you as an individual. Such cookies are activated with your consent, and you can withdraw your consent at any time.

Tracking and targeting technologies might be used in order to optimise the delivery of advertising and other marketing on the internet. These cookies track and collect information about your online activities to help advertisers deliver more relevant advertising to you according to your interests. These cookies may share this information with other organisations or advertisers. Such cookies are activated with your consent, and you can withdraw your consent at any time.

We also might integrate certain so-called social media offers or external content on our websites. When using social media, you also use the respective offers of the social media providers, which also use cookies. Such cookies are activated with your consent, and you can withdraw your consent at any time.

In principle, you can also view our website without cookies. However, most browsers automatically accept cookies as the default setting. But you can prevent or restrict the storage of cookies on your end device. Please refer to the instructions of your browser manufacturer for details of how this works. You can delete cookies that have already been set at any time via your browser. You can use the "Do Not Track" settings of your browser to manage cookies. You can also deactivate cookies from certain third-party providers on the page of the "Digital Advertising Alliance" at http://www.aboutads.info/choices/ or on the page "Your Online Choices" at https://www.youronlinechoices.com. If you do not accept necessary cookies, this may lead to functional restrictions of our offers.

When using cookies from other providers ("third party cookies"), it is possible that the collected data may be transferred by the respective provider to a country outside the EU or the EEA that does not offer an adequate level of data protection. In particular, the US currently does not offer adequate protection under the GDPR because government authorities in the US have the right to access personal data held by companies under certain circumstances without adequate remedies being available to data subjects.

You can also use the Cookie Consent Management Tool integrated into our platform to manage your individual settings for the use of cookies. There you will also find detailed information on the individual cookies.

The tool enables you to give consent to data processing via the website, in particular the setting of cookies, as well as to make use of your right to withdraw consent already given.      The processing of data and the use of cookies is necessary for the documentation and administration of your settings.
