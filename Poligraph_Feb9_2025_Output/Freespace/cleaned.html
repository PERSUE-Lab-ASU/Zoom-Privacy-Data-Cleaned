We encourage you to contact us first, so we have the opportunity to address your concerns directly. If you would like assistance in identifying the appropriate supervisory authority, please contact us, and we will provide you with the relevant information. 

To exercise any of these rights or if you have any questions or concerns regarding the processing of your Personal Information, please contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this Privacy Policy.
