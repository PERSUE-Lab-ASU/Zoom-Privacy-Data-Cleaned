Privacy Policy

This Privacy Notice discloses the practices of NWN Corporation and Carousel Industries of North America, LLC (collectively, “NWN Carousel” or “we”) concerning information we obtain by and through your use of the NWN Carousel websites (collectively, the “Website”) and our technology solutions (collectively, the “Services”). When we process information about you that was collected from our Website, we are typically acting as a data controller– meaning NWN Carousel determines how and why the information is being processed. However, our Services are primarily directed to our clients (“Clients”) and prospective clients, which are generally businesses. When we process information about you that was collected through your use of our Services, we are typically acting as a data processor– meaning NWN Carousel processes the information under the direction of our Clients.

Regardless of whether NWN Carousel is acting as a data processor or data controller though, we are committed to respecting your privacy and takes seriously its task in providing appropriate protection and management of your information. The purpose of this Privacy Notice is to explain the types of information NWN Carousel obtains about visitors to our Website and users of our Services, how the information is obtained, how it is used, and your choices and rights regarding your personal information.

1. How We Collect Information About You

The information we collect through your use of our Website and Services generally falls into two categories: Personally Identifiable Information (“PII”) and Non-Personally Identifiable Information (“Non-PII”). If we combine Non-PII with PII, the combined information will be treated as PII for as long as it remains combined.

Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

PII refers to information that lets us know the specifics of who you are.

PII Collected Through Website. The PII we collect through our Website generally relates to your identity and contact information, such as, your name, company name, job title, company address, e-mail address and phone number. We may request PII when you register on the Website, purchase or utilize Services that we offer, or when you request or receive information regarding our Services. We collect PII only when you voluntarily provide it to us so if you do not want us to use or collect this information, please do not provide us with it. If you choose not to provide the information we request, you may be unable to use or access certain content on our Website.

PII Collected Through Services. The PII we collect through your use of our Services generally relates to your identity and contact information, such as, your name, company name, job title, company address, e-mail address and phone number. Our processing of this information is subject to agreements NWN Carousel has with our Clients, who control whether and to what extent our Services process your PII.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information (Non-PII)

Non-PII refers to information that does not by itself identify a specific individual. The Non-PII we collect when you visit our Website may include the Uniform Resource Locator (“URL”) of the Web site that you came from before visiting our Website, which pages you visit on our Website, which URL you go to next, which browser you used to come to our Website, your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, your device ID, and any search terms entered on our Website. Our Website may automatically collect Non- PII, including the web page from which a visitor enters our Website, which pages a visitor visits on our Website, and how much time a visitor spends on each page, etc.

Passwords

Access to certain parts of our Website requires a username and password, including the Experience Management Platform. A username and password permit us to track certain information, such as software license operations. You are responsible for any action connected with the use of your password, and you should not share your password with anyone. To request a new password, please follow the instructions on the relevant Website login page. NWN Carousel customers with a current maintenance agreement may request customer support via our Website. For customer support, a customer will generally be required to submit his/her name, Organization ID and contact information, such as phone number, email address and/or facsimile number.

2. Cookies

A cookie is a small piece of data that your internet browser places on your device so that it can be recognized when it returns in the future. We use cookies and similar technologies to secure our Website, remember your preferences, and personalize content for your future visits.

Some of the most common cookies, along with their primary purposes, that we use on our Website are listed below:

Google Analytics (_ga, _gid). In order to market to anonymous visitors, we use Google Analytics to support digital advertising using data collected from Website visitors as they anonymously browse the Websites. We use the data from Google Analytics to ensure that our marketing is targeting the appropriate Website visitors. Website visitors may use the Ads Settings to opt-out of Google Analytics for Display Advertising and customize Google Display Network ads by going to https://www.google.com/settings/ads. Google Analytics’ currently available opt outs are available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/. Third-party vendors, including Google, may show our ads on sites across the Internet. We and third-party vendors, including Google, use first-party cookies (such as the Google Analytics Cookie) and third-party cookies (such as the Double Click cookie) together to inform, optimize, and serve ads based on your past visits to the Websites.

TechTarget (TS01fac3f6). TechTarget cookies may be used to build a profile of your interests in order to show you advertisements that may be relevant to you on other sites.

LinkedIn Insight Tag. The LinkedIn Insight Tag is a piece of lightweight JavaScript code that we have added to our websites to enable in-depth campaign reporting and to help us unlock valuable insights about our website visitors. We use the LinkedIn Insight Tag to track conversions, retarget website visitors, and unlock additional insights about members interacting with our LinkedIn adverts.

The LinkedIn Insight Tag enables the collection of metadata such as IP address information, timestamp, and events such as page views. All data is encrypted and retained for 180 days. The LinkedIn browser cookie is stored in a visitor’s browser until they delete the cookie or the cookie expires (there’s a rolling six-month expiration from the last time the visitor’s browser loaded the Insight Tag).

You can opt out of cookies from LinkedIn on your LinkedIn settings page and recommend you read their Cookie Policy for more information.

Demandbase Cookie (https://support.demandbase.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000463206-Cookie-based-identification-FAQs). The Demandbase cookies are retained for thirteen months on Demandbase systems. 

Adobe Dynamic Chat Cookies collect content such as name, contact information and free text for the purposes of marketing our services to users of Dynamic Chat and is retained until a user requests their data be deleted.

These companies use the data they process in accordance with their privacy policies, which may enable these companies to collect and combine information about your activities across various platforms.

Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually refuse cookies, or selectively accept certain cookies, by adjusting the preferences in your browser. If you turn off or refuse cookies, there may be some features of our Website that will not be available to you, and some portions of the Website may not display properly or be less personalized.

Please see the section entitled “Your Rights and Choices” for an explanation of your choices related to these communications.

Market Research.  We may use your information to improve our existing Services and the content of our Website, and research and develop new offerings. Unless otherwise specified and agreed to, most of these research and development activities will only use anonymous and aggregate data and not your PII. In circumstances in which your PII will or may be used for research or development purposes, we will ask for your explicit consent to do so.

Cookies.  Perform activities described in the section entitled “Cookies.”

The Lawful Basis for Processing European Personal Information

When we collect information about you through our Website, the lawful basis on which we process your personal information is our legitimate interests, namely, to provide you with information marketing our Services. We do not disclose your contact information to third-parties for their marketing purposes. When we collect information about you through your use of our Services, the lawful basis on which we process your personal information is because it is necessary to perform contractual obligations to you or our 4847-0956-7946.1 Clients (i.e. processing the data is necessary in order for us to provide you or our Clients with Services). In addition, we may process your personal information in order to comply with our legal and regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements and for the establishment, exercise or defense of legal claims.

No Sale of Personal Information for California and Nevada Residents

NWN Carousel does not sell your personal information under California or Nevada law, which defines certain disclosures of personal data for monetary or valuable consideration as “sales.”

4. How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable, standard industry technological and physical safeguards to protect your personal data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction. We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. Transactions, access to transactional records, or access to account information occurs over a server that has safeguards such as secure server software (SSL) which encrypts information before transmission. You can confirm that pages are encrypted, and we urge you to do so before submitting or transmitting sensitive information, by viewing the lock icon on the bottom of most browsers. However, no safeguards are infallible.

5. How Long We Keep Your Personal Information

In general, we will only retain your information for the time necessary to realize our legitimate business purposes and to comply with the law. However, we may continue to store anonymous or anonymized information, such as Website visits without identifiers, in order to improve our services.

6. Storage and Processing of European Personal Information Outside the European Economic Area (EEA)

If you are located in the EEA, please note that as part of our service, we may transfer your personal information to other regions, including to the United States. We protect our transfers out of the EEA with approved legal safeguards that may include Standard Contractual Clauses and other contract terms executed between NWN Carousel and its data controller Client, other certification mechanism approved by the European Union.

Generally speaking, we do not share your PII with third-parties unless those thirdparties are providing you with a service on our behalf or we have otherwise partnered with those third-parties. NWN Carousel requires these third parties to:

  1. only process the PII for limited and specified purposes consistent with your consent;

  3. notify NWN Carousel and cease processing PII (or take other reasonable and appropriate remedial steps) if the company determines that it cannot meet its obligation to provide the same level of protection for PII as is required by applicable law.

We do not disclose your PII to third-parties for their direct marketing purposes.

In certain circumstances, we may share your information with third-parties, including the following:

Service Providers, Affiliates, Agents and/or Business Partners.  We may use other companies to perform certain services including, without limitation, facilitating some aspects of our Website or Services, such as processing credit card transactions and/or sending emails, as well as hosting and maintenance, billing, customer relationship services, database storage and arrangement, and direct marketing campaign services. We share your information with these service providers only as necessary to perform these functions and provide such services, and only pursuant to binding contractual obligations requiring such third-parties to maintain the privacy and security of your data.

Special Circumstances. We also may disclose your information, including PII, in the following circumstances:

  • Required by Law. In response to a subpoena or similar investigative demand, a court order, or other request from a law enforcement or government agency or as otherwise required by applicable law.
  • Protect the Rights and Property of NWN Carousel. To establish or exercise our legal rights and/or to defend against legal claims.
  • Maintain Security. When we believe disclosure is appropriate in connection with efforts to investigate, prevent, or take other action regarding illegal activity, suspected fraud or other wrongdoing.
  • Enforce our Website Policies. To enforce our Website’s terms and conditions or other agreements or policies.
  • Corporate Transaction. In connection with a corporate transaction, such as the sale of all or a portion of our business, a divestiture, merger, consolidation, or asset sale, or in the event of bankruptcy.

If we anticipate disclosure of your information to any third-party other than those set forth above, you will be given an opportunity to opt out of disclosure of your information. However, you cannot opt out of disclosure of your personal information if required by lawful order.

11. Your Choices and Rights

You have several choices regarding the use and disclosure of your information.

Information You Provide.  With respect to the collection of your information, if you do not want your PII collected, please do not submit it to us. If you have already submitted information and would like to review, correct or remove it from our records, please contact us by submitting your request at: Privacy Request Form.

Marketing Messages.  We may send you marketing communications, about our Services that may interest you. You can opt out of our marketing communications at any time by selecting the “unsubscribe” link in the e-mail or contacting us at nwnprivacy@nwnit.com. Please note that some non-marketing communications are not subject to general opt-out, such as communications relating to Services we are providing you or our Client, sales transactions, software updates and other support related information, downloads you may request via email, patches and fixes. By providing a telephone number through the Website, you are consenting to be contacted by SMS text message. Message and data rates may apply and reply STOP to opt out of further messaging. NWN Carousel will not share any mobile data originating from you through a text messaging campaign except with your consent or as necessary to provide you communications about our services or services that you requested.

Further, no mobile information will be shared with third parties for their direct marketing purposes.

Cookies.  If you wish to disable cookies, you can set your browser not to accept cookies as described in the section entitled “Cookies.”

Information Shared with Third-Parties.  You may also choose whether your PII may be used for a purpose that is materially different from the purposes for which the information was originally collected or subsequently authorized by you. You may contact NWN Carousel as indicated below regarding the company’s use or disclosure of your PII. Unless NWN Carousel offers you an appropriate choice, we use PII only for purposes that are materially the same as those indicated in this Privacy Notice.

Rights for European Economic Area (“EEA”) Residents

In addition, if you are a resident of the EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access. Find out if we use your personal data, access your personal data and receive copies of the PII that we collect and retain about you.
  • Right to Rectification. Access your personal data and have it corrected or amended if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Right to Erasure. Request your personal data be deleted or restricted under certain circumstances. For example, if NWN Carousel is using your personal data on the basis of your consent and has no other legal basis to use such, you may request your personal data be deleted when you withdraw your consent.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing. If you believe your personal data is inaccurate, unlawful, no longer necessary for our business purposes, or if you object to our processing of your personal data, you also have the right to instruct us to restrict the processing of your data pending our investigation and/or verification of your claim.
  • Right to Data Portability. Obtain a transferable copy of some of your PII which can be transferred to another provider when the personal data was processed based on your consent.
  • Right to Object. Object to NWN Carousel’s processing of your personal data based on NWN Carousel’s legitimate interest or legal and regulatory obligations as further set out the section entitled “The Lawful Basis for Processing European Personal Information,” which NWN Carousel will review as to whether we have a prevailing interest or legal and regulatory obligations in processing your personal data.

In addition, if we process any of your personal data based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time without penalty.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, or raise a complaint on how we have handled your personal data, please contact us by submitting your request at: Privacy Request Form. If you are not satisfied with our response, or believe we are processing your personal data in violation of the law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

12. Nevada Residents

Under a Nevada privacy law (SB 220), certain Nevada residents may opt out of the sale of certain categories of PII. As of the effective date of this Privacy Notice, we do not sell PII covered by that law. However, if you are a Nevada resident who would like to optout of any future sale of your information in the unlikely event we change our practices, you may do so by contacting us at our Privacy Request Form. Your request must include your full name, street address, city, state, zip code, and an email address so that we can contact you if needed to verify your identity and the authenticity of the request.
