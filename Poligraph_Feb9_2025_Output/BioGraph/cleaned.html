Privacy Policy
(Personal Information Protection Policy)

Marginal Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "our company") establishes this Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "the Policy") regarding the handling of privacy information in the services provided on this website (hereinafter referred to as "the Services").

Marginal Co., Ltd. recognizes that customer's personal information is vital information constituting privacy in the implementation of internet-related business, homepage planning and production business, system development business, and smartphone application planning and development business. When handling personal information in our operations, we establish internal regulations for compliance with laws and regulations related to personal information and personal information protection, and we also establish organizational systems to ensure appropriate protection of personal information. By making efforts to properly protect personal information, we will respect our customers and respond to their expectations and trust in our company.

Collection, Use, and Provision of Personal Information

We will identify the purpose of use of personal information within the scope of our business activities and handle the collection, use, and provision of personal information fairly and appropriately to the extent necessary to achieve the intended purposes. We will also take measures to prevent the use of personal information beyond the intended purposes.

Compliance with Laws and Regulations

We will comply with laws and regulations related to personal information, guidelines set by the government, other standards, and social order and make efforts to ensure appropriate protection of personal information.

Appropriate Management of Personal Information

We are fully aware of the risks of unauthorized access, loss, destruction, alteration, leakage, etc., regarding the personal information we handle. We will implement reasonable security measures and take appropriate corrective actions in case of any issues.

Response to Inquiries

We will appropriately respond to inquiries from individuals regarding personal information we handle, including disclosure, correction, suspension of use, and complaints.

Continuous Improvement

We will establish management regulations and an organizational structure concerning the protection of personal information, thoroughly operate them among all employees, conduct periodic reviews, and strive for continuous improvement.
