SCOPE AND APPLICABILITY

  • This Policy explains Appointy’s approach to any Personal Information being collected through the Appointy Sites or which they have collected through a third party and the purposes for which they process such Personal Information.). It also describes your rights in respect of our processing of your Personal Information.
  • Personal Information for the purpose of this Policy refers to the definition of Personal Information in Business Data and Personal Information in End User Data which Appointy collects and processes in their capacity as data controller (i.e. when Appointy determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data). “You”, “your” for the purpose of this Policy refer to both Businesses and End Users as applicable within the limits of this Privacy Policy.
  • This Privacy Policy only applies when Appointy is the data controller of Personal Information. This Policy only applies to the use of your Personal Information by us or on our behalf. It does not apply to:
    • Personal Information collected by third parties during your communications/dealings with those third parties or your use of their products or services (for example, where you follow links to third party websites over which we have no control).
    • Personal Information processed, stored or hosted by us when we act as a Processor on behalf of our Customers in the course of providing our Services, in which case the privacy statement of the relevant Business will apply, and our data processing addendum with such Business will govern our processing of your Personal Information.

OVERVIEW OF OUR COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

  • Personal Information for the purpose of this Policy refers to the definition of Personal Information in Business Data and Personal Information in End User Data which Appointy collects and processes in their capacity as data controller. Personal Information also refers to the Personal Information collected through visitors to our Website at www.appointy.com (“Visitors”).
  • Personal Information of Visitors: If you browse Appointy’s Website or contact Appointy via their Website, Appointy will collect, process and store your Personal Information in connection with your interaction with them and their Website.
  • Personal Information in Business Data: As a data controller, Appointy collects and processes Personal Information provided by the Business, such as the Business’s contact person name, contact person phone number, their email id and any other information necessary for the provision of the Appointy Services to the Business. The Personal Information collected may be used for the following purposes:
    • To create and manage the Business’s account on the Appointy Services.
    • To communicate with the Business and provide customer support.
    • To provide the requested services and features to the Business.
    • To improve and optimize the Appointy Services.
    • To comply with legal obligations and assist in the prevention or investigation of fraud or other illegal activities.

Such Personal Information may be used by Appointy for the limited purpose of:
    • Verifying creation of End User Account through Appointy Sites.
    • To provide reminders and notifications related to the appointments.
    • To improve and personalize the End User experience on Appointy Sites.
    • To comply with legal obligations and assist in the prevention or investigation of fraud or other illegal activities.

CALIFORNIA-RESIDENT SPECIFIC RIGHTS

  • To the extent you are a ‘consumer’ as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2019 and any subsequent amendments (“CCPA”) and Appointy is a ‘business’ as defined under CCPA, the following applies to you.
  • Under the CCPA, “personal information” is information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular consumer or household. Subject to the provisions of the CCPA, you have the right to request that we disclose certain information to you about our collection, use, disclosure, or sale of your personal information over the past 12 months.
  • Right to request for information
    You have the right to request information about:

    • Categories of Personal Information Appointy has collected about you.
    • Specific pieces of Personal Information Appointy has collected about you.
    • Categories of sources from which the Personal Information is collected.
    • Business or commercial purpose for collecting Personal Information.
    • Categories of third parties with whom the business shares Personal Information. The list of categories of Personal Information collected and disclosed during the prior 12 months, and that may be collected and disclosed going forward, are listed under sections 3 and 4 of this Policy.
  • Right to request for deletion of any Personal Information collected about you by Appointy.
    You have the right to request that we delete any of your personal information that we collected from you and retained, subject to certain exceptions. Once we receive and confirm your verifiable consumer request, we will delete (and direct our service providers to delete) your personal information from our records, unless an exception applies.
  • How to submit an access or deletion request:
    If you seek to exercise the foregoing rights to access or delete Personal Information, please contact us at contact@appointy.com. We respond to all requests we receive from you wishing to exercise your CCPA rights within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable data protection laws. By writing to us, you agree to receive communication from us seeking information from you in order to verify you to be the consumer from whom we have collected the personal information and such other information as reasonably required to enable us to honor your request.

    Only you, or a person or business entity registered with the California Secretary of State that you authorize to act on your behalf (an “authorized agent”), may make the requests set forth above. The request should include your contact information and describe your request with sufficient detail that allows us to properly understand, evaluate, and respond to it. In addition, you should provide sufficient information that allows us to reasonably verify that you are the person about whom we collected the personal information or an authorized representative of that person. In order to protect the security of your personal information, we will not honor a request if we cannot verify your identity or authority to make the request and confirm the personal information relates to you. The method used to verify your identity will depend on the type, sensitivity, and value of the information, including the risk of harm to you posed by any authorized access or deletion. Generally speaking, verification will be performed by matching the identifying information provided by you to the personal information that we already have. Any disclosures we provide will only cover the 12-month period preceding our receipt of your request (and will not be made more than twice in a 12-month period). If we cannot comply with a request, or cannot fully comply with a request, the response we provide will also explain the reasons we cannot comply.

  • We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.
