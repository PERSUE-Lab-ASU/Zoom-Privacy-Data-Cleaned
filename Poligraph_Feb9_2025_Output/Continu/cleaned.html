If you are a United States resident of the State of California, additional data protection rights are provided under your state.   • The right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • The right to delete personal information collected from them.
  • The right to opt out of the sale of their personal information.
  • The right to non-discrimination for using their CCPA rights.
  • The right to limit the use and disclosure of sensitive personal information.

You have the right to complain to a Data Protection Authority about our collection and use of your Personal Data. For more information, please visit California's CCPA website.
