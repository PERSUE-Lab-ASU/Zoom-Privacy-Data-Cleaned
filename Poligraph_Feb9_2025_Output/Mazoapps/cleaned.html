Privacy Policy for Mazoapps Appointment Booking Platform

This Privacy Policy describes how Mazelon Technologies Private Limited ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our booking platform (the "Service").

**1. Information We Collect**
- *Personal Information:* When you use our booking platform, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide when booking appointments or registering for an account.

- *User Data Policy:* We may collect user data related to your interactions with the platform, including booking history, preferences, and usage patterns. This data helps us personalize your experience and improve our services.

- **Usage Information:** Mazoapps may automatically collect information about how you access and use the appointment booking platform, including your IP address, browser type, device information, and interactions with the platform.

- *Integration Data:* If you choose to integrate third-party services like Google Meet for virtual appointments, we may collect information necessary for the integration, such as meeting links, timestamps, and participant data.

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:
  • ◼ To provide, maintain, and improve our booking platform and related services.
  • ◼ To facilitate appointment bookings, manage schedules, and communicate with you about your appointments.
  • ◼ To personalize your experience and provide tailored recommendations based on your preferences and usage patterns.
  • ◼ To analyze trends, track usage, and gather insights to enhance the functionality and user experience of the platform.
  • ◼ To comply with legal and regulatory requirements and enforce our terms of service.

**3. How We Share Your Information**
We may share your personal information with the following parties:

  • ◼ Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information in response to a legal request, court order, or to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

**4. Data Security**
We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

**9. WhatsApp Broadcasting Feature**
Our WhatsApp broadcasting feature allows businesses to send messages, updates, and promotional content to their customers efficiently. To facilitate this service, we collect and process the following information:

  • Recipient Data: Includes phone numbers and names (if provided).
  • Broadcast Content: The text, templates, media, or other information you choose to send.
  • Usage Metrics: Delivery logs, read receipts, and engagement data related to messages sent.

This data is processed strictly to enable the delivery of messages via WhatsApp's Business API, ensure service functionality, and provide analytics to our users. We do not share or sell this data to third parties, except as required to deliver messages through WhatsApp (Meta) or comply with legal obligations.

Users of this feature are responsible for ensuring that they have obtained consent from recipients before sending messages and must comply with WhatsApp’s Business Policy and applicable privacy laws.

We take data security seriously and employ industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. Data retention is limited to the duration necessary to provide this service and comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
