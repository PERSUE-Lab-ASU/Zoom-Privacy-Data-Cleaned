Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you have the option to change your browser settings to decline them. Field Force Location Tracking on NoPaperForms Mobile App

The app asks the user’s consent for recording their location. If the user chooses to allow it, the app captures their location when they check in and check out on the app. The location is captured every 30 minutes while the app is running in the background. This is done for partners to know when and where their field agents are spending their working hours. It stops when the user has checked out in the app. All of this happens only if the partner institution has requested for this feature. It is disabled by default.

This information is exclusively shared with our partners who have requested it and will be used only for mapping user locations. Users may disable the mobile application's access to this information at any time by editing the settings on their mobile device. The data stored on the mobile device and their location information to which the mobile application have access will be used in the context of the mobile application, and transferred to and associated with your account in the corresponding services (in which case the data will be stored on our servers) or products (in which case the data will remain with you unless you share it with us).

Google Calendar Access on NoPaperForms Mobile App

The Google Calendar access is used to synchronize the follow-up events for a user on the NoPaperForms Mobile App. The user can also access his follow ups directly on Google Calendar. The user receives a notification on the app before the scheduled follow-up event. The user’s email address is never used or saved by NoPaperForms nor is it sent to any third party. The user can turn this feature off from the Mobile App should they choose to do so. By default, the feature is disabled.
