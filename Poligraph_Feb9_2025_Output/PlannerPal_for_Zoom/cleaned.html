  1. Your Rights and How You Are Protected: You have rights regarding your personal data, including access, correction, deletion, objection, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.
  • Right to be Informed: You have the right to know why and how we use your personal data, how long we keep it, and who we share it with. This information is provided in this policy.
  • Right of Access: You can ask for a copy of your personal data and check if we are processing it lawfully. This is known as a "data subject access request." Details on making this request are in the section ‘Data Access Request Process’.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request corrections to your personal data. If we have incomplete or inaccurate data, we'll correct it, though we might need to verify new information you provide.
  • Right to Erasure: You can ask us to delete or remove your personal data if there's no good reason for us to keep processing it. However, legal reasons may prevent us from erasing it, and we'll notify you if applicable.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data, especially in cases like direct marketing or when we rely on legitimate interests. It's not an absolute right and depends on specific circumstances, please talk to us if you wish to prevent us from using elements of personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request the restriction or suppression of your personal data in certain situations, like ensuring its accuracy, preventing erasure when it's still needed, or during verification after an objection, please talk to us if you wish to restrict or supresses elements of personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request the transfer of your personal data to yourself or a third party. We'll provide it in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format. This right applies to automated information you initially consented to, or data used for a contract.
  • Right to not be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing: We do not use data to enable any automated decisions, including profiling, and as such this right specifically is not applicable.

If you want to exercise any of these rights, contact us at team@plannerpal.co.uk.
