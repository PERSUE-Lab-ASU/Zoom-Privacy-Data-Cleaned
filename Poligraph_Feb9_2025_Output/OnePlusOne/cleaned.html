Privacy Policy

Introduction

1Plus1 Inc. ("OnePlusOne") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share information collected through our services ("Services"). By using our Services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as name, email address, IP address, and payment details when you use our Services.

  • Non-Personal Information: We collect non-personal data such as usage statistics and demographic information to improve our Services.

Use of Information

We may use the information collected to:

  • Provide and improve our Services

  • Process transactions and send related information

  • Communicate with you, including sending promotional materials

  • Prevent fraud and ensure the security of our Services

As part of our organization's philosophy in meeting the highest standards of privacy and security standards, we will utilize all commercially viable means to reduce the storage and processing of personal information.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

With your consent

  • For processing transactions (e.g., payment processors)

  • For legal reasons (e.g., to comply with laws, regulations, or legal requests)

Cookies

We may use cookies to:

  • Enhance your experience on our website

  • Understand website usage and improve functionality

  • You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. OnePlusOne has established a security program based on the Trust Services Criteria 2017 (SOC 2) standard.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our agreements.

Your Privacy Rights

As a firm believer of privacy rights for our customers, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data.

  • Right to Correct: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal data.

  • Right to Processing: You can request to limit the processing of your data.

  • Right to Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data for certain purposes.

  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

  • Right to Consent: You can withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.

  • To exercise these rights, contact us at bruno@joinoneplusone.com
