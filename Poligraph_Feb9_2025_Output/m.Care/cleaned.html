Use of Your Personal Data

We use this information to provide features of m.Care. The information may be uploaded to our servers or it may be simply stored on your device.

If you elect to share Google Fit data with us, m.Care will periodically request the selected data types from Google Fit and upload the results to m.Care. The collected data is then available to you and your care team from within the m.Care application.

You can enable or disable access to this information at any time, through your Device settings.

What About Cookies?

We use cookies to store information between visits to our site. Cookies are a mechanism to remember information about a visitor from one page to another. We use cookies to remember your name and the contents of your shopping cart. This allows you to leave our site, come back later, and still have the contents of your shopping cart intact. Our website requires the use of cookies. Turning off cookies or rejecting the LifeScience Technologies, LLC, Inc. cookie will prevent you from accessing the website.
