Privacy Policy for Live AI Coach


Effective Date: November 17, 2024

​

Thank you for using Live AI Coach! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and process your personal information when you use our Zoom App Marketplace application (“App”). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

​

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information from users beyond the minimum necessary to facilitate the functionality of the App. Specifically:

  • Direct Messages: The App generates real-time direct messages during your Zoom meeting. These messages are visible only to you and are not stored or retained by us.

  • Meeting Content: We do not capture, record, or store any part of your Zoom meeting, including audio, video, chat content, or shared materials.
     

2. How We Use Your Information

The App processes meeting-related content solely for the purpose of generating real-time insights and suggestions during your Zoom meeting. Once the meeting ends:

  • All processed information is permanently deleted and not retained.

  • No information is used for analytics, profiling, or marketing purposes.
     

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store or retain any personal information or meeting content. All data processed by the App is ephemeral and automatically deleted after being used to provide real-time insights. This ensures:

  • No user data is saved on our servers.

  • No meeting content or messages are stored beyond the duration of the meeting.
     

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or disclose any personal information or meeting content to third parties for any purpose. All processing is conducted locally within the Zoom environment during the active meeting session.
 

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure that data processed during meetings is protected from unauthorized access. However:

  • Since we do not store or retain data, there is no risk of post-meeting data breaches or leaks.
     

6. Your Rights

Since the App does not collect or store your personal information:

  • There is no stored data to access, modify, or delete.

  • If you have questions or concerns about privacy, you can contact us at privacy@liveaicoach.me
     

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or functionality. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically. Changes will take effect immediately upon posting to the App Marketplace.
