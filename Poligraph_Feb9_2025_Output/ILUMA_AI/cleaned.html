Welcome to the Privacy Policy of ILUMA, Inc. (“ILUMA”, “we”, “us”, “our”). ILUMA products and services (hereinafter: the “Service” or  “Services”) are accessed by Users through our website: www.getiluma.ai, and through our web app: app.getiluma.ai. 

​

The privacy and security of your personal information is of the utmost importance to ILUMA. By using the Services, you accept the practices set forth in this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully before using the Services. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, you may not access or otherwise use the Services.

  1. The Information We Collect

When you register to use the Services, communicate with a member of our ILUMA team, or send us an email, you’re giving us information (“Personal Information”) that we collect. That Personal Information may include your IP address, name, physical address, email address, phone number, credit card information, and other details like gender, occupation, and other demographic information. By giving us this information, you consent to your information being collected, used, disclosed, and stored by us, only as described in our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy. 

In order to use our Services, you will be required to include:

  • name

  • email address

  • phone number

ILUMA collects Personal Information from Users that is accessible through any of its third-party integrations, such as but not limited to: Slack, Zoom, or Google.  The type of data collected includes platform username, first name, last name, email address, and professional title. Personal Information of other users of those platforms, specifically username, first name and last name, may be collected in order to provide more details regarding the insights and analytics that ILUMA AI provides to its Users, such as employees involved in team discussions on public, company-wide platforms, or employee engagement analytics inside the ILUMA dashboard on the ILUMA web app. All ILUMA data, and by extension, all User data, is managed and hosted by Amazon Web Services with encryption at-rest and in-transit following Amazon Security guidelines and best practices.

    2. User Personal Information

User Personal information is only stored for the duration of time that the User engages with the Services, or until the User requests the deletion or transfer of their data, which can be done by contacting support@getiluma.ai (refer to section 5 of this Policy). ILUMA will only store User Personal Information for as long as is necessary for the purpose of maintaining the User's ILUMA account or as long as there are any applicable legal retention obligations. After the respective processing purpose ceases to apply and the retention obligations end, User Personal Information will be routinely deleted.

    3. Account Information 

ILUMA receives and stores the information you provide when you use the Services. For example, we collect information when you: provide information to sign up for an account with the Services; provide information in your Account Profile; login and operate your account within the Services; or communicate with us by email for any purpose. We may get information about how and when you use the Services. This information may include your IP address, time, date, browser used, and actions taken by you within the application. All this information is stored for the duration of your usage of the Services, or upon your request for data deletion.

We may use and disclose your Personal Information only as follows:

To promote use of our Services. For example, if you leave your Personal Information when you visit our website and don’t sign up for any of the Services, we may send you an email asking whether you want to sign up. And if you use any of our Services, and we think you might benefit from using another Service we offer, we may send you an email telling you about it.

We use third parties for secure credit card transaction processing, and we send billing information to those third parties to process your orders and credit card payments. 

  • To send you System Alert Messages. For example, we may let you know about temporary or permanent changes to our Services, like planned outages, new features, version updates, releases, abuse warnings, and changes to our Privacy Policy.

  • To enforce compliance with our Terms of Use and applicable law. This may include developing tools and algorithms that help us prevent violations.

  • To provide customer support.

  • To protect the rights and safety of our Users and third parties, as well as our own.

  • To meet legal requirements like complying with court orders and valid subpoenas.

  • To provide information to representatives and advisors, like attorneys and accountants, to help us comply with legal, accounting, or security requirements.

  • To prosecute and defend a court, arbitration, or similar proceeding.

  • To support and improve the Services we offer. 

  • To communicate with you about your account for informational, not promotional, reasons.

  • To transfer your information in the case of a sale, merger, consolidation, or acquisition. In that event, any acquirer will be subject to our obligations under this Privacy Policy, including your rights to access and choice. ILUMA will delete or transfer a User’s data at the request of that individual. To request to have your data deleted or transferred, email support@getiluma.ai. Data requested to be deleted or transferred will be carried out within 24 business hours.

   8. Cookies

When you register to use ILUMA, we store “cookies,” which are strings of code, on your computer. We use those cookies to collect information about when you visit our Website, when you use the Services, your browser type and version, your operating system, and other similar information. You may turn off cookies that have been placed on your computer by following the instructions on your browser, but if you block our cookies, it may be more difficult (and maybe even impossible) to use the Services. When you play one of the videos on the Website, the Flash player may use local shared objects, which are also called Flash cookies. We don’t use these Flash cookies for anything, but we can’t stop the player from dropping them.

 

   9. Use by Children

We do not offer the Services for use by children and, therefore, we do not knowingly collect information from, and/or about children under the age of 18. If you are under the age of 18, do not provide any information to us without the involvement of a parent or a guardian. In the event that we become aware that you provided information in violation of applicable privacy laws, we reserve the right to delete it. If you believe that we might have any such information, please contact us at support@getiluma.ai.
