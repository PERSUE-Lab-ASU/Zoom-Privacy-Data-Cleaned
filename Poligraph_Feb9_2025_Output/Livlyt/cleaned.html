What information do we collect about you?

2.1 You provide us with the following categories of information about you and this is information about you that you give us, usually via our Platform, or by phone, e-mail or otherwise. It includes information you provide when you register to use our Platform, subscribe to our service, search for a product, place an order, submit a query, and when you report a problem with our Platform:

2.1.1 Contact: basic contact information, such as your first and last name, mobile numbers, email address, work address, as well as billing and shipping details.

2.1.2 Identity: additional information you provide, including your photo, gender, date of birth, and personal information in or about the content you provide

2.1.3 Communications: records of any correspondence and communications if you contact us, including information you supply if you report a problem with our Platform to us. This covers information we learn about you from via different means of communications including (letters, emails, texts, in-app messaging & other digital messaging, calls).

2.1.4 Financial: We do not access, collect or store your payment information. We use a payment processor to manage this for us.

2.1.5 Marketing: you may also provide us with your personal direct marketing preferences, like whether you would like to receive email or text updates from us

2.2 Information we receive from other sources: We are working with third parties (including, for example, retailers, business partners, sub-contractors, contractors, in technical, payment and delivery services, debt collection agencies, advertising networks, analytics providers, and search information providers). We may receive information about you from other organisations, including:

2.2.1 Credit: credit score and insolvency information, from our third-party credit score providers and identity verification providers. LivLyt will assess and keep your LivLyt credit limit.

2.2.2 Verification: We use a third-party service to verify who you are. You will be asked to provide them with an appropriate form of government ID (like scan of a the ID or ID number) and in some cases, a selfie (to match against your ID) or such other documents or information required by us from time to time. Your use of this service is regulated by that organisation's own terms and privacy policy.

2.2.3 Advertising: Advertisers may share technical information and information about your visits with them, including your experiences or interactions with them.

2.3 Information we collect about you from your use of our Platform. We will automatically collect information from you each time you use our Platform.

2.3.1 Technical information: Technical information may include the Internet protocol (IP) address, login information, browser type and version and, browser plug-in types and versions, device settings (e.g. language, time zone), device or similar IDs, operating system and platform, hardware version, mobile operator or ISP.

2.3.2 Information about your visit: Information about your visit may include the full Uniform Resource Locators (URL), clickstream to, through and from our site (including date and time), products you viewed or searched for, page response times, download errors, length of visits to certain pages, page interaction information (such as scrolling, clicks, and mouse-overs), methods used to browse away from the page, and any phone number used to call our customer service number or social media handle used to connect with our customer service team and our social media accounts.

2.3.3 Location data: Location data includes specific geographic locations (such as through GPS, Bluetooth, or Wi-Fi signals) which we use to provide location services (if you ask or permit us to), so that we can deliver content, advertising or other services that are dependent on knowing where you are, like checking for fraudulent transactions. Location data may be collected in combination with device ID, so we can recognise your mobile browser or device when you return to the Service. Delivery of location services will involve us checking any of the following: 

2.5.3.1    the coordinates (latitude/longitude) of your location; 

2.5.3.2    your current country or region, by referencing your current IP address against public sources; and/or 

2.5.3.3    your Identifier for Advertisers (IFA) code for your Apple device, or the Android ID for your Android device, or a similar device identifier. 

2.5.3.4    You can opt-out of location sharing.

2.4 Information placed on your computer: We may store some information such as cookies on your computer. Cookies are pieces of information that an application transfers to the hard drive of a visitors computer for record-keeping purposes. This information facilitates your use of Platform and ensures that you do not need to re-enter your details every time you visit it. You can erase or choose to block this information from your computer if you want to. Erasing or blocking such information may limit the range of features available to the visitor on Platform. We also use such information to provide visitors a personalized experience on Platform. We may use such information to allow visitors to use Platform without logging in upon returning.

2.5 Information collected from your mobile device: In addition to the information mentioned above, we receive the following from your mobile device:

2.5.1 Phone state: We access read only information to phone state including phone number, device network. This allows us to provide you with a seamless experience and a higher caliber of customer service.

2.5.2 Location: It is used strictly to show you availability of product stock in your area as well as deliver your order to your exact address. We will never gather or use your specific device location without first getting your explicit permission.

2.6 Links: Our Platform may contain links to other sites. Such other sites may use information about your visit to this Platform. Our Policy does not apply to practices of such sites that we do not own or control or to people we do not employ. Therefore, we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the accuracy or the integrity of the content included on such other sites. We encourage you to read the individual privacy statements of such websites.

How do we use your information?

3.1 We use information held about you in the following ways:

3.1.1 understand how individuals use our Platform, and how we can improve it.

3.1.2 ensure content from our site is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer.

3.1.3 provide you with the information, products and services that you request from us or we think you may be interested in.

3.2 If you create and use your account with us, we use your Contact, Identity, Log- in, Financial, Credit, Verification and Automatically Collected information to:

3.2.5 provide aggregated reporting information to, and otherwise manage and fulfil our agreements with, our shareholders, investors and finance providers.

3.2.6 identify you when you sign-in to your account and give you appropriate access to our Platform (in accordance with your agreement with us).

3.2.7 enforce or apply our terms or other agreements with you.

3.2.8 notify you about changes to our service. We may conduct some profiling and automated decision-making to help us determine whether or not to verify and approve your account, including on the basis of your credit history. If you would like more information about our automated-decision making practices, or would like to request a manual review of any decision, please contact us.

3.3 When you contact or engage with us, we use your Contact, Identity, Log-in, Financial, Credit Verification and Communications information to:

3.3.1 provide you with customer support, including

3.3.2 contacting you if you've asked us to do so, including troubleshooting problems, and helping with any issues concerning our Platform, and

3.3.3 providing you with the information, products and services that you request from us.

3.4 If we share marketing or advertising with you, we may use your Contact, Marketing, Advertising and Automatically Collected information to:

3.4.1 provide you with promotional update communications by email, SMS, in-App alerts, and phone about our services about goods or services we feel may interest you.

3.4.2 contact you for your opinions about our Platform, including through surveys and other market research.

3.4.3 understand how you use and interact with our services and the things you're connected to and interested in.

3.4.4 provide you with personalised recommendations, promotional updates and marketing to improve your experience with our Platform.

3.4.5 measure or understand the effectiveness of advertising we serve to you and others, and to deliver relevant advertising to you. We may conduct some profiling and automated decision-making to help us provide you with relevant information, suggestions and recommendations for products. You can opt-out of further marketing at any time by selecting the "unsubscribe" link at the end of all our promotional updates and marketing to you, or by sending us an email at hello@livlyt.com

3.5 When we maintain and improve our Platform, we may use your Account, Marketing Advertising and Automatically Collected information (including Location data) to:

3.5.1 administer our Platform and services and for internal operations, including audits, troubleshooting, data analysis, testing, research, statistical and survey purposes.

3.5.2 evaluate and improve our products, services and Platform, including developing and testing new features.

3.5.3 keep our Platform safe and secure.

3.5.4 to detect and protect against error, fraud or other criminal activity.

3.5.5 improve our Platform to ensure that content is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer, and to alert you to any hardware or software incompatibility issues.

3.5.6 allow you to participate in interactive features of our service, when you choose to do so.

3.6 Information we receive from other sources We may combine this information with information you give to us and information we collect about you in our legitimate interests (where we have considered that these are not overridden by your rights). We will use this information and the combined information for the purposes set out above (depending on the types of information we receive).

Livlyt’s use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. For more information, please review the Google API Services User Data Policy.

3.7 Aggregated Data

3.7.1 We may pseudonymise and/or anonymise and aggregate any of the above categories of information.

3.7.2 We use aggregated information (such as statistical data or customer profile information) to help us analyse how visitors use our site (including behaviour patterns and the tracking of visits across multiple devices) and interact with us on social media (for example, statistics about demographics or users per country), provide more useful information to our customers, and understand which of our services are of most interest.

3.7.4 Where such aggregate information is derived from your personal data we will take steps to pseudonymise or anonymise your personal data, so that you cannot be easily re-identified from aggregate information retained or used for these purposes.

5.2.1 in order to store it.

5.2.2 in order to enable us to provide goods or services to you and fulfil our contract with you. This includes order fulfilment, processing of payment details, and the provision of support services.

5.3 If we transfer the information outside the UAE, we will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is subject to appropriate safeguards, such as relying on a recognised legal adequacy mechanism, and that it is treated securely and in accordance with this Policy.

What about payment processing?

6.1 Payment details you provide will be encrypted using secure sockets layer (SSL) technology or similar encryption before they are submitted to us.

6.2 Payments made on the Platform are made through our payment gateway providers in each country. You will be providing credit or debit card information directly to our payment gateway providers which operate a secure server to process payment details, encrypting your credit/debit card information and authorising payment. Information which you supply is not within our control and is subject to their own privacy policy and terms and conditions.

How do we protect your information?

7.1 We take reasonable steps, including physical, technical and organisational measures, to protect your personal information from unauthorised access and against unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction and damage.

7.2 All information you provide to us is stored on our secure servers. Any payment transactions will be encrypted using SSL technology.

7.3 Unfortunately, the transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee the security of your data transmitted to our Platform; any transmission is at your own risk. Once we have received your information, we will use strict procedures and security features to try to prevent unauthorised access.

7.4 External links: Our site may, from time to time, contain links to external sites. If you follow a link to any of these websites, please note that these websites have their own privacy policies and terms and conditions. Please check these policies before you submit any personal data to these websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or the content of such sites.

7.5 Child safety: Protecting the safety of children when they use the Internet is important to us. Our Platform is intended for use only by persons who are at least 18 years of age. You may not use our Platform unless you are 18 or older.

How long we keep your information?

8.1 We will keep personal data for:

  1. as long as you have an account with us in order to meet our contractual obligations to you, and

  2. the time period provided under the applicable law.

8.2 If you opt-out from us sending you promotional updates and marketing, or object to any other processing of your personal information, we may keep a record of your opt-out or objection so we can ensure we respect your direct marketing preferences.

8.3 We may also retain aggregate information beyond this time for research purposes and to help us develop and improve our services. You cannot be identified from aggregate information retained or used for these purposes.
