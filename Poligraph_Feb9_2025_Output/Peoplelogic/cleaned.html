We may also implement pixel tags provided by other companies, for the same purpose. The information we obtain in this manner enables us to customize the Service and to measure the overall effectiveness of the Service and associated web site and web activities.

We analyze these logs to better understand aggregate behavior and to monitor performance of our Service.
