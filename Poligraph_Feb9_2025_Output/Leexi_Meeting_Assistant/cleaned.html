Your rights

You have the right to access your personal data processed by Leexi.

Thus, you have the right :

  • to withdraw your consent at any time if you have already given your consent to the processing of your personal data.

  • to object to the processing of your data if the processing is carried out on a legal basis other than consent. However, in the case of commercial prospecting, you must specify the reasons for your request "in view of your particular situation".

  • to access your data. You have the right to know what data is being processed and to obtain a copy.

  • to have incorrect or incomplete data about you rectified. You have the right to obtain the accuracy of your Data and to request that it be updated or corrected.

  • to restrict the processing of your Data. In this case, Leexi will only process the data for storage purposes. You have the right to have your data held by Leexi limited if:

    • you challenge the accuracy of the information (exercise the right of rectification) concurrently with your request for limitation.
    • the processing is unlawful and you object to the erasure of the data and instead request the restriction of the use of the data.
    • you have objected to the processing (exercise of the right to object) and you exercise your right to restriction during the verification as to whether the legitimate grounds pursued by the controller prevail over those of the data subject.
    • or Leexi no longer needs the personal data for the purposes of the processing, but they are still necessary for the establishment, exercise or defence of legal claims.

  • to have your personal data erased. You have the right to have your data held by Leexi erased if :

    • the data is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or is processed in another way.
    • you have withdrawn your consent on which the processing was based and there are no other legal grounds for the processing.
    • you object or are opposed to the processing of the data and there are no compelling legitimate grounds for the processing.
    • the data have been processed unlawfully.
    • the data must be erased to comply with a legal obligation under EU or Member State law to which Leexi is subject.
    • or the data concerns a child under the age of 16.

  • to the portability of your data. You have the right to retrieve your data in a machine-readable format and store it yourself or transfer it to a third party. This applies provided that the data is processed by automated means and the processing is based on your consent, on a contract to which you are party or on pre-contractual obligations.

  • to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority for personal data.
