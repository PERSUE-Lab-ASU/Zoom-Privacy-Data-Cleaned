We may collect information that can be used to identify you (“Personal Information”) when you use www.brighthire.com (the “Site”), use the BrightHire software platform, or apply for a job with us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to Personal Information about job applicants that we process on behalf of our customers or Personal Information about employees and contractors of our customers who participate in interviews for roles at our customers.
