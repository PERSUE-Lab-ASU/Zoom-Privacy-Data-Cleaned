Cookies and other tracking technologies

We utilize various technical tools, such as cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies, to track the activities of our users on our Online Services. Cookies are small pieces of data sent by a website to your computer's hard drive when you browse a website, while web beacons refer to tracking technologies used to verify if you have accessed specific content on our Online Services. We use or may use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Performance cookies: These cookies help us count visits and traffic sources, allowing us to measure and improve the performance of our site. They provide insight into the most and least popular pages on our site and show us how users navigate through the site. The information collected by these cookies is anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users.
  • Targeting cookies: These cookies are set by our advertising partners through our site. They may use your browsing history to create a profile of your interests and display relevant advertisements on other websites. These cookies uniquely identify your browser and device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will not receive targeted advertising across different websites.
  • Functional cookies: These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as videos and live chats. We may set these cookies, or they may be set by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages. If you do not allow these cookies, some or all of these features may not work correctly.
  • Strictly necessary cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be turned off in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions taken by you, such as setting privacy preferences, logging in, or filling out forms. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site may not work correctly as a result. You can update your Cookie preferences anytime on our website. We may link the information we gather from cookies or other technologies to the personally identifiable information you provide while using our Online Services. We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which ¬¬remain on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a personalized and interactive experience on our Online Services. You can remove persistent cookies at any time by following the directions in the “Help” section of your Internet browser. Please note that if you choose to disable cookies, some features of our Online Services may not work correctly. Our partners, affiliates, and service providers may also use cookies and other technologies to enhance your experience while interacting with our Online Services, and to deliver or target advertising. We may receive individual and aggregated reports based on the use of certain technologies by these companies.
