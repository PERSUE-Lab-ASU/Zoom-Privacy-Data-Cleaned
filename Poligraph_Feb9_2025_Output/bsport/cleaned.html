8. Hypertext links and cookies

When browsing the bsport.io website, cookies may be installed on the user’s computer. A cookie is a small text file. It does not identify the user but it records information relating to the user’s browsing of a website. The data obtained is used to facilitate subsequent browsing of the website and enable various measurements of visit frequency.

The bsport.io website uses analytical cookies.

However, the user may change the settings of the computer to reject cookies as follows:

On Internet Explorer: Select the tools button (gear cog icon on upper right) / ‘Internet options’. Click ‘Privacy’ and select ‘Block all cookies’. Click Ok.

On Firefox: at the top of the browser, click the menu button and go to ‘Options’. Select ‘Privacy and security’. Set ‘Firefox will choose:’ to ‘Use custom settings for history’. Uncheck cookies to deactivate them.

On Safari: Click on the menu icon at the top-right of the browser (gear cog symbol). Select ‘Preferences’. Click ‘Display advanced settings’. In the ‘Privacy’ section, click on ‘Content settings’. Under ‘Cookies’, you can choose to block cookies.
