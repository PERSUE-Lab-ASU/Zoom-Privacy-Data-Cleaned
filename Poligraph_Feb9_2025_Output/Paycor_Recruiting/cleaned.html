  • Any further personal data contained in any files that you share via the Gravity application

Cookies

When you sign in through the Gravity application, Paycor will assign you a unique identifier and send such information to your computer or device hard drive in the form of a cookie, which is a very small bit of code. This code is uniquely yours and allow you to use the Gravity application’s functionality with minimal subsequent registration procedures. If you apply for a job opening that has been shared through the Gravity application, Paycor will assign you a unique identifier and send such information to your computer or device hard drive in the form of a cookie. Because you assist Paycor in developing your Personal Profile username and password, you have a significant role in protecting your Personal Profile and Personal Information. No one can access or edit your Personal Profile without knowing your Personal Profile username and password, so do not share these with others.

Personal Choices

Paycor acknowledges an individual’s right to access their Personal Information. Social Network Profiles

All use of your Personal Information by any social network service provider is subject to each network’s posted terms of service and privacy policy.

Vouching Content

Paycor may ask you to vouch for people that have applied to job openings you have shared on the Gravity application. Your Employer will be responsible for determining who is allowed to view your responses to such vouching requests.

In addition to the circumstances articulated previously in this Privacy Policy above, with regard to the Gravity application, Paycor may also disclose your Personal Information with:

Paycor Smart Sourcing

EFFECTIVE DATE: July 18, 2023

Paycor Smart Sourcing’s AI-powered, talent-sourcing bots (“Bots”) collect and use data from various online sources to rapidly identify the right talent for each job and to reach out to that talent – sometimes before they may even start searching for their next job.

In addition to the provisions outlined in the Paycor Privacy Policy, the following information below applies to the use of Paycor Smart Sourcing.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

Paycor Smart Sourcing may collect Personal Information from you or about you:

  • When you uploaded Personal Information to publicly available websites and social media platforms
  • From third parties in order to obtain contact information for candidate leads

Personal Information collected may include:

  • Race, ethnicity, religion, health, and sexual orientation (anonymized) if provided through the use of services

How we process an individual’s personal data

Following collection of personal data, our system creates a candidate profile for each individual whose personal data has been collected. The process involves merging the personal data collected from various different sources and, where there are any discrepancies, uploading the most recent available.

Our system also uses certain predictive algorithms to fill in blanks in individual profiles, such as where the personal data collected does not include a particular skill or experience which might normally be included. In situations such as these, our system in conjunction with information collected by our Bots, predicts whether a person is likely to have a certain skill and adds it to their profile..  Before providing the profiles to the customer, the Bots search through the original sources of the personal data collected to determine if any information needs to be updated. Once all relevant data has been updated, the profiles are pseudonymized and provided to the customer. This process may be repeated until the customer selects the individuals they wish to engage or interview; all throughout the customer’s search the profiles remain pseudonymized.

For individuals located within the US, we will provide contact information if we are able to obtain it. For individuals based in the EU and Israel, we will contact them to, notify: that we hold their personal data, how and why we hold and process that personal data, and that they have been selected by a customer for direct contact in connection with a potential job opportunity. For Individuals based in Israel and the EU, we will not provide the customer with the contact and other identifying details until we have obtained their consent.
