§ 3. Cookies

  1. Cookies are small packages of computer data, in particular small text fragments, that are stored on the Internet user's terminal equipment (e.g. Smartphone, computer) by the websites, including the Website, visited by that person. Cookies contain a range of information that is often necessary for the proper functioning of websites (Cookies).
  2. Cookies are used when the user is browsing the Website.
  3. The Cookies used by the Company are safe for the devices of the Website users. 
  4. The range of the Cookies that may be installed on the device of a given user depends on his/her choices made within the Website. If the user does not consent to other Cookies, in line with the functionalities and information displayed within the Website, only the essential Cookies will be used, i.e. those necessary for the correct display of the Website and its use by the user. 
  5. The Company uses the following types of Cookies:
    1. Session Cookies, which are stored on the device of the Website user and remain there until the session of the relevant browser ends. The stored information is then permanently deleted from the user's device;
    2. Permanent Cookies, which are stored on the device of the Website user and remain there until deleted. They are not deleted from the user's device once the session of the relevant browser has ended or the device has been switched off.
  6. The Company uses the following types of Cookies on the Website:
    1. essential (technical) Cookies, which are required for the proper operation of the Website. The Company uses them to:
      1. enable the user to use the services available on the Website;
      2. maintain the user's session on the Website;
      3. ensure the proper display of the Website;
      4. remember whether the user has consented to the display of selected content;
      5. marketing, which are used for marketing activities;
      6. analytical, which are used to measure user activity on the Website and the effectiveness of marketing activities carried out by the Controller. The Controller uses them to:
      7. analyse traffic on the Website;
      8. check the sources of traffic on the Website, i.e. the sources of redirections.
