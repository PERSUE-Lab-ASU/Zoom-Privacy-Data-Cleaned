Privacy Policy

​

 Updated December 2023 

​

1. Introduction to Kolabrya Welcome to Kolabrya. We specialize in enhancing meeting productivity through our AI-driven web application, kolabrya.ai. Our platform is designed to visualize meeting discussions in real-time and generate valuable insights, such as decisions, action items, and summaries from live meetings.

​

2. Kolabrya.ai - AI Web Application Kolabrya.ai utilizes AI LLM technology to transform the way meetings are conducted, offering a unique and efficient meeting experience.

​

  • Sharing of User Data with Third-Party AI Platforms: Our platform is committed to maintaining transparency in how we handle user data. An essential component of our service involves the use of advanced AI-powered Large Language Models (LLMs) to process and analyze meeting transcriptions. When you use our platform, the content of your meetings, including discussions and the names of participants, is securely transmitted to these AI models for processing. This data is essential for us to provide key features such as real-time transcription, meeting summaries, and analytical insights. We ensure that the sharing of this data is conducted in strict adherence to our privacy standards, with robust measures in place to safeguard your information. Our AI partners are selected based on their commitment to data security and privacy. The use of this data is confined strictly to the stated purposes of enhancing your meeting productivity and is not used for any other form of processing or analysis. 

    • This data is not used to train AI models​

  • Data Collection and Use: We collect calendar information including meeting titles, links, dates, and times and meeting transcriptions. This information, obtained with user consent, is used to schedule the Kolabrya bot for joining meetings and providing our services.

  • Integration with Conference Tools: Kolabrya.ai integrates with various conferencing tools such as Google Meet, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams.

  • Specifically for Google : Kolabrya's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements and we ensure that this data is accessed and used solely for the purpose of enhancing meeting productivity.

  • Data Storage and Security: User data is stored securely in encrypted databases. After meetings, users have the option to delete their data from our systems.

  • Data Sharing and Processing: User data is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes. We use Large Language Models (LLMs) to analyze meeting data and generate insights, ensuring that this data does not contribute to the training of these models.

  • Data retention : We retain data to provide the advertised services including meeting history, meeting summary, action items, tasks, names of participants, and other features. This information is stored in our secure encrypted data bases as long as the users want. This data can be deleted using delete functionality and deletion will erase all history and logs from our databases. The AI LLM Kolabrya uses retains data including meeting transcription, participant names, meeting title, and time stamps for a period of 30 days to monitor abuse; and this data is deleted after 30 days.

  3. Right to Erasure: Under certain conditions, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, commonly referred to as the 'right to be forgotten'.

  4. Right to Restriction of Processing: You may request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, which means that your data can only be held by us and not used further.

  5. Right to Data Portability: Where applicable, you have the right to receive the personal data that you have provided to us in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and also to request that we transmit this data to another controller.
