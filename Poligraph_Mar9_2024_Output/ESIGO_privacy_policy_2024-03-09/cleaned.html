Overview

A cloud PSTN service targeted on providing hosted local PSTN connection for UC platforms.

Features

Call from everywhere

  1. Multi-devices
    • Make calls via PC/mobile/hard phones
  2. Multi-countries
    • Subscribe numbers from different countries in APAC
  3. Reporting and monitoring
    • Monitor on the PSTN connections
    • Monitor on the PSTN costs
  4. Intelligent provisioning
    • One-touch phone number subscription
    • One-touch user migration between different UC platforms
      – Zoom
      – Microsoft
      – WebEx
      – Avaya

Benefits

Flexible working experience

Access to your work phone from home or on holiday using multiple devices.

Scalability

Increase or decrease the usage on the demand of your company

Reduced IT cost

No purchase or maintenance of hardware

Access to automatic updates

Regular updates on the platform.
