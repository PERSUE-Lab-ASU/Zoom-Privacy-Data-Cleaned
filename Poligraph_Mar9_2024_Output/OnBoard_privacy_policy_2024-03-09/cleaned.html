      HOW DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION FROM YOU?

      We collect personally identifiable information we receive through your use of Passageways Websites in several ways: (i) when you register for Passageways’ Services through Passageways Websites, (ii) when you (or the Associated Entity) use the Services, and (iii) when you elect to contact us electronically to request a trial, ask a question or for other reasons.

      We also receive automatic and passive data (both personally identifiable and otherwise) from the computer used to access Passageways Websites and when you interact with Passageways Websites. In this regard, Passageways Websites use common automated information gathering tools such as logs files that record page activity, “one-pixel gifs,” “clear gifs,” or “web beacons” which are small bits of code embedded in Passageways Websites to gather certain information, and/or “cookies.” When you visit Passageways Websites, whether or not you are a registered user of the Services (as “Registered User”), Passageways’ servers send a “cookie” to your computer. Cookies are small strings of text that are sent by Passageways Websites to your browser or device and then stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive or device. Passageways uses cookies that are session-based (“Session Cookies”) and persistent-based (“Persistent Cookies”). Session Cookies exist only during one session. They disappear from your computer when you close your browser software or turn off your computer. Persistent Cookies remain on your computer after you close your browser or turn off your computer. To the extent Passageways Websites contains any third party-provided features or functions, such features or functions may also send cookies from such companies or their advertising partners to your browser (known as “third party cookies”). Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but it is possible to change your browser setup so that it warns you each time a cookie is being sent, blocks third party cookies or blocks or rejects all cookies. However, rejecting cookies may prevent you from taking advantage of certain portions of Passageways Websites.
