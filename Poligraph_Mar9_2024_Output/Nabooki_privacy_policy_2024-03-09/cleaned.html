The website nabooki.com (“Website”) is operated by Nabooki Pty Ltd ACN 169 881 430 trading as nabooki (“Nabooki”, “we” or “us” or “our” in this document). In this Privacy Policy, an individual or an entity that uses our Services is referred to as “User”, “Users” or “you”.

There are five categories of Personal Information that we collect:

Nabooki collects various non-personally identifying information, such as for example the number of visitors coming to the website, geolocation data with your permission, cookie-related data as described below, online identifiers (e.g. IP address, device IDs) etc. This is collected to help us understand the way our website is used and to improve our Services.

If you are an individual who interacts with a User using our Services – that User is the controller of your information and you should contact them directly for assistance with any requests or questions relating to your personal information.

We may, for statistical, security or quality purposes, use cookies and other online usage tracking tools to perform and improve our Services.

Specific uses of your information include but are not limited to record:
