Keypoint Connect Privacy Policy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Therefore:

  • Data we log from the use of Keypoint Connect is used for diagnostic purposes only.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use any data we collect from the use of Keypoint Connect for advertising or marketing.


Keypoint Connect Security and Privacy Statement

The host PC, the Connect websites (Server) and the participant web browsers all communicate via https and wss over a TCP connection on port 433. Encryption requires TLS 1.2, or higher, which is enabled by default on modern versions of Windows and supported by most major web browsers. The server facilitates the exchange of basic, polling-related, information, between the host PC and the participants. This information includes data such as:

  • Meeting IDs.
  • The text for questions and choices.
  • Numeric and text-based responses submitted by participants.
  • Optional participant login criteria.
The operator of the host PC may request names or pre-defined user IDs to uniquely associate participant responses with an individual within the host PC. The operator may also provide a password to limit voting to participants with knowledge of the meeting ID and password.

The server may log operational information and meeting details for diagnostic purposes. Examples of this information include:

  • Meeting IDs
  • License information
  • Number of participants
  • Question IDs
  • Number of responses
  • Other information exchanged between the host PC, the server and the participant browsers.
Any information, such as screen sharing, audio or video, that is shared with participants via 3rd party meeting applications is not logged by Keypoint Connect and is therefore not addressed by this statement.


Online Meeting Platform Integration Privacy Policy

We do not log any account or personal information specific to your online meeting hosting platform. For example, a presentation shared with attendees via Microsoft Teams, during which participants may use external or integrated voting keypads to respond to questions, may temporarily log Keypoint Connect related information specific to the voting but, will not log Microsoft Teams related information pertaining to the overall meeting.


Webex Embedded App Privacy Policy

The Webex embedded apps framework provides the option to allow apps access to limited Webex related personal data. If the host of the meeting has configured Keypoint to request participant names, the Keypoint Connect embedded app will attempt to return the display name of the participant or, a derived identifier which is unique to the session but is not personally identifiable. Keypoint may also temporarily log Keypoint Connect related information specific to the voting but, will not log any additional Webex related information pertaining to the user or the overall meeting.


Zoom (Embedded) App Privacy Policy

In addition to the standard Keypoint-related information listed above, the app accesses the following Zoom-specific information:

  • The user's PID is stored to facilitate authorization and deauthorization of the app.
  • The user's role (host or other) is determined to provide the host with an option to abstain from the voting.
  • The meeting ID which is used to connect the virtual keypads to the Keypoint Connect meeting.

Data Retention

The specific information necessary to authorize the app will be retained for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. This information is removed immediately upon deauthorization of the app.

Zoom (Web) Integration Privacy Policy

We do not log any information specific to your Zoom meetings. Limited information is associated with your Keypoint Connect account during authorization of the Zoom integration app. This information is automatically removed upon successful deauthorization of the Zoom integration app.


Data Retention

The specific information necessary to associate your Keypoint Connect license with your Zoom account will be retained for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. This information is removed immediately upon deauthorization of the Keypoint Connect Zoom Integration.
