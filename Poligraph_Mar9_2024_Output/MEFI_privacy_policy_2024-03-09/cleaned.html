Privacy Policy for MEFI

Last updated: 03.11.2023

This Privacy Policy describes how LIKE WEB SRL ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our MEFI application (the "App") integrated with the Zoom service.

Information We Collect

  • When you use the App, we may collect personal information you provide us, such as your name, email address, and Zoom meeting details.
  • We collect data necessary to schedule your Zoom meetings, including chosen dates, times, and participant information.
  • Information generated through the use of our App, such as usage data, preferences, or any custom data as required by our CRM functionalities.

Use of Information

  • To create and manage your scheduled Zoom meetings.
  • To sync with our CRM and create tasks associated with your Zoom meetings.
  • To improve our App and customer service.

Sharing of Information

  • We may share information with our CRM system to create associated tasks.
  • Information about your scheduled Zoom meetings may be shared with participants as necessary.

Your Data Control Rights

  • You may manage your Zoom meeting preferences directly through Zoom.

Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to help protect your information and to maintain security of our App.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us
