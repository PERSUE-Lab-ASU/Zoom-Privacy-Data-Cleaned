Booking Autopilot – Zoom integration Privacy Policy

Booking Autopilot PTY LTD

1. Overview
Our Zoom integration is designed to enable offering online classes by scheduling meetings and enabling parents to join them from the parent portal.

For a centre to offer online classes via Zoom it needs a Zoom account to be connected to Booking Autopilot. Once an account is connected, Booking Autopilot will schedule a meeting at start of each day for each class running in the centre. By default, the Zoom meeting will be scheduled with video on for the host and the participants.

Parents will be able to see a Join online meeting button next to the classes in the parent portal dashboard on the day of the class. They will automatically be put into a “waiting room” until the host (the teacher) start the meeting.

2. Scope of Booking Autopilot – Zoom integration and data stored
Booking Autopilot – Zoom integration doesn’t use the Recording or Chat scopes.

We attest that we will retain data collected through our Zoom integration only for as long as necessary to perform our Zoom integration’s functions as disclosed.

Booking Autopilot doesn’t collect and store any sensitive Zoom user data – Booking Autopilot only stores access and refresh tokens, and Zoom User Id for the connected Zoom account. These are deleted when user disconnects their account (revokes access).

3. Data that is not collected through the Booking Autopilot – Zoom integration

Booking Autopilot doesn’t collect, through the Zoom integration, the user or participant’s:
(1) race or ethnic origin;
(2) political, religious, or philosophical opinions or beliefs;
(3) trade union membership;
(4) biometric or genetic information;
(5) information about personal health, sexual activity, or sexual orientation; or
(6) criminal history
(7) financial data, payment data
(8) authentication information
(9) personally identifying information (PII)
(10) Personal User Data from users or participants under the age of 16

4. Data collected through the Booking Autopilot – Zoom integration is not shared to other parties

Booking Autopilot doesn’t disclose to fourth parties, any data collected through your Zoom integration.

Booking Autopilot doesn’t use, or disclose to a fourth party, any data collected through your Zoom integration for: (1) building profiles or recording use; (2) monitoring employees; (3) location tracking; or (4) attention tracking or ‘heat maps’

Booking Autopilot will not sell any data collected through your Zoom integration, or use any such data for: (1) advertising or marketing using profiles based on such data, regardless of whether the data is anonymized or aggregated; (2) conducting or assisting with surveillance; or (3) reverse engineering or reconstructing profiles.
