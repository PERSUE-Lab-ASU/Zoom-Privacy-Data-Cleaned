  1. To provide you with information requested from us, relating to our products or services.

Storing Your Personal Data

Data you supply will be stored in the Microsoft Azure cloud. To learn more about BlueCamroo’s move to Microsoft Azure you can click here.

To make the system as secure as possible we encrypt each session at the browser level with a 128-bit encryption. BlueCamroo also offers a highly granular permission algorithm to protect access to personal data. This allows the admin to determine how much access to data each user or group will get. BlueCamroo users’ responsibility is to protect their access to their BlueCamroo workspaces. BlueCamroo is not liable for any data breach if a third party gains access to personal data via a lost password, virus, malware on an affected access point device or any other user-prompted error made outside of our control.

BlueCamroo will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will also retain personal data we process on behalf of our clients for as long as needed to provide services to our client. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. 

Disclosing Your Information

We will internally share your personal information to necessary members of BlueCamroo to provide a better service for you. This includes, where applicable, our subsidiaries, our holding company and its other subsidiaries (if any).

We may also disclose your personal information to third parties:

  1. Where we are legally required to disclose your information.
  2. To assist fraud protection and minimize credit risk.
  3. Upon when we present you an opportunity and you explicitly consent to having your details shared.

Use of Cookies on www.bluecamroo.com

On occasion, we may gather information about your computer for our services and to provide statistical information regarding the use of our website.

Such information will not identify you personally it is statistical data about our visitors and their use of our site. This statistical data does not identify any personal details whatsoever.

Where used, these cookies are downloaded to your computer automatically. This cookie file is stored on the hard drive of your computer as cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive. They help us to improve our website and the service that we provide to you.

All computers have the ability to decline cookies.  This can be done by activating the setting on your browser which enables you to decline the cookies. Please note that should you choose to decline cookies, you may be unable to access particular parts of our website.

Use of Cookies on Your BlueCamroo Workspace

BlueCamroo requires cookies to function. If you disable cookies in your browser or computer then BlueCamroo will not function.

General Data Processing Regulations (GDPR) Provision

This policy extends to our clients. However, if your organization is using BlueCamroo to store and/or process the Personal Information of your clients then it is your organization’s responsibility to update their own privacy protocols.

GDPR Information

In order to keep in compliance with the EU’s GDPR we will be sharing with you some information on how we store and process your data:

  • Who is the data controller?
    • The data controller is BlueCamroo Inc. for information stored about BlueCamroo Users. Each workspace has the ability to collect and store information and each company that uses BlueCamroo and is subject to  the GDPR has to assign their own Data Controller.
  • How can users contact our data controller?
    • Users can contact us via Support@BlueCamroo.com. Non-users that have questions about our GDPR policies can email us at Info@BlueCamroo.com.
  • Does BlueCamroo use personal information to make automated decisions?
    • We send out emails to alert users of updates to BlueCamroo; users have the option of opting out of these emails. We use user’s name and email to identify support tickets and open a Case in our BlueCamroo workspace when we receive an email to our Support email address.
  • Is providing personal information mandatory?
    • We only collect the information needed so that BlueCamroo can run effectively. The business contact info, and the contact info for the users. We also run a survey before a demo to find out what features of BlueCamroo they are interested in.
  • Do you transfer personal information internationally? If so, why?
    • We do not transfer any information. All of the data we collect is stored on the Microsoft Azure Cloud.
