Next LvL Ai ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to safeguarding the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how we use and protect it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

1. Data We Collect

a. Zoom Data:

  • For users integrating with Zoom, we collect and store links to your meeting/phone call transcripts, analyzing these to provide you with valuable insights regarding those meetings and associating these insights with your contacts in HubSpot based on the attendee email addresses for that meeting.

b. Google Data:

  • For users integrating with Google, we collect and store links to your Google Meet transcript Google Doc files, then analyze these files with artificial intelligence to provide you with key insights. We also find meeting attendee email addresses using a Google Calendar Integration. Through integration with Gmail we can automatically draft follow up emails to your clients based on the outcomes of your meetings.

2. How We Use Your Data

a. Integration with HubSpot CRM:

  • Your meeting and call transcripts may be integrated with your HubSpot CRM account to offer relevant data and insights to enhance your sales processes.

b. Integrations with AI models:

  • Text from your meeting and call transcripts may be processed using our custom integrations with external AI models in order to provide accurate details about your meetings.
