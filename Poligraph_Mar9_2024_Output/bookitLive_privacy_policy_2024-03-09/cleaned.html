Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

bookitLive is committed to protecting the privacy of its service providers and their clients. This privacy policy applies to the websites managed by bookitLive International as well as the services and products provided through this site. Use of the bookitLive websites and services indicates your consent to the collection and use of information as described below.

This privacy policy applies specifically to the bookitLive online booking software provided by bookitLive International and hosted in Australia.

This privacy policy sets out how we collect, use and handle Personal Information that we collect from:
Visitors, people who visit or browse our website,
Service Provider, business who establish an account with us,
Clients, people who access our services to make a booking or product purchase or otherwise access our services.

Privacy of data

bookitLive will not use data inputted on their system in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. bookitLive collects information from users at various points during the booking process. When a booking is made some users may leave information and a message. The content is seen by service provider and the user.

Ownership of data

bookitLive has no ownership or rights whatsoever to the data collected by the service provider. The service provider owns the data.

Customer data

bookitLive uses customer data only in connection with providing the booking services – we do not monitor, sell or use customer data for any other purposes. We do not control the actions of anyone with whom you or any other service user may choose to share information.

Right of access

Individuals have the right to access any personal data that we hold about you and in some circumstances provided a copy of the data.
Right of erasure: In certain circumstances, you have the right to the erasure of personal data that we hold about you
Right to rectification: You have the right to require us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data

To exercise your rights, please submit a support request. Please note that we may request you to provide us with additional information in order to confirm your identity and ensure that you are entitled to access the relevant personal data.

Encryption of data

bookitLive takes every precaution to protect user information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, the information is protected both online and off-line. The system data transmission is protected by 128/256 bit SSL encryption at all times. While on a secured page the lock icon will appear on Web browsers such as Chrome, Safari, Firefox and Microsoft Internet Explorer. Alternatively this may also appear as https in the address bar.

Data Retention

How long we retain your Personal Data depends on the type of data and the purpose for which we process the data. We will retain your Personal Information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Staff confidentiality agreements

All user information is restricted in bookitLive’s offices. Only bookitLive officers, employees and authorised agents who need the information to perform a specific job such as support activities are granted access to personally identifiable information. All staff have signed Confidentiality agreements.

Server security

bookitLive servers storing personally identifiable information are kept in a secured Tier 3 data centre environment. bookitLive application web and database servers are housed in Sydney, NSW Australia Datacentre and managed by Australian Pacific Data Centres (APDC).

SMS services

To provide secured SMS services, bookitLive use Australasia’s largest commercial SMS provider, MessageMedia, an Australian based company with approximately 8000+ business clients including Australia’s four major banking institutions, government and healthcare entities. MessageMedia provide a business-grade SMS service based on a high availability resilient/redundant platform and use encryption technology to safeguard customer data integrity. MessageMedia only use Tier-1 Australian carriers such as Telstra and Optus to ensure all our clients’ phone numbers and message content is not passed to unreliable or potentially fraudulent third-parties.

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data that websites send to your computer or other web-based devices to uniquely identify your browser or to store information or settings on your browser based on your use. Cookies allow us to identify you when you return to the bookitLive web application, providing you with a streamlined and customised experience. bookitLive may employ the use of cookies to analyse trends and administer our web application.

You can always control the use of cookies, but if you choose to disable all cookies, it may limit your use of certain functions or features of the bookitLive web application.

Analytics Tools

The bookitlive website and web application use Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager, which are web analytics services provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”), to evaluate your use of the bookiLlive website and web application. Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager place a third-party cookie on your computer that is then used to compile reports of visitor traffic and internet usage. Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager does not have a database of individual profiles for each visitor and only collects aggregate data.

Log Files

We log IP addresses for security reasons and log file/system activity to administer the system. However, none of the information stored in our log files, including but not limited to IP addresses, is linked to personally identifiable information.

bookitLive uses log files comprising of non-personally identifiable information to analyse trends, administer the website and web application, track movements throughout the website and web application

This information may include your IP address or other proxy servers you use to connect to the Internet, device and application identification numbers, your browser type, your Internet service provider (or mobile carrier), your operating system and system settings, and the location and time zone associated with your usage. Based on certain Internet standards.

Sharing

If you are a user making a booking, bookitLive never shares your personal information with anyone except the service provider with whom you request a booking. Each service provider only receives the information particular to your bookings with them. However we have no access or control over how the service provider will use this information and cannot guarantee the veracity of data or whether it is being used in a legal or illegal way.

bookitLive will not, without your consent, divulge your personal information to a third party except in the following circumstances:

  • If required to do so by law or by any organisation with appropriate authority;
  • To any financial institution with whom we hold an account for verifying credit status and / or validating financial transactions;
  • We may provide aggregate statistics about our sales, clients, traffic patterns and other site information to third parties, but these statistics will not include any information that could personally identify you

bookitLive is not responsible for the privacy practices of other companies or websites.
