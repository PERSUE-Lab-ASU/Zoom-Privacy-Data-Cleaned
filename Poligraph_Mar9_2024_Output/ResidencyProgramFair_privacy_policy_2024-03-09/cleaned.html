Residency Program Fair Secretariat(“Secretariat”, “we”, “us” or “our”) will keep your personal information, but we are trying to protect it by stipulating the handling of the personal information we have kept as follows.

【Purpose of Use of personal information】

1)Use a remote description system
2)Confirmation of and response to inquiries about our services.
3)Shipping for document request
4)Service implementation

【Provision of personal information to Third Parties】

In principle, we will not provide personal information to third parties without the user's consent unless required by law.

【Outsourcing of personal information processing】

We outsource part of our business in order to provide better services to our customers in terms of business operations. Participant's personal information may be entrusted to a subcontractor. In this case, select a company that is allowed to handle personal information appropriately, determine the matters necessary to prevent leakage of personal information of participants by appropriate management and confidentiality of personal information in contracts,We will manage it appropriately.

【Voluntary provision of personal information】

Providing personal information is voluntary.However,If you do not provide the information required to use the service, you may not be able to use the service.

【Request for disclosure of personal information】

You have the right to request notification, disclosure, correction, addition, deletion and veto of use or provision of the purpose of use of personal information. For more information, please contact us the following window.

【Contact us】

If you have any questions about personal information or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us..
