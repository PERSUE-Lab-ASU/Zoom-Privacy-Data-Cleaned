Gracent Tech LLC Privacy Policy for Suggestly

Introduction

Gracent Tech LLC ("Gracent", "we", "us", or "our") offers the Suggestly app ("Suggestly", "App"), a service that connects to your Zoom calls, transcribes conversations, and provides relevant information from company FAQs and other company information. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect through our App, how we use and protect that information, and the choices you can make about how we use this information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Our App collects information in a few different ways:

1.1 Google OAuth Access Data

Suggestly connects to your Google Account via OAuth and requests access to certain scopes for enhanced functionality. The scopes we request access to include:
  • Primary Email Address: Your primary Google Account email address is used for identification and direct communication purposes.
  • Personal Info: This includes any personal info you've made publicly available on your Google profile. We use this information to customize user experience.
  • Google Drive Files: With your permission, our App can see, edit, create, and delete specific Google Drive files that you use with the App. This includes company FAQ documents or other relevant information that you choose to upload to the App. The content of these documents is used to generate relevant responses during Zoom calls, enhancing the functionality of the App by making it more knowledgeable about your company's specifics. This also allows for a seamless integration where necessary documents can be managed directly through the App.
  • Google Calendar (Read-only): The App has read-only access to any of your calendars accessible via Google Calendar. This is used for scheduling features within the App, such as scheduling bots for upcoming calls.

1.2 Zoom OAuth Access Data

Suggestly connects to your Zoom account via OAuth and requires certain permissions to provide its services effectively. Below are the Zoom scopes we request access to and why we need them:
  • zoomapp:inmeeting: This scope is essential for Suggestly's operation as it enables the app to provide suggestions directly within the Zoom client. This ability is at the heart of Suggestly's functionality, enabling it to deliver real-time FAQ responses efficiently during your Zoom meetings.
  • user:read: Suggestly requires this scope to uphold data privacy and security standards. This scope allows the app to authenticate user identities and permissions, ensuring that the real-time FAQ responses are delivered only to authorized users during a Zoom meeting.
  • meeting:read: This scope is critical for Suggestly's functionality. It permits the app to accurately identify each meeting participant. This ability allows Suggestly to make relevant suggestions based on the conversation context and each participant's role within the call.
  • meeting:write: This scope is vital for Suggestly to provide a seamless user experience. By having this permission, Suggestly can automatically fetch information about the meeting lifecycle events like when a meeting is created or started. As a result, users don't need to manually input this information, making the app more intuitive and user-friendly.
  • meeting_token:read:local_recording: This scope is pivotal for Suggestly to access and process the real-time audio stream from your Zoom meetings. The obtained local recording meeting tokens are used to power Suggestly's transcription feature. As a result, Suggestly can listen in real-time, transcribe the conversation, and generate contextually relevant suggestions during your Zoom meetings. This scope is critical for the fundamental functionality of the Suggestly app.

1.3 Real-Time Zoom Streaming Data

Suggestly captures and processes the real-time streaming data of your Zoom calls to provide its core functionality. Here's why this data capture is necessary:
  • Real-Time FAQ Responses: Suggestly uses the real-time audio and video streams of your Zoom calls to generate on-the-spot FAQ responses. The app listens to the conversations, identifies relevant questions or topics, and provides immediate responses based on the pre-loaded FAQ documents. This real-time processing allows for efficient and timely communication during your Zoom meetings.
  • Contextual Information: Capturing real-time streaming data enables Suggestly to understand the context of the ongoing conversation. This contextual understanding is paramount in delivering accurate and relevant responses to the participants, contributing to a more productive and engaging meeting experience.
  • User Experience Personalization:The real-time streaming data helps Suggestly adapt to the dynamics of the conversation. It allows the app to adjust the nature and timing of its responses according to the flow of the meeting, enhancing the overall user experience.

1.4 Application Use Data

As part of the regular operation of Suggestly, we collect standard application use data to improve your experience, secure your interactions, and streamline our service. Here's how we utilize this information:
  • Cookies: We use cookies to personalize your experience on Suggestly, remember your preferences, and understand usage patterns. These small files stored on your device help us customize the App to your needs and enhance your interaction with our service.
  • IP Addresses: We collect your IP address to ensure a secure and stable connection to Suggestly. Your IP address helps us diagnose server issues, manage the App, and identify broad demographic trends. This information aids in maintaining the reliability of our service.
  • Session Information: Session data includes details about your interaction with the App, such as the date and time of your access, pages viewed, and the amount of time spent on the App. This information helps us understand how users navigate and use Suggestly, enabling us to improve the service and its features.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you're using to access Suggestly, including the type of device, operating system, and browser. This information helps us optimize the App for different devices and platforms for a smoother user experience.

2. Consent

Consent for data collection and processing is obtained explicitly at the time of sign up. By agreeing to our terms and continuing to use the App, users provide ongoing consent to the practices outlined in this policy.

3. Data Retention

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your Personal Data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.
Usage Data and meeting transcripts are retained for a period of 30 days. After this period, they are permanently deleted from our systems.

4. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
We employ data encryption and secure cloud storage through Google Cloud Platform to ensure the safety of your data.

  • Right to Request Erasure: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data that we hold, under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request that we transfer your personal data directly to you or to a third party chosen by you, where technically feasible.
