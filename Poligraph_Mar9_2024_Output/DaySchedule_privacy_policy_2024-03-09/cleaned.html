Data Retention

We retain User information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Agreement and comply with legal obligations. Once the information is no longer required, we will securely delete it.

All the appointments, calendar booking data is automatically deleted or archived after 3 years depending on the subscription plan or enterprise agreement:

Plan Retention Period Description
Basic 1 Year Appointment booking data is retained for 1 year from the date of the appointment. This allows for record-keeping, addressing any disputes, and providing historical data if needed. After 1 year, the data is securely deleted or archived to S3.
Professional 2 Years Appointment booking data is retained for 2 years from the date of the appointment. This extended retention period provides additional historical data for analysis, reporting, or compliance purposes. After 2 years, the data is securely deleted or archived to S3.
Enterprise 3 Years Appointment booking data is retained for 3 years from the date of the appointment. This longer retention period ensures availability of comprehensive historical records for auditing, regulatory compliance, or business analysis. After 3 years, the data is securely deleted or archived to S3.

What are cookies

Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the Service or a third-party to recognize you and make your next visit easier and the Service more useful to you.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies.

How DaySchedule uses cookies

When you use and access the Service, we may place a number of cookies in your web browser.

We use cookies for the following purposes: to enable certain functions of the Service, to provide analytics, to store your preferences, to enable advertisements delivery, including behavioral advertising.

We use both session and persistent cookies on the Service and we use different types of cookies to run the Service

Essential cookies. We may use essential cookies to authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts.
