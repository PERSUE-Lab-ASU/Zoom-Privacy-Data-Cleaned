We store certain information, including Usage Information, from your browser using “cookies.” Cookies are small text files that are stored on a user’s computer hard drive. If you prefer not to receive cookies, you can set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies and refuse the cookie when your browser alerts you to its presence.
