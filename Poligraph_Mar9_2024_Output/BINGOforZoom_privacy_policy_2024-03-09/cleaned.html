Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Happy Games LTD’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, https://www.happygames.club/, and other games we own and operate.

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used.

‍

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we’ll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

We will give access or remove customer data upon the customer's request. Data is deleted from the production environment within the next 30 days. You can request for the same, by dropping a mail to: hello@happygames.club

‍

You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services.

‍

Your continued use of our website will be regarded as an acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us through an email at hello@happygames.club.
