Personal information we collect and how we use it

We also use tracking and analysis tools to fulfill our legitimate interest in improving the operation, utility, and security of our Website and Services. These tools enable us to collect personal and statistical information about you, your devices, and your use of our Website, such as:

Please see our Cookie Policy for further information.

You may register a user account on our Website or request a quote for Services by providing us with your:

Your rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information according to the terms of the EU General Data Protection Regulation: 

  • The right to access. You may ask us to confirm whether we are processing your personal information. 
  • The right to rectification. If we are processing information about you which is not accurate or complete, you are entitled to correct it. If we have shared your information with others, we may, where possible, assist you in correcting your information with the appropriate party.
  • The right to erasure. You may ask us to delete your personal information under certain conditions, such as when we no longer need your information. 
  • The right to restrict processing. You may ask us to restrict our use of your personal information under certain conditions. 
  • The right to object to processing. You may ask us to stop processing your personal information, and we will do so if:
    • we are processing your personal information in our legitimate interests or those of a third party, unless we demonstrate a compelling reason for the processing 
    • we are processing your personal information for the purpose of direct marketing
  • The right to data portability. Under certain circumstances, you may obtain the personal information that you have provided to us in a standardized and machine readable format. 
  • The right to withdraw consent. If we are processing your personal information on the basis of your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time. 
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. You have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority.
