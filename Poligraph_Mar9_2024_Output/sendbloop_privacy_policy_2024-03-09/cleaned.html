Use of collected personal data

In order to analyze site traffic (number of visits, pages viewed, etc.), we use a third-party service. The data is anonymized and does not identify individual user behavior, but rather a general trend.

Event logs (or logs )

When you visit our site, our servers may record data from your browser. This may include your "Internet Protocol" (IP) address from your computer, the browser used and its version, the pages you visit, the date and time of the visit and other elements.

Zoom data

When you link your account to Zoom, we request the scope to view information about the profile, this allows us to display your name and profile picture in the application as well as to get the owner account of the Zoom account.

We only store Zoom data we need to make sendbloop functionnal. This data is stored securely on our server and kept while you use the Service.

By using sendbloop.com, you agree with Zoom Privacy Policy.

Commercial data corresponds to the data accumulated during the use of the services provided by the site. This may include uploaded files (eg company logo), information regarding your subscription, analytics and other metrics, and other information created or generated while using our service.

Duration of storage of your data
