Infinity AI Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Dec 5, 2023]

Introduction

Welcome to Infinity AI. This Privacy Policy governs your use of our website getinfinityai.com, our application app.getinfinityai.com, and any related services (collectively, the "Platform"). This policy explains how Infinity AI ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information.

Information Collection

1. Information You Provide

  • Account and Profile Information: Includes your name, email address, password, and any other information you choose to provide.
  • Content You Generate or Provide: Information in your user content, messages, search queries, or data you import into our services.

2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Information: Details of your interactions with our services, including traffic data, location data, logs, and other communication data.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and other technologies to collect data about your use of our Platform and services.

3. Information from Third Parties

  • Integrations: If you integrate third-party services with our Platform, we may receive information related to those integrations.

Use of Your Information

We use your information for various purposes:

  • Service Provisioning: To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Communication: To communicate with you, including responding to your requests and providing customer service.
  • Marketing: To send you promotional messages and information about services we think will interest you.
  • Research and Development: To develop new tools, products, or services and to enhance our existing offerings.
  • Compliance and Protection: For legal reasons, including enforcing our Terms of Service, and protecting our rights and the rights of others.

Sharing of Information

  • Service Providers: We engage third-party companies to assist in providing our services, such as data analytics and hosting services.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith it is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Security

We implement measures aimed at securing your personal information from accidental loss and unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and maintained on, computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access, Update, and Deletion: You can review, change, or delete your personal information by logging into the Platform.
  • Opt-Out of Communications: You can opt out of receiving marketing emails from us.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy frequently to stay informed of our privacy practices.

Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at contact@getinfinityai.com.
