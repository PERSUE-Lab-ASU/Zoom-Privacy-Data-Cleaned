Privacy Policy

In handling personal information, we consider personal information to be the most precious asset of the company and take all possible security measures. In addition, all directors and employees of the Company shall understand the purpose of the Personal Information Protection Policy and shall implement it faithfully.

Management of personal information

  1. We will acquire, use, and provide personal information in an appropriate manner that takes into account the content and scale of our business.
  2. We will clearly specify the purpose of use of personal information and take appropriate measures to ensure that personal information is not handled in a manner that exceeds the purpose of use.
  3. We will comply with laws and regulations, national guidelines, and other standards regarding the handling of personal information.
  4. We will establish a safety management system to prevent unauthorized access to personal information and the leakage, loss, destruction, or damage of personal information. In addition, we will take appropriate corrective and preventive measures against accidents related to personal information.
  5. We have established an inquiry desk to promptly respond to requests for disclosure of personal information, as well as inquiries, complaints, and consultations regarding the handling of personal information.
  6. The Company will continuously review and appropriately improve its personal information protection management system.
