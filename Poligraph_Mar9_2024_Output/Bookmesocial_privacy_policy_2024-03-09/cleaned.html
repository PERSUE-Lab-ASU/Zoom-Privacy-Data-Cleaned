Privacy Policy

Welcome to BookMe! We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when using the Service, including but not limited to:


  • Personal Information: This includes, but is not limited to, your full name, email address, phone number, and any other information provided during the creation of your BookMe account.

  • User Content: Content generated or posted on BookMe, such as profile data, availability, and visual media.


1.2. Automatically Collected Information


We may also collect certain information automatically when you use the Service, including:


  • Log Data: Data concerning your use of the Service, which may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device-related details.

2. How We Use Your Information


The information collected by BookMe is employed for the following purposes:


  • Service Provision and Maintenance: To offer and sustain the Service.

  • Facilitation of Bookings and Transactions: To enable bookings and transactions between users.

  • Enhancement and Personalization: To enhance and personalize the user experience.

  • Communication: To interact with users, address inquiries, and provide customer support.

  • Policy Enforcement: To uphold our Terms of Service and other pertinent policies.

  • Notifications: To disseminate updates, promotions, and other information germane to the Service.


3. Sharing Your Information


BookMe may share your information with third parties in the following scenarios:

We respect your data rights, which include:
