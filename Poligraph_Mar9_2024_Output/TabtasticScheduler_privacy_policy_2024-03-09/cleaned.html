What data do we collect?

When you use the Tabtastic service, either via the Tabtastic website or by interacting with the Tabtastic Discord bot, we collect and process the following data:

  • Personal identification information (Name, Email Address)
  • Billing information (Billing Address)
  • Usage information (Participation records at tournaments on Tabtastic)
  • Connected accounts on other platforms (Discord & Zoom account identifiers)

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide Tabtastic with most of the data we collect. We collect and process data when you:

  • Sign in to the Tabtastic website
  • Sign up or register for a Tabtastic tournament
  • Place an order for a Tabtastic tournament
  • Voluntarily complete user surveys or feedback forms

Tabtastic may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

  • From Discord, Inc., when you add the Tabtastic Discord integration to your Discord server or when you interact with the Tabtastic Discord integration in other servers
  • From Zoom Video Communications, Inc., when you add the Tabtastic Zoom integration to your Zoom account and when you schedule Zoom meetings using the Tabtastic Zoom integration
  • From Stripe Payments Europe Limited, when you complete transactions on the Tabtastic website using Stripe Payments

How will we use your data?

Tabtastic collects your data so that we can:

  • Identify and manage your account
  • Allow you to sign up and register for Tabtastic tournaments
  • Provide you information about the Tabtastic tournaments you are participating in
  • Process your orders for Tabtastic tournaments
  • Allow you to interact with Tabtastic through other services

If you agree, Tabtastic will share your data with partner companies so that they may offer you their products and services.

  • Stripe Payments Europe Limited: Email and customer reference provided for processing payments for Tabtastic services and fraud prevention
  • Amazon Web Services, Inc.: Email provided for sending notification and billing emails regarding Tabtastic tournaments

When Tabtastic process your order, it may send your data to, and also use the resulting information from, credit reference agencies to prevent fraudulent purchases.

How do we store your data?

Tabtastic securely stores your data on servers provided by Hetzner Online GmbH, located in Nuremberg, Germany. The servers are secured using Public-Private key cryptography with Ed25519 keys.

Tabtastic will keep your personal data for as long as you retain your account with us. If you request to delete your account, all personal information will be deleted, except where required for financial records.

Tabtastic will retain participation records at tournaments for as long as the tournament remains on the Tabtastic platform, but personal information can be removed from participation records at any time.

Marketing

Tabtastic does not use your information for marketing purposes.

What are your data protection rights?

Tabtastic would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request Tabtastic for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that Tabtastic correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Tabtastic to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that Tabtastic erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that Tabtastic restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to Tabtastic’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that Tabtastic transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: hello@tabtastic.net

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How do we use cookies?

Tabtastic uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including:

  • Keeping you signed in to the Tabtastic website
  • Anonymously analysing usage patterns for anti-fraud purposes, via Stripe Payments Europe Limited

What types of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:

  • Functionality – Tabtastic uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. This also includes anonymous usage analysis for anti-fraud purposes. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

Privacy policies of other websites

The Tabtastic website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

Tabtastic keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 26 November 2021.
