Privacy Policy for Re:catch

 

At Business Canvas Inc., we are committed to protecting your privacy. Our meeting scheduling web software is designed to make it easier for you to schedule and attend virtual meetings, and it integrates with your Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook Calendar. This Privacy Policy sets out the basis on which we will process any personal information we collect from you, or that you provide to us, in connection with your use of our meeting scheduling software.

 

Information Collection and Use

When you use our meeting scheduling software, we collect and store the following information:

1.     Information you provide directly: This includes information you provide when you create a user account, such as your name, email address, and calendar account details.

2.     Information about your usage of the software: This includes information about the meetings you schedule and attend, including the date, time, and participants of the meeting.

3.     Information from your calendar account: This includes information about your calendar events, such as the date, time, and subject of the event, as well as information about your calendar preferences and settings.

We use this information to provide, maintain, and improve our meeting scheduling software and to provide you with the best possible user experience.

 

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with third-party service providers that perform certain services on our behalf, such as hosting and maintenance of our software, and processing payment transactions. These third-party service providers are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are restricted from using your information for any purpose other than providing services to us.

 

Access to and Control Over Your Information

You have the right to access and control the information we collect from you. You can request a copy of your information at any time, and you can request that we delete your information at any time by contacting us at hello@recatch.cc.

 

Security

We take the security of your information seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
