Cookies are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive, at a web site’s request. The Company Cookies do not collect any personal information. The Cookies are used primarily for the following purposes:

  • to store your preferences and settings on your device and to enhance your experience;

The Cookies used by the Site may come from the Company.

Our services use three categories of Cookies:

  • Necessary Cookies: These Cookies are required for optimal navigation and operation of the Site. For example, these Cookies are used to implement the basket function, such that the goods in your basket stay saved while you continue with the purchase. The necessary Cookies also serve to save inputs and settings which you have made so that you don’t have to constantly repeat them, and to adapt Company’s content to your individual interests. Only limited use of the Site is possible without necessary Cookies.

  • Statistical Cookies: These Cookies collect device and access data to analyze the use of the Site, such as which areas of the Site are used how (so-called surfing behavior), how fast content is loaded and whether errors occur. These Cookies only contain anonymous or pseudonymous information and are only used to improve the Site and to find out what our users are interested in, and to measure how effective our advertising is. Statistical Cookies can be blocked without adversely affecting the navigation and operation of the Site.
