Privacy Policy
eeds will not sell or rent your personal information for marketing purposes and will only share your personal information as described in this policy. From all participants, we collect their name, professional degree, office location, email, and phone number for contact and identification purposes. Your organization or education provider for CME events you attend will have access to your personal information, and we will provide your contact information to the organization if requested.
