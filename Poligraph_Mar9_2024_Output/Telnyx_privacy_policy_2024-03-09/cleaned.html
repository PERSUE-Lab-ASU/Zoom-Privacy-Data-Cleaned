Details on Telnyx's Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy provides important information about how Telnyx handles personal information in line with the General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679.

This privacy statement (fully explained at https://telnyx.com/privacy-policy) details how we collect personal data and how we will treat them. By supplying your personal data through our website or by sharing personal data via the email addresses by our staff members, you implicitly declare that you have taken notice of our privacy policy and that you concur with the use of your personal data.

GDPR and CCPA Rights

If you make a request to exercise any of your applicable GDPR or CCPA rights, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please submit a request here: https://telnyx.com/request-to-control-review-data
