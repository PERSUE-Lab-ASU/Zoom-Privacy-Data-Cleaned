5. INFORMATION WE COLLECT FROM USERS

In order to use the Service, users must submit their name and email to the Service. The Service provides users with one place to organize and present their knowledge and content. Students may sign up either by going to the site or by a teacher adding them to a class. We use the names and email address of students only to provide the Service, and do not disclose them to anyone other than a student's own teachers or organization administrators except under the circumstances described in Section 7 below. Information we collect from users includes:

  • Personal Information You Provide at Registration. If you register for an account on our Service, we ask for your name and email address. If you connect your Google account to PlayPosit through Google SSO or the Google Drive integration, we will also receive other Google account profile information. Note that all information received from Google will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy.
  • We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with our Service. We keep track of your “clicks” and pages you view on our Site. We use “cookies” and obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses the Site, such as the Internet Protocol (IP) address of your computer, information about your browser software and operating system, and the date and time you access our site. “Cookies” are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser and provide certain features on our Site. The “Help” feature in most Web browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, or how to disable some cookies altogether. Keep in mind that disabling cookies may result in some features of the Service not working properly.
  • If you choose to connect your PlayPosit account to your Screencastify account, PlayPosit will use the Google APIs to retrieve videos hosted in Screencastify and then upload them to a third-party remote system for further processing before making them available for your use in the PlayPosit platform.
