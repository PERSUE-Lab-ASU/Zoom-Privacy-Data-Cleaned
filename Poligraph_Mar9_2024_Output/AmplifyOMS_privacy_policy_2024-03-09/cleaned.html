Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1/1/2024
 
This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for amplifyOMS, LLC. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by this website. It will notify you of the following:
 

1. Information We Collect

At amplifyOMS, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect personally identifiable information from you through this website, and we want you to know how this information is used and shared.
 
1.1 Personally Identifiable Information
We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We only have access to and collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as through email or other direct contact. We will never sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.
 
1.2 Identity Resolution Software
We use identity resolution software from third-party data providers to gather information about our website visitors. This information helps us understand and improve our services and user experience. The data collected may include personal information, such as your name, phone number, email address, state, and zip code.
 

2. How We Use Your Information

We will use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries and fulfill your requests. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, except as necessary to fulfill your specific request, for example, to process an order.
 

3. Communication

Unless you request otherwise, we may contact you via email in the future to inform you about special offers, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy. By submitting your contact information on our website, you agree to be contacted by any of the provided methods, and you acknowledge that data usage rates may apply as imposed by your data provider.
 

4. Your Access and Control Over Information

You have the right to:
 
– See what data we have about you, if any.
– Change/correct any data we have about you.
– Have us delete any data we have about you.
– Express any concerns you have about our use of your data.
 
You can exercise these rights at any time by contacting us via the email address or phone number provided on our website.
 

5. Data Retention

The duration for which we retain your Personal Data depends on the type of data and the purpose for which we process it. We will retain your Personal Information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
 

6. Security

We take the security of your information seriously. When you submit sensitive information via our website, it is protected both online and offline. We encrypt and secure sensitive information, including credit card data, during transmission and storage. Our employees who require access to personally identifiable information are granted access only for specific job-related purposes, such as billing or customer service. Our computer systems and servers that store personally identifiable information are maintained in a secure environment.
 

7. Contact Us

If you believe that we are not abiding by this privacy policy or if you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us immediately via telephone at 940-340-3139.
 
This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Please check this page periodically for any updates. Your continued use of our website after any changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of those changes.
