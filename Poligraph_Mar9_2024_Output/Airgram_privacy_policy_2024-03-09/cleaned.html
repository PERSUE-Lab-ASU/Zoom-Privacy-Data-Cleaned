Privacy Policy

Latest update on September 22, 2023

At Airgram Inc. collectively referred to herein as 'we', 'us',' ours', and 'Airgram', we respect the privacy of our Customers, Authorized Users referred to herein as 'Users', and the importance of the information they entrust to us. With this in mind, we provide you this privacy policy (the 'Policy') to help you understand the kinds of information we may gather about you when you access our Web Sites (at https://www.airgram.io), our Services Airgram and other interactions (e.g. customer support conversations, user surveys, user onboarding etc.) you may have with Airgram, how we may use and disclose the information, and how you can control, correct and/or update the information. By accessing or using the Airgram Web Sites or Services, you are accepting the policies and practices described in this Policy. Each time you visit or use the Web Site or Services, you agree and expressly consent to our collection, use, and disclosure of the information that you provide as described in this Policy.

Information We Collect

To engage our Services as a User, you must be a Customer which is a registered account holder or authorized by a Customer to use their login information. As a User, you may record conversations or upload files using our web application. For content and data that you upload to or make available through the Service (“User Content”), you are responsible for ensuring this content is in accordance with our Terms of Service, and that the content is not violating other users’ privacy. When you register as a Customer and use the Services we will collect the following information as listed below. We will not sell collected data to other companies.

WE COLLECT:

Your email address

WHY WE COLLECT IT AND HOW WE USE IT:

  • To create and support your account and provide you with Services.

  • To communicate with you, for example, informing you about your account status, security updates, and website application information.

  • To contact you about features, products, services, and other promotions that can enhance your use of the Services, in accordance with your communications preferences.

  • To enforce compliance with our Terms.

HOW WE RETAIN IT:

We keep your email address until you delete your account by sending an email to support@airgram.io.

WE COLLECT:

Event id, title, date, start time, stop time, video conferencing tool, description, organizer, attendees, and other calendar information from Google or Outlook Calendar for current and following days.

WHY WE COLLECT IT AND HOW WE USE IT:

To automatically create meetings on Airgram,

  • Get Event id, title, date, start time, video conferencing tool, description, and organizer and attendees’ email addresses to create an Airgram recording.

  • Get the Zoom meeting, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams URL to attend the meeting.

  • Get the organizer and attendees’ email addresses to send invitations via email.

  • Upload meeting notes to event details.

WE DO NOT:

We do not share your Google or Outlook Calendar information with third-party data processors.

HOW WE RETAIN IT:

We delete all historical information after the event has passed. We delete all information from Google or Outlook Calendar when you disconnect Google or Outlook Calendar from Airgram.

WE COLLECT:

All events related to the use of the Services including access, record, share, edit, and delete events.

WHY WE COLLECT IT AND HOW WE USE IT:

Usage data is retained in log files to monitor the quality of service and to troubleshoot or respond to customer queries.

HOW WE RETAIN IT:

Log files are stored for up to five days and are then deleted.

WE COLLECT:

Web browser information: We assign a unique cookie to your web browser when you log in as a user to the Services. We do not assign cookies for visitors who do not log in to the service.

WHY WE COLLECT IT AND HOW WE USE IT:

We use session cookies to retain login status and state in a particular session when using Airgram via a web browser.

HOW WE RETAIN IT:

We retain web cookies for up to two weeks whenever the user logs out of Services.

WE COLLECT:

Video recordings of conversations that you hold or attend meetings using the services.

WHY WE COLLECT IT AND HOW WE USE IT:

To create a transcript of the service using the Airgram proprietary. We process the resultant transcript to derive key phrases and other summary data and assign a speaker ID if the speaker is enrolled by the user. As you are using Airgram to record meetings, you are responsible for collecting consent from all participants in the meeting prior to starting the recording.

To create AI-generated content for the service using GPT or Claude. We process the resultant transcript to create an AI summary, provide information, and answer questions through an AI chatbot interface (Airgram Chat).

WE DO NOT:

We do not select any segment of raw or partially processed video or associated transcribed text from multiple recorded conversations from different Users of the Services to be used as training data to automatically train our Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR).

Only after receiving explicit permission from the customers, we manually transcribe certain recorded conversations to further enrich our training data. We do not access your recordings and transcripts at any point. Only when you personally share access with individual employees for technical support. Also in those cases, only recordings and transcripts with access rights given to the support team member will be accessed.

HOW WE RETAIN IT:

We store a copy of the recording, the associated transcript, speaker ID, key phrases, and other related metadata until it is deleted by the user.

WE COLLECT:

Recordings and certain user data from other Services.

WHY WE COLLECT IT AND HOW WE USE IT:

  • We process all recorded conversations from third-party sources the same way we process recordings directly captured via the Services, but we will not use any data in aggregate for Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR).

  • We integrate with third-party services to automate the capture and/or upload of recorded audio conversations for processing through the Services. We may use stored credentials including API keys, to access these services. We may also support direct login to certain third-party services through the Services via OAuth, which means that no user credentials for the third-party services are stored.

  • Certain third-party service providers may access the Service using API keys provided by Airgram.

  • Third-party service (except AI) will not collect or restore any data of our users, such as audio, transcriptions, agendas, notes, messages, and other information in Airgram.

HOW WE RETAIN IT:

  • Recordings uploaded from third-party services and associated with a User account are processed and retained using the same guidelines for recordings directly captured or uploaded through the Services.

  • Use and retention of data from third-party services that use the Airgram APIs may be governed by the policies of those third-party services.

WE COLLECT:

If you successfully authorized Zoom, Airgram will collect your Zoom meeting information, Zoom UID, and authorization status.

WHY WE COLLECT IT AND HOW WE USE IT:

  • To create a Zoom meeting

  • To collect your Zoom meeting information and activate the Zoom auto-join feature.

WE DO NOT:

We do not share your Zoom information with third-party data processors.

HOW WE RETAIN IT:

We keep your Zoom information until you disconnect Zoom or delete your account.

We use services provided by a number of third parties to process usage and certain device data for business intelligence and troubleshooting purposes. These third parties may use cookies, web beacons, or other unique identifiers to track your use of the Services. We do not share login or personal information with third-party data processors unless you choose to share them yourself.

  • Google Analytics—We use Google Analytics to detailed user event data from the website. More information may be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

  • We may also share Personal Information or Customer Data as necessary to comply with legal or regulatory requirements and to respond to lawful requests, court orders, and legal processes, as well as, to protect and defend the rights, property, or safety of us or third parties, including enforcing contracts or policies, or in connection with investigating and preventing fraud.

  • We may also share transcripts with AI (such as GPT or Claude) to create AI-generated content (including AI summary, AI chatbot, and other AI features). Please be clear that transcripts and queries submitted by you may be used to train AI models or improve their service offerings. More information about GPT may be found at: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy. More information about Claude may be found at: https://console.anthropic.com/legal/privacy

Security

Airgram is committed to protecting your information and we take appropriate commercially reasonable physical, electronic, and other security measures to help safeguard personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Only the minimal required communication to and between the servers is allowed.
