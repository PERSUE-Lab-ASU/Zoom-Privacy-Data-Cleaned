Informed Interview Intelligence Platform Privacy Policy

Informed Decisions LTD ("Informed Decisions", or "us," "our," "we") has developed an interview platform (the” Platform”) to interview and assess potential and current employees. The platform uses algorithms and analysis of data in order to produce a detailed assessment of each participant's fit for an employment position and to provide insight to decision makers to support people decisions. The platform is integrated into the organization's internal employee assessment tools. The use of the Platform is subject to this Privacy Policy (the "Privacy Policy"). "You" includes anyone using the Platform for any reason, including anyone conducting interviews, serving as an administrator of the Platform, and anyone generating or using reports via the Platform. If not otherwise defined herein, capitalized terms have the meaning given to them in the Terms of Service, which is available here (the "Terms").

Informed Decisions respects your right to privacy and your ability to make educated decisions about your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we collect, and how we use, transfer and protect the information we receive, as well as your ability to control certain uses of the collected information.

"Personal Information" means information that can be directly associated with a specific person such as a name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address.

You are under no obligation to provide us with any Personal Information and provision of such information is entirely at your discretion and with your consent, however, if you do not consent to provide any or all such information, we may not be able to provide (a portion of) the Services through the Platform.
